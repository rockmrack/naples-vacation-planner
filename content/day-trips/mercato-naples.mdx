## Why Visit Mercato?

**Mercato** is Naples' modern lifestyle center—an open-air shopping and dining destination with upscale stores, excellent restaurants, live music, and a movie theater.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | North Naples (US 41) |
| **Drive Time** | 15 minutes from downtown |
| **Parking** | Free |
| **Vibe** | Modern, upscale, lively |

---

## What to Do

### Shopping
- Whole Foods Market
- Nordstrom Rack
- Sur La Table
- Boutiques and more

### Dining
- **The Pub** – Gastropub, great burgers
- **MASA** – Upscale Mexican
- **Bravo!** – Italian
- **Burn by Rocky Patel** – Steak, cigars

### Entertainment
- Silverspot Cinema – Luxury theater
- Live music (weekends)
- Events throughout year

---

## Events

- **Free concerts** on the lawn
- **Art nights**
- **Food festivals**
- Check calendar for schedule

---

## Tips

- **Evening is liveliest** – dining, entertainment
- **Whole Foods** for picnic supplies
- **Movies + dinner** – great combo
- **Different vibe** than Old Naples (modern)
