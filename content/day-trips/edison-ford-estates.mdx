## Why Visit Edison & Ford Estates?

**Edison & Ford Winter Estates** offers a glimpse into how two of America's greatest inventors lived—featuring beautiful gardens, historic homes, a museum of inventions, and the famous Edison Banyan tree.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~30 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 40 minutes |
| **Admission** | $25 adults, $15 kids |
| **Hours** | 9 AM - 5:30 PM |

---

## What to See

### Historic Homes
- Edison's "Seminole Lodge"
- Ford's "The Mangoes"
- Original furnishings and artifacts

### Botanical Gardens
- 20+ acres of tropical gardens
- Largest banyan tree in US
- Edison's laboratory
- Over 1,700 plant species

### Museum
- Edison's inventions
- Historic phonographs
- Light bulb collection
- Model T Ford

## Tips

- **Allow 2-3 hours** minimum
- **Morning is cooler** for garden walks
- **Combine with downtown Fort Myers** for lunch
- **Don't miss the lab** – original equipment
