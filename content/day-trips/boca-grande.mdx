## Why Visit Boca Grande?

**Boca Grande** on Gasparilla Island is Florida's most understated wealthy enclave—no high-rises, no chain stores, just beautiful beaches, historic charm, and world-famous tarpon fishing.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~75 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 90 minutes |
| **Toll** | $6 Boca Grande Causeway |
| **Vibe** | Historic, upscale, quiet |

---

## What to Do

### Beaches
- Gasparilla Island State Park
- Beautiful, uncrowded
- Lighthouse at park entrance

### Main Street
- Quaint shops and restaurants
- No chains allowed
- Historic inn and buildings

### Tarpon Fishing
- World capital of tarpon fishing
- May-July peak season
- Book charters in advance

---

## Where to Eat

- **Loose Caboose** – Local favorite, great burgers
- **PJ's Seagrille** – Upscale seafood
- **Pink Elephant** – Historic landmark

---

## Tips

- **Bring cash** – some places cash-only
- **Rent bikes** – great way to explore
- **Visit the lighthouse** – beautiful state park
- **Don't expect nightlife** – it's quiet
