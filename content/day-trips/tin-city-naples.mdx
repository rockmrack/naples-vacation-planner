## Why Visit Tin City?

**Tin City** is Naples' funky waterfront shopping village—historic clam shacks turned into unique shops, with boat tours, fresh seafood, and great people-watching on Naples Bay.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Naples Bay, downtown |
| **Parking** | Free lot |
| **Vibe** | Casual, waterfront, touristy |
| **Good For** | Families, boat tours, shopping |

---

## What to Do

### Shopping
- Unique, locally-owned shops
- Shell and nautical gifts
- Art and crafts
- T-shirts and souvenirs

### Boat Tours
- Dolphin watching cruises
- Shelling trips
- Sunset cruises
- Fishing charters

### Dining
- **Riverwalk at Tin City** – Waterfront dining
- **Pincher's Crab Shack** – Casual seafood
- Multiple casual options

---

## History

Originally a clam processing area in the 1920s—the "tin" buildings were repurposed into shops. It's a working waterfront with local character.

---

## Tips

- **Morning** for boat tour departures
- **Casual atmosphere** – come as you are
- **Visit 5th Ave after** – 5 minute drive
- **Free parking** – rare in downtown Naples
