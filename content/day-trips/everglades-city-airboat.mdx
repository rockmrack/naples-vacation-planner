## Why Take an Everglades Airboat Tour?

Airboat tours from **Everglades City** offer the most authentic Everglades experience—zip across the sawgrass, see alligators up close, and experience wild Florida with local captains.

## What You'll See

- **Alligators** – Guaranteed (usually many)
- **Wading birds** – Egrets, herons, ibis
- **Bald eagles** – Often spotted
- **Sawgrass prairies** – Iconic landscape
- **Cypress domes** – Beautiful trees

---

## Tips

- **Book in advance** during peak season
- **Bring ear protection** – airboats are loud
- **Sunscreen and hat** – no shade on airboat
- **Go in winter** – dry season = more wildlife
