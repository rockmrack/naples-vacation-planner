## Why Deep Sea Fish from Naples?

Naples is a world-class fishing destination with easy access to Gulf of Mexico waters—home to grouper, snapper, kingfish, tarpon, and more. Experienced captains ensure success.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Departure** | Naples City Dock |
| **Duration** | Half-day (4 hrs) to Full-day (8+ hrs) |
| **Cost** | $130-300+ per person |
| **License** | Included on charter |

---

## What You'll Catch

### Year-Round
- **Grouper** – Delicious, popular
- **Snapper** – Multiple species
- **Amberjack** – Strong fighters

### Seasonal
- **Kingfish** (Nov-Mar)
- **Tarpon** (May-Aug)
- **Cobia** (Spring)
- **Mahi-mahi** (Year-round, peak summer)

## Tips

- **Book in advance** during peak season
- **Bring Dramamine** if you get seasick
- **Light breakfast** – don't fish hungry
- **They clean your catch** – take it to restaurant!
