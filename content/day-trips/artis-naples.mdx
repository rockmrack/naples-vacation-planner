## Why Visit Artis-Naples?

**Artis-Naples** is Southwest Florida's premier performing arts and visual arts center—home to the Naples Philharmonic orchestra and the Baker Museum, housed in a stunning architectural landmark.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Pelican Bay |
| **Performance Season** | October - May |
| **Museum Hours** | Tue-Sat 10 AM - 4 PM |
| **Tickets** | From $25 |

---

## The Venues

### Hayes Hall
- 1,477-seat concert hall
- World-class acoustics
- Naples Philharmonic home
- Visiting performers

### Baker Museum
- Contemporary art
- Rotating exhibitions
- Significant collection
- Don't miss!

### Daniels Pavilion
- Smaller venue
- Chamber music
- Intimate performances

---

## What to See

### Naples Philharmonic
- Full symphony season
- Pops concerts
- Chamber music
- Guest artists

### Baker Museum
- Modern & contemporary art
- Photography exhibitions
- Sculpture garden
- Educational programs

---

## Tips

- **Book early** for popular concerts
- **Combine with Waterside Shops** next door
- **Dress code** – smart casual for performances
- **Free parking** – ample lot
