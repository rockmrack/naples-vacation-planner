## Why Fort Myers?

Fort Myers sits just 35 minutes north of Naples and offers a **completely different experience**—think historic estates, manatee encounters, and lush nature preserves. It's the perfect complement to Naples' beach-focused attractions.

---

## The Perfect Day Trip Itinerary

### Morning: Edison & Ford Winter Estates (9:00 AM – 12:00 PM)

This **21-acre historical site** was the winter retreat of two of America's greatest inventors: **Thomas Edison** and **Henry Ford**.

**What You'll See:**
- Edison's laboratory (where he invented the phonograph)
- 1,700+ species of plants in the botanical gardens
- Historic homes of both Edison and Ford
- The Edison Botanic Research Laboratory
- Massive Banyan Tree (one of the largest in the US)

**Practical Info:**
- **Hours:** 9 AM – 5:30 PM daily
- **Cost:** ~$25 adults, ~$12 children
- **Guided tours:** Recommended for full experience
- **Time needed:** 2–3 hours

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Book the "Historian Tour" for deeper insights into Edison's inventions and friendship with Ford and Harvey Firestone.
</ExpertTip>

---

### Late Morning: Manatee Park (12:00 PM – 1:30 PM)

**November through March**, Manatee Park is one of the best places in Florida to see wild manatees up close—for free!

**Why They're There:**
Manatees gather in the warm water discharged from the nearby power plant when Gulf temperatures drop.

**What to Know:**
- **Best viewing:** Cold mornings (below 68°F)
- **Cost:** Free admission, $2/hour parking
- **Facilities:** Kayak/paddleboard rentals, nature trails
- **Tip:** Use the elevated viewing platforms for best photos

---

### Lunch: Downtown Fort Myers (1:30 PM – 2:30 PM)

The **Downtown River District** is walkable and charming:

| Restaurant | Vibe | Must-Try |

| **Ford's Garage** | Edison-era theme | Burgers |
| **Izzy's Fish & Oyster** | Fresh seafood | Raw Bar |
| **The Veranda** | Upscale, historic | Southern brunch |

---

### Afternoon: Six Mile Cypress Slough Preserve (2:30 PM – 4:30 PM)

A **peaceful escape** into Florida's natural landscape.

**The Experience:**
- **1.2-mile raised boardwalk** through wetlands
- Spot alligators, turtles, herons, and more
- Interpretive nature center
- Shaded, easy walk suitable for all ages

**Practical Info:**
- **Cost:** Free admission, $1/hour parking
- **Hours:** Sunrise to sunset daily
- **Time needed:** 1–2 hours

---

### Optional Add-Ons

1. **Fort Myers Beach:** Lively beach town, Times Square area, casual dining

3. **IMAG History & Science Center:** Interactive museum for families
4. **Calusa Nature Center:** Planetarium, aviaries, nature trails

## Practical Tips

### Getting There
- **From Naples:** Take I-75 North or US-41 North
- **Drive time:** 35–45 minutes
- **Parking:** Varies by location ($1–2/hour typically)

### Best Time to Visit
- **Manatee Season:** November – March
- **Avoid:** Summer afternoons (heat and storms)
- **Edison Estates:** Best in morning before it gets hot

### What to Pack
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Binoculars for wildlife
- [ ] Camera
- [ ] Light layers (gardens can be shady/cool)
- [ ] Water bottle
