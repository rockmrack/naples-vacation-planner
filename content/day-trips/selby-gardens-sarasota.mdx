## Why Visit Selby Gardens?

**Marie Selby Botanical Gardens** is world-famous for its orchid collection—over 6,000 orchids in a stunning bayfront setting in downtown Sarasota.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~80 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 90 minutes |
| **Admission** | $26 adults |
| **Hours** | 10 AM - 5 PM |

---

## What to See

### Orchid Collection
- 6,000+ orchids
- Tropical display house
- Rare species from around world
- Rotating exhibits

### Bromeliad Gardens
- Extensive collection
- Tropical landscaping
- Colorful displays

### Bayfront Grounds
- Stunning Sarasota Bay views
- Historic buildings
- Art installations

---

## Two Locations

### Downtown (Main)
- Orchid focus
- Sarasota Bay waterfront
- Traditional gardens

### Historic Spanish Point
- Nature trails
- Archaeological sites
- Different experience

---

## Tips

- **Morning light** best for photography
- **Allow 2-3 hours**
- **Combine with downtown Sarasota**
- **Spring** for peak orchid blooms
