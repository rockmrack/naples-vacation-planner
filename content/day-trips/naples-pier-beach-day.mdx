## Why Naples Pier & Beach?

**Naples Pier** is Florida's most iconic pier—a 1,000-foot structure offering incredible sunsets, dolphin viewing, and access to Naples' beautiful Gulf beach. It's the quintessential Naples experience.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Downtown Naples |
| **Parking** | Paid lots nearby |
| **Beach Access** | Free |
| **Pier Access** | Free |

---

## The Pier

### What to Expect
- **1,000 feet long** – Gulf views
- **Fishing** – No license needed from pier
- **Dolphins** – Often seen from pier
- **Sunset** – Don't miss it!

### Best Times
- **Sunrise** – Quiet, beautiful light
- **Sunset** – Iconic, crowded but worth it
- **Midday** – Fewer people, hot

---

## The Beach

### Beach Activities
- Swimming in calm Gulf waters
- Shelling (best at low tide)
- Beach volleyball
- People watching

### Nearby
- **3rd Street South** – Shopping, dining (5 min walk)
- **5th Avenue South** – More dining (10 min walk)

---

## Sample Day

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9 AM | Arrive, claim beach spot |
| 10 AM | Swim, shell, relax |
| 12 PM | Lunch on 3rd Street |
| 2 PM | More beach time |
| 5 PM | Walk pier, wait for sunset |
| 6:30 PM | Sunset! |
| 7 PM | Dinner on 5th Ave |

---

## Tips

- **Arrive early** for parking (or walk from downtown hotels)
- **Bring umbrella** – little natural shade
- **Stay for sunset** – it's legendary
- **Weekdays less crowded**
