## Skip the 6-Hour Drive

Key West is typically a 5–6 hour drive from Naples via the Overseas Highway. But the **Key West Express** offers a much better option: a **high-speed catamaran** that whisks you to paradise in about 3.5 hours while you relax, eat breakfast, and watch for dolphins.

---

## Key West Express Basics

### Departure Points

| Location | Drive from Naples | Notes |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| **Fort Myers Beach** | 45 min | Primary departure |
| **Marco Island** | 20 min | Seasonal only |

### Schedule (Typical)

| Direction | Departure | Arrival |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| To Key West | 8:00 AM | 11:30 AM |
| Return | 6:00 PM | 9:30 PM |

*Note: Check keywestexpress.net for current schedules*

### Pricing (2024)

| Ticket Type | Price |
|-------------|-------|
| Adult Round-Trip | ~$165–185 |
| Child (6–12) | ~$130 |
| Under 6 | Free |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Book 2+ weeks in advance for best prices. Arrive 1 hour before departure for check-in. Bring motion sickness meds if you're sensitive—the Gulf can be choppy.
</ExpertTip>

## Your Day in Key West

### 11:30 AM – 12:30 PM: Arrival & Orientation

The ferry docks at the **Historic Seaport**. You'll have about 6.5 hours to explore.

**Getting Around:**
- **Walking:** Key West is very walkable (1 mile × 4 miles)
- **Conch Train:** Narrated tour (~90 min, $35)
- **Trolley:** Hop-on/hop-off option
- **Bikes/Scooters:** Rentals available at the port

---

### 12:30 PM – 2:00 PM: Lunch on Duval Street

**Duval Street** is Key West's famous main drag—bars, shops, and restaurants galore.

| Restaurant | Vibe | Must-Try |
|------------|------|----------|
| **Pepe's Café** | Local classic | Breakfast all day |
| **El Siboney** | Cuban hole-in-wall | Ropa Vieja |
| **Half Shell Raw Bar** | Waterfront | Conch fritters |

---

### 2:00 PM – 4:30 PM: Explore the Highlights

**Must-See Attractions:**

1. **Ernest Hemingway Home & Museum**
   - 6-toed cats roaming the property
   - Where he wrote "For Whom the Bell Tolls"
   - $18 adults, tours every 30 min

2. **Southernmost Point Buoy**
   - Iconic photo op (90 miles to Cuba!)
   - Expect a short line

3. **Key West Lighthouse**
   - Climb 88 steps for panoramic views
   - $12 adults

4. **Fort Zachary Taylor State Park**
   - Best beach in Key West
   - Civil War-era fort

---

### 4:30 PM – 6:00 PM: Sunset Celebration

**Mallory Square** hosts a nightly sunset celebration with:
- Street performers
- Artists and vendors
- Food and drinks
- Spectacular Gulf sunsets

**Alternative:** Grab a drink at the rooftop bar at **Sunset Pier** at Ocean Key Resort.

---

### 6:00 PM: Board the Ferry Home

The return trip is a great time to:
- Enjoy dinner on board
- Watch the stars come out over the Gulf
- Relax after a full day

## Practical Tips

### What to Pack
- [ ] Sunscreen and sunglasses
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Light layers (ferry has AC)
- [ ] Motion sickness meds (just in case)
- [ ] Cash for street vendors
- [ ] Camera

### Weather Considerations
- **Best months:** October – May (calm seas)
- **Summer:** Hot, potential afternoon storms, rougher seas
- **Avoid:** Hurricane season (Aug–Oct) can cause cancellations

### Ferry Tips
- **Arrive 1 hour early** for check-in
- **Seating:** Upper deck for views, lower deck for less motion
- **Food/drinks:** Available for purchase on board
- **Luggage:** Not necessary for day trip, but small bags okay
