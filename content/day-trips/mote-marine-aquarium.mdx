## Why Visit Mote Marine?

**Mote Marine Laboratory & Aquarium** combines world-class marine research with family-friendly exhibits—see manatees, sharks, sea turtles, and learn about ocean conservation.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~85 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 90 minutes |
| **Admission** | $26 adults, $20 kids |
| **Hours** | 10 AM - 5 PM |

---

## What to See

### Manatees
- Hugh and Buffett – resident manatees!
- Learn about rehabilitation
- Feeding times

### Sharks
- 135,000-gallon shark habitat
- Shark encounter experiences (extra)
- Touch tanks

### Sea Turtles
- Sea Turtle Rehabilitation Hospital
- Rescue and release programs
- Interactive exhibits

---

## Research Focus

Mote is a working research lab:
- Shark research
- Red tide research
- Coral restoration
- Conservation programs

---

## Tips

- **Feeding times** – check schedule
- **Touch tanks** – kids love them
- **Combine with** Siesta Key or St. Armands
- **Allow 2-3 hours**
