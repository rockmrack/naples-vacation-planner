## Why Visit Naples Zoo?

**Naples Zoo at Caribbean Gardens** is a delightful, intimate zoo featuring African wildlife, a unique boat ride to primate islands, and lush tropical gardens—all just minutes from downtown.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Central Naples |
| **Drive Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Admission** | $25 adults, $17 kids |
| **Hours** | 9 AM - 5 PM |

---

## Top Experiences

### Safari Canyon
- Giraffes, zebras, antelope
- African savanna feel
- Giraffe feeding experience ($10)

### Primate Expedition Cruise
- Boat ride to islands
- Monkeys, apes, lemurs
- Included with admission
- Unique experience!

### Alligator Bay
- Florida natice wildlife
- Gator feedings
- Panther habitat

---

## Best For Families

- **Size**: Manageable for all ages (43 acres)
- **Playground**: Kids play area
- **Strollers**: Available for rent
- **Food**: On-site café

---

## Tips

- **Go in morning** – animals more active
- **Don't miss boat ride** – it's unique
- **Book giraffe feeding** early
- **Winter/spring** best weather
