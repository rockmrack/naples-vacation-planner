## Why Golf in Naples?

**Naples** is one of America's premier golf destinations with over 80 courses—more per capita than almost anywhere in the US. From luxury private clubs to accessible public courses, it's golf paradise.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Number of Courses** | 80+ |
| **Peak Season** | November - April |
| **Tee Times** | Book in advance (essential in season) |
| **Price Range** | $50 - $350+ |

---

## Top Public/Semi-Private Courses

### High-End ($$$$)
- **Tiburón Golf Club** – Ritz-Carlton, two courses
- **LaPlaya Golf Club** – 18 holes, Robert Cupp design
- **Naples Grande Golf Club** – Resort course

### Mid-Range ($$$)
- **Valencia Golf Course** – Great value
- **Hibiscus Golf Club** – Classic layout
- **Arrowhead Golf Club** – Well-maintained

### Value ($$)
- **Naples Beach Hotel Golf** – Fun, short course
- **Lely Resort** – Public play, 2 courses

---

## Private Clubs (Guest Access)

Some allow resort guest play or reciprocals:
- Grey Oaks
- Quail Creek
- Mediterra

---

## Tips

- **Book 2+ weeks ahead** in peak season
- **Twilight rates** save 30-50%
- **Summer rates** much cheaper (but hot!)
- **Caddies** available at higher-end courses
