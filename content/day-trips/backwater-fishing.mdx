## Why Backwater Fish?

Backwater fishing in Naples' mangrove estuaries offers a different experience—calm waters, beautiful scenery, and world-class inshore species like snook and redfish.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Naples Bay, 10,000 Islands, Marco |
| **Duration** | Half-day (4 hrs) to Full-day |
| **Cost** | $400-600 per boat (1-2 anglers) |
| **Best For** | Inshore/flats fishing |

---

## Target Species

### Year-Round
- **Snook** – Prized gamefish
- **Redfish** – Strong fighters
- **Seatrout** – Good eating
- **Snapper** – Mangrove snapper

### Seasonal
- **Tarpon** (Apr-Jul) – The ultimate challenge
- **Jack crevalle** – Powerful
- **Sheepshead** (Winter)

---

## The Experience

### What to Expect
- **Poling/trolling motors** – Quiet approach
- **Sight fishing** – See fish in clear water
- **Light tackle** – Fun fight
- **Mangrove scenery** – Beautiful

### Skill Level
- Beginners welcome
- Guides teach techniques
- Often more success than deep sea
- Calmer waters (less seasickness)

---

## Tips

- **Polarized sunglasses** – essential for sight fishing
- **Light clothing** – it gets hot
- **Morning trips** – fish more active
- **Listen to your guide** – they know the spots
