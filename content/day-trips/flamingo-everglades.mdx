## Why Visit Flamingo?

**Flamingo** is the end of the road in Everglades National Park—38 miles from the Homestead entrance, offering incredible kayaking, guaranteed manatee sightings, and Florida Bay access.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~100 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 2 hours |
| **Park Entry** | $30/vehicle |
| **Visitor Center** | Open daily |

---

## Top Experiences

### Kayaking
- Paddle to Hells Bay (famous trail)
- See manatees in marina
- Rent kayaks on-site
- Guided tours available

### Boat Tours
- Backcountry boat tours
- See alligators, crocs, birds
- Sunset tours spectacular

### Wildlife Viewing
- Manatees in marina (winter)
- American crocodiles (rare elsewhere)
- Roseate spoonbills
- Hundreds of bird species

---

## The Drive Down

The 38-mile drive from main entrance is an experience:
- Multiple stops with boardwalks
- Anhinga Trail (best short walk)
- Pa-hay-okee Overlook
- Mahogany Hammock

---

## Tips

- **Full day trip** – plan 6+ hours
- **Bring food** – limited options at Flamingo
- **Winter is best** – wildlife concentrated
- **Gas up** – no gas at Flamingo
