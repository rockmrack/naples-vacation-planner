## Why Visit Clam Pass?

**Clam Pass Beach** offers a unique experience—a scenic 3/4-mile boardwalk through a mangrove forest leading to a beautiful, less-crowded beach in the Pelican Bay area.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~6 miles from downtown |
| **Drive Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Parking** | $10 (in Clam Pass Park lot) |
| **Beach Access** | Free tram or walk |

---

## The Boardwalk

### What to Expect
- **3/4-mile elevated boardwalk**
- Winds through mangrove forest
- Wildlife viewing opportunities
- Free tram available (every 15 min)

### Wildlife Spotted
- Herons and egrets
- Ospreys
- Crabs and fish
- Occasional dolphins

---

## The Beach

- Less crowded than Naples Pier
- Calm, clear water
- Good for families
- Concession stand on beach
- Chair/umbrella rentals available

---

## Tips

- **Park fills early on weekends**
- **Walk the boardwalk** – it's the best part
- **Tram is free** if you don't want to walk
- **Sunset is spectacular** from the beach
