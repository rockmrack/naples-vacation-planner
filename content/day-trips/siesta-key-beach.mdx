## Why Visit Siesta Key Beach?

**Siesta Key Beach** is regularly voted America's #1 beach—famous for its pure quartz sand that stays cool underfoot even on the hottest days. It's worth the drive.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~85 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 90 minutes |
| **Parking** | Free (but fills early!) |
| **Famous For** | Cool quartz sand |

---

## What Makes It Special

### The Sand
- **99% pure quartz** – brought from Appalachian runoff
- **Never gets hot** – comfortable barefoot all day
- **Ultra-fine, powdery** – like walking on sugar

### The Beach
- Wide, expansive shoreline
- Calm, clear water
- Family-friendly
- Volleyball, amenities

---

## Siesta Key Village

After the beach:
- Walkable village nearby
- Shops and restaurants
- Beach bars
- Casual, fun atmosphere

---

## Tips

- **Arrive by 9 AM on weekends** – parking fills up
- **Bring umbrella** – minimal natural shade
- **No coolers with alcohol** – allowed but no glass
- **Stay for sunset** – gorgeous
