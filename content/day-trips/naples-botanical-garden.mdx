## Why Visit Naples Botanical Garden?

**Naples Botanical Garden** is a world-class 170-acre garden featuring themed areas representing tropical ecosystems from around the world—from Brazilian rainforest to Asian gardens.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | South Naples |
| **Drive Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Admission** | $25 adults, $15 kids |
| **Hours** | 9 AM - 5 PM |

---

## Garden Highlights

### Brazilian Garden
- Rainforest plants
- Orchids and bromeliads
- Water features

### Asian Garden
- Japanese influences
- Koi ponds
- Peaceful meditation areas

### Caribbean Garden
- Tropical fruits
- Palm collection
- Florida natives

### Children's Garden
- Interactive play areas
- Perfect for families
- Educational activities

---

## Best Photo Spots

1. **Kapnick Brazilian Garden** – Orchid house
2. **Preserve Cypress** – Reflection lake
3. **Asian Garden** – Moon gate
4. **Kathleen's Garden** – Butterflies

---

## Tips

- **Allow 2-3 hours** minimum
- **Morning is cooler** and better light
- **Wear comfortable shoes** – lots of walking
- **Don't miss the butterfly conservatory**
