## Why See Manatees?

Florida manatees are gentle giants—and winter is the best time to see them when they gather in warm-water areas. Several great viewing spots are accessible from Naples.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Best Season** | November - March |
| **Why Winter** | Manatees seek warm water |
| **Best Viewing** | Cool days (below 68°F) |
| **Cost** | Free - $75 (guided tour) |

---

## Best Viewing Spots

### Closest to Naples

**Flamingo (Everglades)** – 2 hours
- Marina area concentrations
- Kayak alongside them
- Very reliable

**10,000 Islands** – 1 hour
- Boat/kayak tours
- Natural habitat
- Guided tours available

### Worth the Drive

**Homosassa Springs** – 4 hours
- Famous manatee springs
- See dozens guaranteed
- Underwater viewing

**Crystal River** – 4 hours
- Swim with manatees (legally!)
- Once-in-a-lifetime experience
- Book tours early

---

## Tips

- **Cold fronts** bring manatees to warm areas
- **Morning** – more active
- **Don't touch** – illegal and harmful
- **Polarized glasses** help see underwater
