## Why Visit Koreshan State Park?

**Koreshan State Park** preserves the site of a fascinating 1890s utopian community that believed the Earth was hollow. The preserved settlement and beautiful grounds make it unique.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~20 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 30 minutes (Estero) |
| **Admission** | $5 per vehicle |
| **Hours** | 8 AM - Sundown |

---

## The Koreshan Unity

### The Story
- Founded 1894 by Cyrus Teed
- Believed Earth was hollow, curved inward
- Celibate community of ~200
- Beautiful tropical landscaping

### What Remains
- 11 restored historic buildings
- Art hall, planetary court
- Gardens and grounds
- Fascinating history

---

## Other Activities

### Nature
- Kayak/canoe on Estero River
- Nature trails

- Picnic areas

- Full-facility campground

## Tips

- **Take the ranger tour** – best way to learn history
- **Bring kayak** – nice river paddle
- **Pack a picnic** – beautiful grounds
- **Unique story** – research before going
