## Why Visit Big Cypress?

**Big Cypress National Preserve** protects over 700,000 acres of wild Florida—featuring scenic drives, hiking trails, and incredible wildlife viewing away from the crowds at Everglades.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~30 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 40 minutes |
| **Admission** | Free |
| **Hours** | Open 24 hours |

---

## Top Experiences

### Loop Road Scenic Drive
- 26-mile unpaved loop
- Excellent wildlife spotting
- Alligators, deer, birds
- Free, no reservation needed

### Kirby Storter Roadside Park
- Easy boardwalk trail
- Great for families
- Guaranteed wildlife

### H.P. Williams Roadside Park
- Wheelchair accessible
- Alligator viewing
- Restrooms available

---

## Wildlife You'll See

- **Alligators** – Abundant!
- **Florida panthers** – Rare but possible
- **Black bears** – Occasional
- **Deer, otters, birds**

---

## Tips

- **Start at Oasis Visitor Center** for maps/info
- **Drive slowly on Loop Road** – wildlife everywhere
- **Bring water and snacks** – no services
- **Dry season (winter)** is best
