## Why Visit Waterside Shops?

**Waterside Shops** is Naples' most upscale shopping destination—featuring luxury brands, beautiful architecture, and waterfront fine dining in the Pelican Bay area.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Pelican Bay, North Naples |
| **Drive Time** | 15 minutes from downtown |
| **Parking** | Free valet available |
| **Vibe** | Ultra-luxury, elegant |

---

### Designer Boutiques
- Louis Vuitton
- Gucci

- Saks Fifth Avenue

## Fine Dining

- **Brio Tuscan Grille** – Italian
- **Blue Martini** – Lounge, live music
- **California Pizza Kitchen** – Casual option
- Multiple lakeside restaurants

---

## The Setting

- Beautiful lake views
- Covered walkways
- Elegant architecture
- Free valet parking

---

## Tips

- **Dress nicely** – it's upscale
- **Free valet** – use it
- **Window shop** if not buying
- **Combine with Artis-Naples** next door
