## Why Visit The Dalí Museum?

**The Dalí Museum** houses the world's most comprehensive collection of Salvador Dalí's work—over 2,400 objects in a stunning building that's a work of art itself.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~150 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 2.5 hours |
| **Admission** | $29 adults |
| **Hours** | 10 AM - 6 PM (Thu til 8 PM) |

## The Collection

### Masterworks
- 8 of Dalí's 18 masterpiece paintings
- Over 2,400 total objects
- Spanning his entire career
- Comprehensive collection worldwide

### The Building
- Geodesic glass "enigma"
- Stunning architecture
- Hurricane-resistant design
- Photo-worthy from every angle

## Planning Your Visit

### Tips
- **Book timed tickets** – especially weekends
- **Allow 2-3 hours**
- **Audio guide included**
- **Garden** – don't miss labyrinth

### Nearby
- St. Pete Pier (5 min)
- IMAGINE Museum (5 min)
- Downtown restaurants
