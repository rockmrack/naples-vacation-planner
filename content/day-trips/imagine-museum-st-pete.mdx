## Why Visit IMAGINE Museum?

**IMAGINE Museum** showcases America's premier collection of contemporary studio glass art—stunning, colorful sculptures in a beautifully designed space in St. Petersburg's arts district.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~150 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 2.5 hours |
| **Admission** | $20 adults |
| **Hours** | 10 AM - 5 PM |

---

## The Collection

### What to Expect
- Stunning blown glass sculptures
- Contemporary American artists
- Colorful, dramatic pieces
- Photography-friendly

### Notable Works
- Chihuly influences
- Studio glass movement
- Modern techniques
- Changing exhibitions

## St. Petersburg Arts

While in St. Pete:
- **Dalí Museum** – Nearby!
- **St. Pete Pier** – New waterfront destination
- **Arts District** – Walkable galleries
- **Beaches** – St. Pete Beach, Pass-a-Grille

## Tips

- **Combine with Dalí Museum** – 5 min apart
- **Photography allowed**
- **Make it a full day** in St. Pete
- **Arts District lunch** – many options
