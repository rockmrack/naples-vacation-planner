## Why Take a Dolphin Cruise?

Naples' waters are home to a large population of bottlenose dolphins—a cruise is the best way to see them up close while enjoying the beautiful coastline and barrier islands.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Departure** | Tin City, Naples Bay |
| **Duration** | 1.5 - 3 hours |
| **Cost** | $35-65 adults |
| **Sightings** | Guaranteed (or cruise again free) |

## What You'll See

- **Bottlenose dolphins** – Guaranteed!
- **Shelling island stops** – Some cruises include
- **Barrier islands** – Beautiful scenery
- **Manatees** – Possible in winter
- **Sea birds** – Pelicans, ospreys, more
