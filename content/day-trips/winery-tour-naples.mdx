## Why Visit Florida Wineries?

Southwest Florida has a surprising wine scene—local wineries produce unique tropical fruit wines alongside traditional wines. Tastings offer something completely different.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Nearest Winery** | ~45 minutes |
| **Tasting Cost** | $8-15 typically |
| **Varieties** | Tropical fruit + grape wines |
| **Best For** | Couples, wine enthusiasts |

---

## Notable Wineries

### Schnebly Redland's Winery
- ~2 hours (near Miami)
- Famous tropical fruit wines
- Beautiful grounds
- Restaurant on-site

### Florida Orange Groves Winery
- ~90 minutes (St. Petersburg area)
- Orange wine specialty
- Tastings and tours

### Fiorelli Winery
- ~90 minutes (Bradenton)
- Traditional grape wines
- Tours available

---

## What to Expect

### Tropical Wines
- Mango
- Key lime
- Passion fruit
- Guava
- Coconut

### Traditional
- Muscadine (native grape)
- Some imported grape wines

---

## Tips

- **Designated driver** or tour company
- **Call ahead** – hours vary
- **Buy to ship** – great souvenirs
- **Combine wineries** for full day
