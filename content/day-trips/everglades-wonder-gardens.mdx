## Why Visit Everglades Wonder Gardens?

**Everglades Wonder Gardens** is a charming 1936 roadside attraction preserved as a botanical garden and wildlife sanctuary—home to flamingos, reptiles, and beautiful tropical gardens.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Bonita Springs |
| **Drive Time** | 25 minutes |
| **Admission** | $17 adults, $10 kids |
| **Hours** | 9 AM - 5 PM |

---

## What to See

### Wildlife
- **Flamingos** – Beautiful!
- **Alligators** – Florida natives
- **Tortoises** – Giant tortoises
- **Exotic birds** – Parrots, macaws

### Gardens
- Tropical landscaping
- Historic banyan trees
- Butterfly garden
- Beautiful photo spots

### History
- 1930s roadside attraction
- Rescued and preserved
- Classic Old Florida feel

---

## Tips

- **Small but charming** – allow 1-2 hours
- **Great for kids** – animals are accessible
- **Photo opportunities** – flamingos!
- **Combine with** Bonita Beach nearby
