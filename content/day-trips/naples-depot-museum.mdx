## Why Visit Naples Depot Museum?

**Naples Depot Museum** tells the story of how the railroad built Naples—housed in the beautifully restored 1927 depot with exhibits on pioneer history, Seminole culture, and early Naples.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Downtown Naples |
| **Admission** | Free (donations welcome) |
| **Hours** | Mon-Sat 10 AM - 4 PM |
| **Time Needed** | 30-60 minutes |

---

## What to See

### Exhibits
- Railroad history
- Seminole Indian artifacts
- Pioneer Naples photos
- Historic train car (outside)

### The Building
- Restored 1927 depot
- Beautiful Mediterranean architecture
- On National Register of Historic Places
