## Why Visit Lake Okeechobee?

**Lake Okeechobee** is Florida's largest lake—the seventh largest in the US—offering world-class bass fishing, incredible bird watching, and a completely different Florida experience.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~100 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 2 hours |
| **Lake Size** | 730 square miles |
| **Best Town** | Clewiston |

---

## What to Do

### Bass Fishing
- World-class largemouth bass
- Hire local guides
- Tournaments year-round

### Bird Watching
- Huge wading bird populations
- Best in winter
- Drive the levee road

### Scenic Drive
- Herbert Hoover Dike road
- Circle the entire lake
- Multiple access points

---

## Towns to Visit

### Clewiston
- "America's Sweetest Town"
- Sugar cane capital
- Historic Clewiston Inn

### Moore Haven
- Lake access
- Nature trails

---

## Tips

- **Bring binoculars** for birds
- **Hire a fishing guide** – lake is huge
- **Drive the levee** for unique views
- **Very different** from coastal Florida
