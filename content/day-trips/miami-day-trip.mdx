## Why Visit Miami?

**Miami** offers everything Naples doesn't—urban energy, international culture, world-famous nightlife, and the iconic Art Deco District. It's a completely different Florida experience.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~105 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 2 hours via I-75/Alligator Alley |
| **Best For** | Culture, nightlife, urban exploration |
| **When to Go** | Year-round, avoid hurricane season |

---

## What to Do

### South Beach & Art Deco
- Walk Ocean Drive's Art Deco buildings
- People-watch on the beach
- Lunch at iconic spots like News Cafe

### Little Havana
- Cuban coffee at Versailles
- Walk Calle Ocho
- Cigar shops and Latin music

### Wynwood Arts District
- World-famous street art
- Galleries and cafes
- Instagram-worthy murals

## Tips

- **Leave early** to maximize time
- **Avoid rush hour** (4-7 PM in Miami is brutal)
- **Bring walking shoes** – lots of exploring
- **Stay overnight** if you want nightlife
