## Why Take a Sunset Cruise?

Naples is famous for spectacular sunsets—and there's no better way to experience one than from the water. Sunset cruises offer romance, relaxation, and often dolphin sightings.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Departure** | Tin City, Naples |
| **Duration** | 1.5 - 2 hours |
| **Cost** | $50-85 per person |
| **Includes** | Sunset + often drinks |

## Top Operations

### Naples Princess
- Large yacht
- Full bar
- Comfortable seating
- Popular for groups

### Sweet Liberty
- Catamaran sailing
- More intimate
- Excellent reviews
- BYOB option

### Pure Florida
- Multiple options
- Eco-focused
- Great for families

---

## What to Expect

- Cruise Naples Bay and Gordon Pass
- Watch sunset over Gulf of Mexico
- Frequent dolphin sightings
- Commentary on local landmarks
- Often includes drinks/snacks

---

## Tips

- **Book 2 weeks ahead** in peak season
- **Arrive 30 min early**
- **Bring camera** – sunset photos!
- **Light jacket** – can be breezy
- **Special occasions** – very romantic
