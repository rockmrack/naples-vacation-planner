## Why Take a Shelling Cruise?

Barrier islands near Naples have far better shelling than developed beaches—a shelling cruise takes you to spots only accessible by boat, where shells wash up in abundance.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Departure** | Tin City, Naples |
| **Duration** | 2-3 hours |
| **Cost** | $45-65 adults |
| **What's Included** | Boat, beach time, guide |

## What You'll Find

### Common Shells
- **Whelks** – Various species
- **Fighting conch** – Beautiful
- **Murex** – Spiny, intricate
- **Coquinas** – Colorful clusters

### Rare Finds
- **Junonia** – Holy grail shell
- **Lion's paw** – Colorful scallop
- **Sand dollars** – Intact ones
- **Alphabet cones** – Prized by collectors

---

## Best Shelling Tips

1. **Go at low tide** – more exposed sand
2. **After storms** – best finds
3. **Walk slowly** – treasures are small
4. **Look in wrack lines** – debris lines on beach
5. **Morning** – before others have searched

## Top Operators

- **Sweet Liberty** – Popular Naples catamaran
- **Naples Explorer** – Shelling focus

## Tips

- **Bring a mesh bag** – for shells
- **Wear water shoes** – wading required
- **Sunscreen** – no shade on islands
- **Stay hydrated** – bring water
