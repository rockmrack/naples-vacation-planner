## Why Visit Lovers Key?

**Lovers Key State Park** offers one of Southwest Florida's most beautiful beaches—over 2.5 miles of pristine shoreline, excellent kayaking, and frequent dolphin and manatee sightings.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~15 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 30 minutes |
| **Admission** | $8 per vehicle |
| **Hours** | 8 AM - Sundown |

---

## What to Do

### Beach
- 2.5 miles of pristine sand
- Less crowded than Naples beaches
- Excellent shelling
- Free tram to beach

### Kayaking
- Paddle through mangroves
- Dolphin and manatee sightings common
- Rentals available on-site
- Guided tours offered

### Wildlife
- Dolphins (frequent)
- Manatees (especially winter)
- Shorebirds
- Osprey and eagles

## Tips

- **Arrive early on weekends** – parking fills up
- **Take the kayak tour** for best wildlife
- **Bring snorkel gear** – good for beginners
- **Stay for sunset** – spectacular views
