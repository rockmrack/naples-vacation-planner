## Why Visit Rookery Bay?

**Rookery Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve** protects 110,000 acres of pristine mangrove estuary—one of the few remaining undeveloped areas along Florida's Gulf Coast.

- Interactive exhibits

- Gift shop

- Guided eco-tours
- Paddle through mangroves
- Wildlife viewing
- Rentals available

### Boardwalks
- Nature trails
- Bird observation platforms
- Self-guided tours

## Wildlife

- 150+ bird species
- Manatees
- Dolphins
- Sea turtles
- Mangrove habitat

## Tips

- **Kayak at high tide** – easier paddling
- **Binoculars essential** – birding
- **Visit learning center** – excellent exhibits
- **Combine with** Collier-Seminole nearby
