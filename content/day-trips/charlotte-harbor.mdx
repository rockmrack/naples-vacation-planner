## Why Visit Charlotte Harbor?

**Charlotte Harbor** is one of Florida's most productive estuaries—270 square miles of pristine water teeming with dolphins, manatees, sea turtles, and incredible bird life.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~65 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 75 minutes |
| **Base Town** | Punta Gorda |
| **Best Activity** | Kayaking/paddling |

---

## What to Do

### Kayaking
- Paddle through mangroves
- Manatee sightings common
- Dolphin encounters
- Guided eco-tours available

### Fishing
- Excellent tarpon fishing
- Snook, redfish, trout
- Hire local guides

### Bird Watching
- Roseate spoonbills
- Wood storks
- Bald eagles
- Hundreds of species

---

## Punta Gorda

Charming base town:
- Historic downtown
- Waterfront restaurants
- Murals throughout town
- Fishermen's Village

---

## Tips

- **Sunrise/sunset** best for paddling
- **Winter** for concentrated wildlife
- **Bring binoculars** for birding
- **Explore Punta Gorda** after
