## Why Visit 5th Avenue South?

**5th Avenue South** is the heart of Naples—a charming tree-lined street packed with upscale boutiques, world-class restaurants, art galleries, and sidewalk cafés. It's Naples' "Main Street."

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Downtown Naples |
| **Length** | ~10 blocks |
| **Parking** | Street meters, garages nearby |
| **Best Time** | Evening for dining, daytime for shopping |

---

## What to Do

### Shopping
- Upscale boutiques
- Art galleries
- Jewelry stores
- Designer clothing

### Dining
- **Barbatella** – Italian, beloved
- **Vergina** – Mediterranean, people-watching
- **Truluck's** – Seafood, upscale
- **Ocean Prime** – Steakhouse

### Entertainment
- Street performers
- Live music venues
- Art walks (monthly)
- Nearby theaters

---

## Nearby

- **Naples Pier** – 10-minute walk
- **3rd Street South** – More shopping
- **Cambier Park** – Green space
- **Naples Players** – Theatre

---

## Tips

- **Evening is magical** – lights, energy
- **Reservations essential** for popular spots
- **Walk to pier** for sunset
- **Art walks** first Saturday monthly
