## Why Take a Food Tour?

Naples has a remarkable culinary scene—a walking food tour introduces you to the best local restaurants and hidden gems with tastings and stories you'd never discover on your own.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Downtown Naples |
| **Duration** | 2.5 - 3 hours |
| **Cost** | $75-100 per person |
| **Includes** | Multiple tastings + guide |

## Tour Operators

### Original Naples Food Tours
- 5th Avenue focus
- 5-6 restaurant stops
- Local history included

### Savory Spoon Food Tours
- Small groups
- Chef perspectives
- Hidden gems focus

## What to Expect

### Tastings
- 5-7 restaurant stops
- Full meal worth of food
- Wine pairings often included
- Sweet and savory

### Learning
- Restaurant histories
- Chef introductions
- Naples culinary scene
- Where locals eat

## Tips

- **Come hungry** – lots of food
- **Wear comfortable shoes** – walking tour
- **Morning tours** cooler in summer
- **Ask questions** – guides are experts
