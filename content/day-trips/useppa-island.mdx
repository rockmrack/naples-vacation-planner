## Why Visit Useppa Island?

**Useppa Island** is an exclusive, 100-acre private island with a fascinating history—once home to Calusa Indians, later a tarpon fishing club for millionaires. Day trips allow non-members to explore.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Access** | Boat only (Captiva Cruises) |
| **Tour Cost** | ~$65 adults |
| **Duration** | Half-day |
| **Includes** | Lunch, island tour |

---

## Getting There

### Captiva Cruises
- Depart from Captiva Island
- ~30-minute boat ride
- Includes lunch at Collier Inn
- Guided island walking tour

---

## What You'll See

### History
- Calusa shell mound artifacts
- Museum with island history
- Historic homes (view from outside)

### Nature
- Beautiful tropical landscaping
- Stunning bay views
- Small but picturesque

### The Experience
- Lunch at historic Collier Inn
- Walking tour with guide
- Time to explore on own

---

## Tips

- **Book in advance** – limited capacity
- **Combine with Cabbage Key** – some tours do both
- **Dress smart casual** – it's an exclusive club
- **Great for history buffs**
