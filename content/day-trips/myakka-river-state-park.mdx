## Why Visit Myakka River State Park?

**Myakka River State Park** is one of Florida's oldest and largest state parks—37,000 acres of wild Florida with prairies, wetlands, ancient oaks, and incredible wildlife viewing.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~60 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 75 minutes |
| **Admission** | $6 per vehicle |
| **Size** | 37,000 acres |

---

## What to Do

### Airboat Tours
- **Gator Gal** airboat
- Amazing wildlife viewing
- Alligators guaranteed
- Book in advance!

### Canopy Walkway
- Elevated boardwalk
- 74-foot observation tower
- See park from above
- Short easy walk

### Hiking
- Multiple trail systems
- Prairies and wetlands
- Wildlife everywhere
- Bird watching

---

## Wildlife

- **Alligators** – Abundant
- **Wild boar** – Common
- **Deer** – Often seen
- **Birds** – Hundreds of species
- **Sandhill cranes** – Winter

---

## Tips

- **Book airboat early** – sells out in season
- **Canopy walk first** – less crowded morning
- **Bring binoculars** – essential
- **Dry season best** (Nov-Apr)
