## Why Visit 3rd Street South?

**3rd Street South** is Old Naples' original shopping district—more intimate than 5th Avenue, with charming courtyards, boutique shops, and the beloved Saturday Farmers Market.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Old Naples |
| **Vibe** | Intimate, upscale, local |
| **Parking** | Street, nearby lots |
| **Near** | Naples Pier (5 min walk) |

---

## What to Do

### Shopping
- One-of-a-kind boutiques
- Art galleries
- Home décor
- Jewelry (Marissa Collections)

### Dining
- **The Bay House** – Waterfront seafood
- **Sea Salt** – Modern Italian-seafood
- **Tommy Bahama** – Island atmosphere
- **Jane's Cafe** – Brunch beloved

### Courtyards
- Hidden courtyards with shops
- Beautiful tropical landscaping
- Photo-worthy spots

---

## Saturday Farmers Market

Don't miss it!
- **When**: Saturday, 7:30 AM - 11:30 AM (Nov-Apr)
- **What**: Local produce, baked goods, prepared foods
- **Why**: Naples' best farmers market

---

## Tips

- **More intimate** than 5th Avenue
- **Explore the courtyards** – hidden gems
- **Saturday morning** for Farmers Market
- **Walk to pier** easily from here
