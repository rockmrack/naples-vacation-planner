## Why Visit Shark Valley?

**Shark Valley** offers one of the Everglades' best wildlife experiences—a 15-mile loop road with guaranteed alligator sightings, tram tours, and a 65-foot observation tower with stunning views.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~70 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 90 minutes |
| **Park Entry** | $30/vehicle |
| **Tram Tour** | $29 adults |

---

## Ways to Explore

### Tram Tour (Recommended)
- 2-hour narrated tour
- Covers full 15-mile loop
- Stops at observation tower
- **BOOK IN ADVANCE** – sells out!

### Bike Rental
- $10/hour bike rental
- Ride the 15-mile loop yourself
- Stop whenever you want
- Take 2-4 hours

### Walking
- Can walk the loop (long!)
- Better for short sections
- Stay on paved road

---

## The Observation Tower

- 65 feet tall
- 360-degree views
- See for miles across sawgrass
- Best at midday (heat thermals)

---

## Wildlife You'll See

- **Alligators** – Everywhere! (hundreds)
- **Wading birds** – Great variety
- **Turtles** – Sunning on logs
- **Snakes** – Occasional

---

## Tips

- **Book tram EARLY** – weeks in advance in winter
- **Morning tours best** for wildlife
- **Bring water** – it gets hot
- **Don't approach alligators** – seriously!
