## Why SUP in Naples?

Naples' calm bays and waterways are perfect for stand up paddleboarding—flat water, beautiful scenery, and frequent dolphin and manatee sightings make it ideal for all skill levels.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Best Locations** | Naples Bay, Vanderbilt Lagoon, Rookery Bay |
| **Rentals** | $30-50/hour |
| **Lessons** | $50-75 |
| **Best Time** | Early morning (calm water) |

---

## Where to Paddle

### Naples Bay
- Easy launch from Tin City
- Calm, protected water
- Dolphin sightings common
- Rentals available

### Vanderbilt Lagoon
- Calm waters
- Near Vanderbilt Beach
- Good for beginners

### Rookery Bay Reserve
- Nature focus
- Mangrove trails
- Guided eco-tours

### Clam Pass
- Scenic mangrove paddle
- Connect to Gulf

---

## Tips

- **Morning is best** – calmest water, less wind
- **Start on knees** – then stand when balanced
- **Watch for boats** – stay in designated areas
- **Bring water** – it's a workout!
