## Why Kayak the 10,000 Islands?

The **10,000 Islands** is one of America's largest undeveloped mangrove estuaries—a maze of islands, channels, and wildlife accessible only by boat. Kayaking is the ultimate way to explore.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | Everglades City, Chokoloskee |
| **Drive Time** | 1 hour from Naples |
| **Guided Tours** | $75-150 |
| **Self-Guided Rentals** | $30-50/day |

---

## Where to Paddle

### Beginner Routes
- **Chokoloskee Bay** – Protected, calm
- **Turner River** – Clear water, manatees
- **Sandfly Island** – Short paddle, beach stop

### Intermediate Routes
- **Indian Key Pass** – More exposed
- **Rabbit Key** – Beach camping possible

### Advanced
- **Wilderness Waterway** – Multi-day expeditions

---

## Wildlife You'll See

- **Dolphins** – Common in channels
- **Manatees** – Especially winter
- **Alligators** – In freshwater areas
- **Crocodiles** – Rare but present
- **Sea turtles** – In open water
- **Hundreds of bird species**

---

## Tips

- **Go with guide first time** – easy to get lost
- **Bring charts/GPS** – if self-guided
- **Tide matters** – time your trip
- **Winter is best** – bugs, weather, wildlife
