## Why Visit The Ringling?

**The Ringling** is one of America's finest museum complexes—featuring a world-class art museum, historic circus museum, stunning gardens, and Ca' d'Zan Venetian mansion on Sarasota Bay.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|

| **Admission** | $25 adults |
| **Hours** | 10 AM - 5 PM (Thu til 8 PM) |

## The Museums

### Museum of Art
- Baroque masterpieces
- Rubens collection
- Italian Renaissance art
- Beautiful courtyard galleries

### Circus Museum
- Historic circus wagons
- Costumes and memorabilia
- Howard Bros. Circus Model (incredible!)
- Celebrates Ringling Bros. heritage

### Ca' d'Zan Mansion
- 36,000 sq ft Venetian Gothic
- John Ringling's winter home
- Stunning waterfront setting
- Extra tour fee ($5)

---

## The Grounds

- 66 acres on Sarasota Bay
- Historic Asolo Theater
- Rose garden
- Bayfront views

---

## Tips

- **Allow 3-4 hours** minimum
- **Thursday evenings** – extended hours, less crowded
- **Don't skip Ca' d'Zan** – it's spectacular
- **Combine with Sarasota** – St. Armands, beaches
