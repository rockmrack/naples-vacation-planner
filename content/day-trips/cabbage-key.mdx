## Why Visit Cabbage Key?

**Cabbage Key** is the legendary island that inspired Jimmy Buffett's "Cheeseburger in Paradise"—a tiny, boat-access-only island with a funky restaurant covered in $70,000+ of signed dollar bills.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Access** | Boat only |
| **From Naples** | 1.5 hours drive + 30 min boat |
| **Boat Tours** | $35-60 |
| **Famous For** | Dollar bill wallpaper, cheeseburger |

---

## Getting There

### From Pine Island
- **Captiva Cruises** – Day trips
- **Tropic Star Cruises** – From Pine Island
- **Private boat** – If you have access

### Typical Day Trip
1. Drive to Pine Island/Pineland Marina
2. Take morning boat tour
3. Lunch at Cabbage Key Inn
4. Explore the island
5. Return afternoon boat

---

## The Experience

### The Restaurant
- Famous cheeseburger ($19+)
- Walls covered in signed dollar bills
- $70,000+ on walls/ceiling
- Bring a dollar to add!

### The Island
- Small (100 acres)
- Historic inn and cottages
- Nature trails
- Old Florida feel

---

## Tips

- **Bring cash** for your dollar bill signature
- **Make lunch reservation** on busy days
- **Don't miss the water tower** view
- **Arrive hungry** – it's about the burger
