## Why Visit St. Armands Circle?

**St. Armands Circle** is Sarasota's premier shopping and dining destination—a European-style circular park surrounded by upscale boutiques, restaurants, and galleries, minutes from Lido Beach.

---

## Quick Facts

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Distance** | ~85 miles |
| **Drive Time** | 90 minutes |
| **Cost** | Free to explore |
| **Vibe** | Upscale, tropical, European |

---

## What to Do

### Shopping
- Upscale boutiques
- Art galleries
- Jewelry stores
- No chains – independent shops

### Dining
- **Columbia Restaurant** – Famous Cuban-Spanish
- **Shore** – Seafood with style
- **Roessler's** – Fine dining
- Many cafes and bars

### The Circle
- Beautiful landscaping
- Statues and fountains
- Pleasant to stroll
- John Ringling created it

---

## Nearby: Lido Beach

Just minutes away:
- Beautiful beach
- Less crowded than Siesta
- Easy parking
- Great combination

---

## Tips

- **Evening is magical** – lights, dinner atmosphere
- **Combine with Lido Beach** – morning beach, afternoon shopping
- **Make dinner reservations** – popular spots book up
- **Window shopping is free** – prices can be high
