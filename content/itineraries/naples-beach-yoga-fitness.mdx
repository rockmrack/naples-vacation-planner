## Fitness in Paradise

There's something magical about sun salutations with sand between your toes and Gulf waves as your soundtrack. Naples offers excellent outdoor fitness options for active travelers.

---

## Beach Yoga Classes

### Regular Classes

| Provider | Location | When | Price |
|----------|----------|------|-------|
| **Vanderbilt Beach Yoga** | Vanderbilt | Mornings | $15-20 |
| **Naples Pier Yoga** | Near pier | Sunrise | $15-20 |
| **Private instructors** | Various | By appointment | $50-100 |

### Hotel/Resort Yoga

| Resort | Offering | Guests Only? |
|--------|----------|--------------|
| **Ritz-Carlton** | Beach/poolside | Yes |
| **LaPlaya** | Morning yoga | Yes |
| **Naples Grande** | Classes | Yes |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Sunrise beach yoga (6:30-7:30 AM) offers the best experience—cool temperatures, calm winds, and magical light. Bring your own mat or rent one. Arrive 15 minutes early.
</ExpertTip>

Yoga on the water for an extra challenge:
