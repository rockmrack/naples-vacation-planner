## Naples: More Accessible Than You Think

While no destination is perfect, Naples offers **surprisingly good accessibility** for travelers with mobility challenges. From beach wheelchairs to accessible boat tours, this guide will help you navigate the Paradise Coast with confidence.

---

## Accessible Beaches

### Beaches with Beach Wheelchairs

| Beach | Wheelchair Rental | Accessible Features |
|-------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Lowdermilk Park** | Free loan | Paved paths, accessible restrooms, shaded areas |
| **Clam Pass Park** | Free loan | Tram to beach, boardwalk |
| **Vanderbilt Beach** | Free loan | Accessible parking, restrooms |
| **Delnor-Wiggins Pass** | Call ahead | State park, accessible boardwalks |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Beach wheelchairs are first-come, first-served. Arrive early (before 10 AM) during peak season. Call the park in advance to confirm availability: Lowdermilk (239) 213-3020.
</ExpertTip>

### How Beach Wheelchairs Work
- Large, balloon-like wheels for sand
- Push-assisted (requires helper)
- Waterproof
- Available at lifeguard stations
- Free of charge (donation appreciated)

## Transportation

### Wheelchair-Accessible Transportation

| Service | Description | Contact |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Manatee Vans** | Van rentals, airport pickup | manateevan.com |
| **Turbo Cab** | 24/7 wheelchair taxi | (239) 507-1111 |
| **Uber WAV** | Wheelchair accessible vehicles | Uber app |
| **Collier Area Transit (CAT)** | Public buses with lifts | catbus.com |

### Rental Vehicles
- Major rental agencies have accessible vehicles
- Book 2+ weeks ahead
- Specify needs at reservation

### Airport Tips (RSW)
- Southwest Florida International is fully accessible
- Curbside wheelchair assistance available
- Pre-arrange van pickup for seamless transfer

### What to Request
- Roll-in shower (not just grab bars)
- Ground floor or elevator access
- Wide doorways (32"+ minimum)
- Accessible parking near entrance
