## Cafe Details

### Narrative Coffee

The best specialty coffee in Naples:
- **Location:** Downtown near 5th Ave
- **Hours:** 7 AM – 5 PM
- **Vibe:** Modern, minimalist
- **Best for:** Coffee purists, remote work
- **WiFi:** Fast and reliable

### Downtown Coffee & Wine

Downtown staple:
- **Location:** 5th Avenue South
- **Hours:** 7 AM – 10 PM
- **Vibe:** Casual, social
- **Best for:** People watching
- **WiFi:** Good

### Cafe Luna

Hidden gem:
- **Location:** 3rd Street South
- **Hours:** 8 AM – 4 PM
- **Vibe:** Garden courtyard
- **Best for:** Relaxed morning
- **WiFi:** Limited
