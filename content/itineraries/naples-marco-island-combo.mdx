## Why Combine Naples + Marco Island?

Naples and Marco Island are just 20 minutes apart, but they offer distinctly different experiences:

| Naples | Marco Island |
|--------|-------------|
| Upscale downtown dining | Quiet island vibes |
| Cultural attractions | Pristine natural beaches |
| Shopping & galleries | Shelling & wildlife |
| Historic charm | Relaxed resort feel |

This 4-day itinerary gives you the best of both worlds.

---

## Day 1: Naples Arrival

### Afternoon: Old Naples

Arrive and settle into Naples:
- Check into your Naples hotel
- Walk the tree-lined streets of Old Naples
- Get oriented on 5th Avenue South

### Evening: 5th Avenue Dinner

Kick off your trip with Naples' best dining:
- **The Local** – farm-to-table excellence
- **Sea Salt** – upscale seafood
- **Vergina** – Mediterranean with great outdoor seating

## Day 3: Marco Island Day

### Morning: Drive to Marco Island

Head south to Marco Island (20 min drive):

**First stop: Tigertail Beach**
- Natural beach with tidal lagoon
- Great for bird watching
- Less crowded than main beaches

### Midday: Lunch at The Snook Inn

The iconic **Snook Inn** offers:
- Waterfront tables
- Live music daily
- Casual seafood
- Watch boats pass by

### Afternoon: Shelling Adventure

Marco Island is known for incredible shelling:

**Option A: South Beach (Driving)**
- Drive to the south end of the island
- Walk the undeveloped beaches
- Best shelling after storms or low tide

**Option B: Shelling Boat Tour (Recommended)**
- Visit uninhabited islands
- More pristine shelling
- Often includes wildlife encounters

## Where to Stay: Split or Single Base?

### Option 1: Stay in Naples (Recommended for 4 days)
- More dining and nightlife options
- Easy day trips to Marco Island
- Better for first-time visitors

### Option 2: Split Your Stay
- 2 nights Naples, 2 nights Marco Island
- Deeper immersion in each destination
- Good for return visitors

### Option 3: Stay on Marco Island
- Quieter, more resort-like
- Shorter drive to island activities
- Fewer dining options
