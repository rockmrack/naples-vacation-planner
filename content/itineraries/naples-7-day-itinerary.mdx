## The Art of Slowing Down in Naples

A full week in Naples is a gift. Unlike rushed itineraries, this 7-day plan embraces Naples' relaxed pace—beach mornings, leisurely lunches, afternoon naps, and sunset cocktails. Plus day trips to Marco Island and the Everglades.

---

## Day 1: Arrival & Old Naples Orientation

### Afternoon: Check In & Explore

Settle into your accommodations, then take a casual stroll through **Old Naples**:
- Walk the tree-lined residential streets
- Peek at the historic cottages
- Get your bearings

### Evening: 3rd Street South

Kick off your week on charming **3rd Street South**:
- Browse galleries at a leisurely pace
- Dinner at **Ridgway Bar & Grill** or **Campiello**
- End with gelato

---

## Day 2: Classic Naples Beach Day

### All Day: Naples Pier Area

No rush today. Just beach:
- Morning walk on Naples Pier
- Set up at the beach south of the pier
- Long lunch break at **The Turtle Club** (beachfront)
- Afternoon swimming and reading
- Stay for sunset at the pier

**This is what Naples does best—give yourself permission to do nothing.**

---

## Day 3: Nature & Gardens

### Morning: Naples Botanical Garden

Spend a peaceful morning at the **Naples Botanical Garden**:
- 170 acres of cultivated beauty
- Brazilian Garden waterfalls
- Orchid Garden
- Allow 2–3 hours

### Afternoon: Corkscrew Swamp Sanctuary

Drive 30 minutes to **Corkscrew Swamp**:
- 2.25-mile boardwalk through ancient cypress
- Bird watching paradise
- Air plants and orchids
- Look for the resident barred owls

### Evening: Quiet Night In

Cook a simple meal or order in. Rest up for tomorrow's adventure.

---

## Day 5: Marco Island Day Trip

### All Day: Marco Island

Explore Naples' island neighbor (see our [Marco Island guide](/day-trips/marco-island-from-naples)):

**Morning:**
- Drive to Marco Island (20 min)
- **Tigertail Beach** – natural, less crowded
- Look for sand dollars and shells

**Lunch:**
- **The Snook Inn** – waterfront, live music, casual vibes

**Afternoon:**
- Drive to the south end for shelling
- OR book a shelling boat tour to uninhabited islands

## Day 6: Culture & Relaxation

### Morning: Your Choice

**Option A: Spa Day**
- Book a morning treatment
- Pool time afterward

**Option B: Artis—Naples / Baker Museum**
- World-class art collection
- Often overlooked by tourists

**Option C: Golf**
- Naples has excellent courses
- Book tee times in advance during peak season

### Afternoon: Venetian Village

Stroll the bay-front **Venetian Village**:
- Upscale boutiques
- Lunch with water views
- Watch the paddle boarders

## Day 7: Farewell Naples

### Morning: Last Beach Session

- Early morning pier walk
- Final swim in the Gulf
- Collect a few shells as souvenirs

### Brunch: 5th Avenue

Treat yourself to a farewell brunch:
- **The Local** – excellent brunch menu
- **Barbatella** – weekend brunch
- **USS Nemo** – if you love seafood

### Departure

Head to RSW Airport (20–30 min from downtown Naples).
