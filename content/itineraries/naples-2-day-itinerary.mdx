### Late Morning: Naples Pier

## Day 2: Sunset Cruise & Farewell

### Morning: Spa or Garden

Choose your relaxation style:

**Option A: Spa Morning**
- The Inn on Fifth Spa
- The Ritz-Carlton Spa

**Option B: Naples Botanical Garden**
- 170 acres of stunning cultivated gardens
- Brazilian Garden waterfalls are especially romantic
- Allow 2 hours; arrive when it opens at 9am

### Afternoon: Venetian Village
