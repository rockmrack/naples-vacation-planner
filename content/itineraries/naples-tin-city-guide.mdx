## A Slice of Old Florida

While 5th Avenue gets all the attention, savvy visitors know to wander down to **Tin City**—a collection of weathered tin-roofed buildings along the historic Naples waterfront. It's laid-back, local, and offers a charming alternative to the polished downtown scene.

---

## What Is Tin City?

Built on the site of a 1920s clam shelling plant, Tin City preserves Naples' **fishing village roots**. Today it's home to:
- 40+ unique shops and boutiques
- Waterfront restaurants
- Boat tour departures
- Local crafts and art
- Old Florida atmosphere

### The Vibe

| 5th Avenue | Tin City |
|------------|----------|
| Upscale, polished | Rustic, casual |
| Designer boutiques | Local artisans |
| Fine dining | Casual seafood |
| Dressed up | Come as you are |

---

## What to Do at Tin City

### 1. Explore the Shops

| Shop Type | What You'll Find |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Local art galleries** | Florida-inspired paintings, sculptures |
| **Souvenir shops** | Shells, beach decor, T-shirts |
| **Clothing boutiques** | Casual beachwear |
| **Specialty stores** | Hot sauce, local honey, sea glass jewelry |
| **Toy stores** | Unique kids' finds |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** The shops at Tin City are locally owned—not chain stores. You'll find unique souvenirs you won't see at the mall. Perfect for memorable gifts.
</ExpertTip>

### 2. Waterfront Dining

| Restaurant | Vibe | Known For |
|------------|------|-----------|
| **Pinchers** | Casual family | Stone crabs, seafood baskets |
| **Riverwalk at Tin City** | Waterfront views | Fresh seafood, sunsets |
| **The Dock at Crayton Cove** | Nearby, upscale casual | Seafood, harbor views |

### 3. Take a Boat Tour

Tin City is the **departure point** for many Naples boat tours:

| Tour | Duration | Highlights |
|------|----------|------------|
| **Naples Princess** | 1.5–3 hours | Sightseeing, sunset cruises |
| **Dolphin Watching** | 1.5 hours | Wildlife spotting |
| **Backwater Fishing** | Half-day | Guided fishing charters |
| **Shelling Tours** | 3+ hours | Keewaydin Island |

### 4. Watch the Sunset

The Tin City waterfront faces west across Naples Bay—**perfect for sunset viewing**:
- Grab a drink at Pinchers
- Sit on the benches along the water
- Watch boats return to harbor

## Crayton Cove: The Artistic Neighbor

Just a short walk from Tin City is **Crayton Cove**, Naples' bohemian arts neighborhood:

### What You'll Find

| Spot | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **von Liebig Art Center** | Exhibitions, art classes |
| **Delnor-Wiggins Gallery** | Local artists |
| **Phil's 41** | Neighborhood bar |
| **The Dock at Crayton Cove** | Upscale-casual dining |

The area has a more artistic, less commercial feel than either 5th Avenue or Tin City.

## Annual Events

### Stone Crab Festival

Tin City hosts the annual **Stone Crab Festival** (late October):
- Fresh stone crab claws
- Live music
- Family-friendly atmosphere
- Celebrates the start of stone crab season
