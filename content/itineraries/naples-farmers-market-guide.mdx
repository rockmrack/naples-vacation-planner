## Shop Like a Local

Skip the tourist traps and experience Naples like residents do—at the vibrant Saturday farmers markets, boutique grocers, and specialty food shops that define the local food scene.

---

## Saturday Farmers Markets

### 3rd Street South Farmers Market (Top Pick)

The crown jewel of Naples markets:

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **When** | Saturdays, 7:30 AM – 11:30 AM |
| **Where** | Behind Tommy Bahama, 3rd Street South |
| **Season** | November – April |
| **Parking** | Street parking or garage |

**What You'll Find:**
- Fresh local produce
- Artisan breads and pastries
- Local honey and jams
- Fresh flowers
- Prepared foods and coffee
- Live music

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Arrive by 8 AM for best selection and to beat the crowds. Bring cash and a reusable bag. The market is dog-friendly!
</ExpertTip>

### Other Markets

| Market | When | Location |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Celebration Farmers Market** | Fri/Sat/Sun | Celebration Park |
| **Naples Beach Farmers Market** | Sundays | Location varies |

## Local Grocers & Specialty Shops

### Wynn's Market

A Naples institution since 1938:
- **Location:** Tamiami Trail North
- **Known for:** Prepared foods, deli, local products
- **Vibe:** Classic Florida grocery

### Food & Thought

Organic and natural foods:
- **Location:** Tamiami Trail North
- **Known for:** Organic produce, hot bar, juice bar
- **Vibe:** Health-focused

### Oakes Farms Seed to Table

Farm-to-market experience:
- **Location:** Immokalee Road
- **Known for:** Local produce, butcher, bakery
- **Vibe:** Modern farm market
