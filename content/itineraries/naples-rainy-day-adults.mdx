## When the Rain Falls

Florida's afternoon storms are legendary. Instead of watching from your hotel window, turn a rainy day into an opportunity for culture, relaxation, and culinary exploration.

---

### Cultural

|----------|----------|------|

| **Revs Institute** | 2.5 hours | ~$20 |
| **Naples Players** | 2-3 hours | ~$40 |
| **Philharmonic** | 2-3 hours | $30-100 |

## Best Restaurants for Long Lunches

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Naples afternoon storms typically hit between 2-5 PM and last 1-2 hours. Plan indoor activities for this window. Mornings and evenings are usually clear.
</ExpertTip>
