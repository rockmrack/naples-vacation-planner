## Art in the Open Air

Naples' downtown streets are an outdoor gallery. World-class sculptures, murals, and installations line 5th Avenue, 3rd Street, and Crayton Cove—offering a free, self-guided art experience.

---

| Sculpture | Location | Artist |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Keeper of the Plains** | 5th Ave & 8th St | Various |
| **Bronze dolphins** | Near pier | Local artist |
| **Seasonal installations** | Cambier Park | Rotating |

### 3rd Street South

| Sculpture | Location | Notes |

### Crayton Cove

The artistic heart of Naples:
- Working artist studios
- Von Liebig Art Center
- Outdoor sculpture garden

## Walking Tour Route

### Suggested 2-Hour Walk

| Stop | Location | Time |
|------|----------|------|
| 1 | Start at Naples Pier | 9:00 AM |
| 2 | Walk 12th Ave to 5th Ave | 9:15 AM |
| 3 | 5th Avenue galleries | 9:30 AM |
| 4 | Cambier Park | 10:00 AM |
| 5 | 3rd Street South | 10:30 AM |
| 6 | Crayton Cove | 11:00 AM |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Morning light (before 10 AM) is best for photography. Many galleries open at 10 AM—time your walk to catch them. First Wednesday Gallery Walks (5-8 PM) feature special exhibitions.
</ExpertTip>

## Indoor Art Experiences

### The Baker Museum (Artis—Naples)

Naples' premier art museum:
- **Collection:** Modern & contemporary
- **Hours:** 10 AM – 4 PM (closed Mondays)
- **Cost:** ~$15 adults
- **Time needed:** 2-3 hours

### Naples Art Association

- Art classes and workshops
- Member exhibitions
- Community gallery

---

## First Wednesday Art Walks

Monthly gallery events:
- **When:** First Wednesday, 5-8 PM
- **Where:** Naples Art District
- **Cost:** Free
- **What:** Gallery openings, artist receptions, light refreshments
