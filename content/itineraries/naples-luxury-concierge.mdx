## Private Yacht & Boat Experiences

### Yacht Charters

| Experience | Duration | Starting Price |
|------------|----------|----------------|
| **Sunset cruise (private)** | 3 hours | $1,500-3,000 |
| **Half-day yacht** | 4-5 hours | $3,000-5,000 |
| **Full-day island hop** | 8 hours | $5,000-10,000 |
| **Overnight charter** | 24+ hours | $10,000+ |

**Includes:** Captain, crew, catering available, water toys

### Exclusive Add-Ons

- Private chef onboard
- Champagne & caviar service
- Jet ski toys
- Sunset dinner at anchor
