## Naples on a Budget? Absolutely Possible.

Naples has a reputation for wealth and luxury—but the **best things here are free or affordable**. World-class beaches, stunning sunsets, beautiful downtown strolling, and nature preserves don't require a VIP budget.

---

## Free & Nearly Free Activities

### Beaches (All Free!)

| Beach | Parking | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| **Naples Pier Beach** | $5/hour or free on side streets | Iconic, central |
| **Lowdermilk Park** | $5/hour | Facilities, family-friendly |
| **Clam Pass** | $10/day (Pelican Bay) | Tram ride, secluded |
| **Vanderbilt Beach** | $5/hour | Beautiful, less crowded |
| **Barefoot Beach Preserve** | $8/vehicle | Natural, uncrowded |

**Budget tip:** Park on residential streets near beaches for free (check signs).

### Free Nature & Parks

| Attraction | Cost | Experience |
|------------|------|------------|
| **Naples Pier sunset** | Free | Iconic, can't miss |
| **Naples Preserve** | Free | 90-acre nature preserve |
| **Gordon River Greenway** | Free | Scenic walking/biking trail |
| **Baker Park** | Free | New park, kayak launch |
| **Farmer's Market (3rd St)** | Free | Saturday mornings |

### Free Downtown Strolling

- **5th Avenue South:** Window shopping, people watching, architecture
- **3rd Street South:** Galleries, boutiques, garden courtyards
- **Crayton Cove:** Artistic neighborhood, harbor views
- **Tin City:** Waterfront, local shops

## Best Value Dining

### Happy Hours (Save 30–50%)

Naples restaurants offer **excellent happy hour deals**, typically 4–6 PM:

| Restaurant | Happy Hour Deal |
|------------|-----------------|
| **Tommy Bahama** | Discounted apps, drinks |
| **The Local** | Half-price small plates |
| **Bar Lurcat** | Wine specials |
| **Café & Bar Lurcat** | Discounted wine, apps |
| **Bonefish Grill** | Bang Bang Shrimp specials |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Many upscale Naples restaurants have happy hour menus that let you experience their cuisine at half the dinner price. Eat "dinner" at 5 PM and you'll save significantly.
</ExpertTip>

Buy supplies and picnic at the beach:
- **Publix:** Deli sandwiches, salads, beach-ready
- **Wynn's Market:** Local grocery, prepared foods
- **Naples Park:** Publix with good selection

## Money-Saving Tips

### Transportation
- Naples is walkable downtown—no car needed for 5th/3rd Ave
- Free downtown trolley (seasonal)
- Share Uber to beaches

### Experiences
- Skip paid tours; beaches and sunsets are free
- State parks ($6–10) offer full-day nature experiences
- Skip the $25 attraction; walk the Botanical Garden perimeter trail (free)

### Food & Drink
- BYOB wine to beach (permitted)
- Early bird specials (4–6 PM)
- Breakfast at your hotel (if included)
- Coffee shop for lunch (cheaper than restaurants)
