## Why Naples for a Girlfriend Getaway?

Naples offers the perfect combination for a memorable girls' trip: **world-class spas**, **upscale shopping**, **gorgeous beaches**, and a **dining and cocktail scene** that's sophisticated without being pretentious. Leave the kids, partners, and stress behind.

---

## The Essential Girls' Trip Ingredients

### What Naples Does Best

| Experience | Why It's Great |
|------------|----------------|
| **Spa Day** | Ritz-Carlton, LaPlaya, boutique options |
| **Shopping** | 5th Ave, 3rd Street, Waterside Shops |
| **Cocktails** | Rooftop bars, wine bars, happy hours |
| **Beach** | Pristine white sand, no hassle |
| **Dining** | From casual brunch to elegant dinner |

---

## Sample 3-Day Girlfriend Getaway

### Day 1: Arrival & Downtown Discovery

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning | Arrive, check into hotel |
| Lunch | **Barbatella** (Italian, shareable plates) |
| Afternoon | Stroll **5th Avenue South** (shopping, gallery hopping) |
| Happy Hour | **Bar Lurcat** (wine, apps) |
| Dinner | **Sea Salt** (upscale seafood, group-friendly) |
| Nightcap | Rooftop at **The Inn on Fifth** |

### Day 2: Spa Day & Beach

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning | **Spa at the Ritz-Carlton** or **LaPlaya** (book ahead!) |
| Post-Spa | Light lunch at spa or **The Local** |
| Afternoon | **Lowdermilk Beach** or **Clam Pass** (relax, recover) |
| Sunset | Beach cocktails or **Naples Pier** |
| Dinner | **Osteria Tulia** (wood-fired Italian) |
| Evening | **Decanted Wine Bar** |

### Day 3: Brunch & Shop 'Til You Drop

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning | **Brunch at The Bay House** (waterfront) |
| Late Morning | **Waterside Shops** (Nordstrom, Saks, boutiques) |
| Lunch | **Brio** or **Seasons 52** (at Waterside) |
| Afternoon | **3rd Street South** (boutiques, galleries) |
| Evening | Farewell dinner at **HobNob** (French bistro) |

## Top Spa Experiences

### Luxury Spa Options

| Spa | Vibe | Signature |
|-----|------|-----------|
| **Spa at The Ritz-Carlton** | Ultimate luxury | Gulf views, full day packages |
| **LaPlaya Beach & Golf Spa** | Beachfront zen | Couples massages, facials |
| **The Inn on Fifth Spa** | Boutique downtown | Convenient, intimate |
| **Woodhouse Spa** | Day spa | Great value |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Book spa appointments at least 2 weeks ahead during peak season. Many spas offer "girlfriend packages" with group discounts. Ask about pool/beach access after treatments.
</ExpertTip>

## Practical Tips

### Where to Stay

| Hotel | Best For | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **The Inn on Fifth** | Downtown access | Walk everywhere, rooftop bar |
| **LaPlaya Beach & Golf** | Beachfront + spa | Romantic, relaxed |
| **Naples Grande Beach** | Full resort | Beach, spa, golf |
| **Hyatt Regency Coconut Point** | Value option | 20 min away, great pool |

### Transportation
- **Downtown:** Walk everywhere
- **Beach day:** Uber/Lyft or rental car
- **Wine outings:** Use rideshare (no DUI!)
