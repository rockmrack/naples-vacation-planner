## Paradise Doesn't Cost a Thing

Naples' best experiences are free. The beaches, sunsets, downtown charm, and nature trails don't require a ticket or reservation. Here are 25 things to do without spending a dime.

---

## Beaches (All Free!)

| Beach | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Naples Pier Beach** | Iconic, central |
| **Lowdermilk Park** | Family-friendly, facilities |
| **Vanderbilt Beach** | Wide, beautiful |
| **Clam Pass** | Natural, scenic |
| **Barefoot Beach Preserve** | Uncrowded, shells |

*Note: Parking fees apply at most beaches ($5-10), but the beach itself is always free.*

---

## Nature & Outdoors

| Activity | Location | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| **Naples Preserve** | Tamiami Trail | 90-acre sanctuary |
| **Gordon River Greenway** | Downtown | 2.5-mile trail |
| **Baker Park** | Downtown | New park, beautiful |
| **Cambier Park** | Downtown | Banyan trees, events |
| **Delnor-Wiggins (outside)** | North Naples | Walk barriers for free |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Naples Preserve is completely free—no admission, no parking fee. It's one of the best-kept secrets for a quiet nature walk.
</ExpertTip>

## Free Beach Parking Hacks

- **Street parking** near beaches often free (check signs)
- **Arrive before 8 AM** for easier spots
- **Walk from downtown** to pier beach
- **Beach access points** sometimes have free spots
