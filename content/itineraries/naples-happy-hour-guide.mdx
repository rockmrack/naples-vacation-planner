## Cheers to Savings

Naples may be known for luxury, but smart visitors know the secret: **happy hour**. From 4-6 PM, even the most upscale restaurants offer significant discounts on drinks and apps.

## Happy Hour Tips

1. **Bar seating** usually required for HH pricing
2. **Arrive early** (4-4:30 PM) for best seats
3. **Cash isn't necessary** but tipping well keeps service strong
4. **Check for sunset** timing—plan accordingly
5. **Friday & Saturday** are busiest—weekday HH is more relaxed
