## Why Seniors Love Naples

Naples consistently ranks as one of the **best retirement destinations in America**—and for good reason. With its safe, walkable downtown, world-class healthcare, championship golf, and refined cultural scene, it's perfectly suited for travelers seeking **relaxation without sacrificing quality**.

## The Naples Senior Advantage

### Why It Works

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Mild winter weather** | Escape the cold (avg 75°F) |
| **Walkable downtown** | No car needed for 5th Ave/3rd Street |
| **World-class healthcare** | NCH system, specialists nearby |
| **Golf paradise** | 90+ courses in the area |
| **Cultural scene** | Artis—Naples, theater, galleries |
| **Low crime** | One of Florida's safest cities |

## Practical Tips for Senior Travelers

### Healthcare
- **NCH Baker Hospital:** Downtown Naples, full-service
- **Pharmacies:** CVS and Walgreens throughout
- **Walk-in clinics:** Multiple locations

### Transportation
- **Trolley:** Free downtown trolley runs seasonally
- **Uber/Lyft:** Widely available
- **Rental cars:** Recommended for day trips
- **Wheelchair vans:** Manatee Vans provides airport pickup

### Weather Considerations

| Season | Temperature | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Nov–Apr** | 65–80°F | Perfect, dry, peak season |
| **May–Jun** | 80–90°F | Warm, some rain |
| **Jul–Sep** | 85–95°F | Hot, afternoon storms |
| **Oct** | 75–85°F | Transitioning to peak |

### What to Pack
- [ ] Light layers (evening can be cool)
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Sun hat and sunglasses
- [ ] Medications with copies of prescriptions
- [ ] Light rain jacket (just in case)
