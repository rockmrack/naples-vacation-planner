## Wild Florida, Close to Naples

Beyond the manicured beaches and golf courses lies wild Florida—mangrove forests, untouched beaches, and ancient cypress swamps. State parks near Naples offer authentic natural experiences.

## Park Details

### Delnor-Wiggins Pass State Park

Naples' premier natural beach:
- **Entry:** $6/vehicle
- **Highlights:** Pristine beach, great shelling, sea turtles (nesting season)
- **Facilities:** Restrooms, showers, concession
- **Best for:** Beach lovers, families

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Arrive by 9 AM on weekends—the parking lot fills up. Bring snorkeling gear for the pass area where fish congregate.
</ExpertTip>

### Collier-Seminole State Park

Wild Florida experience:
- **Entry:** $5/vehicle
- **Highlights:** 6+ miles of trails, kayak launch, historic dredge
- **Facilities:** Picnic areas, boat ramp, camping
- **Best for:** Hikers, kayakers, adventure seekers

### Fakahatchee Strand Preserve

The "Amazon of North America":
- **Entry:** Free
- **Highlights:** Boardwalk, ghost orchids, rare wildlife
- **Facilities:** Minimal
- **Best for:** Serious naturalists, photographers
