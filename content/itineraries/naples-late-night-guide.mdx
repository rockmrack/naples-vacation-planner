## What to Know

### Naples Late-Night Reality

- **Peak hours:** 10 PM – 12 AM
- **True "club" scene:** Limited (head to Fort Myers or Miami)
- **Dress code:** Smart casual most places
- **Age range:** 30s-50s dominate
- **Vibe:** Sophisticated, not rowdy

### Where NOT to Go

- Most 5th Avenue restaurants close by 10 PM
- Downtown is quiet by 11 PM weeknights
- Beach areas have no late-night scene
