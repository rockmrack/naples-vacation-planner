## Top 3 Workspaces

### 1. Kunjani Craft Coffee (The Vibe)
Located across from Waterside Shops.
*   **Pros:** Fast wifi, incredible coffee, African-inspired decor, quiet corners.
*   **Cons:** closes early (usually 3 PM).

## The "After 5 PM" Routine

### Monday: Sunset at the Pier
Close the laptop, walk the beach.

### Wednesday: Trivia Night
Check **Bone Hook Brewing**. Great local brewery scene to decompress.

### Friday: Ave Bar at The Inn on 5th
Celebrate the weekend with a martini on the balcony watching the street below.
