## Escape the Cold

While temperatures drop up north, Naples enjoys perfect 70-80°F weather, sunny skies, and peak season energy. Here's how to make the most of your winter paradise.

---

## Winter Weather

### What to Expect

| Month | High | Low | Rain Days |
|-------|------|-----|-----------|
| **December** | 75°F | 55°F | 3 |
| **January** | 74°F | 54°F | 4 |
| **February** | 76°F | 55°F | 3 |
| **March** | 79°F | 59°F | 4 |

### Winter Reality

- **Mornings:** Cool (50s-60s), perfect for outdoor activities
- **Afternoons:** Warm (70s), beach weather
- **Evenings:** Pleasant, light layers needed
- **Rain:** Rare, brief if any

## Long-Stay Options

### Monthly Rentals

| Type | Location | Monthly Range |
|------|----------|---------------|
| **Condo (1BR)** | Downtown | $4,000-8,000 |
| **Condo (2BR)** | Beach area | $6,000-12,000 |
| **House** | Various | $8,000-20,000 |

### Popular Snowbird Communities

| Community | Vibe | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| **Pelican Bay** | Upscale, beach | Tram to beach |
| **Park Shore** | Waterfront | Venetian Village |
| **Moorings** | Classic Naples | Near downtown |
| **Naples Park** | Affordable | Close to Vanderbilt |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Book winter rentals by August—popular properties go fast. Consider shoulder months (November, April) for better rates with similar weather.
</ExpertTip>
