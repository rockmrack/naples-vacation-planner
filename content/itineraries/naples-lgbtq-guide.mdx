## Naples: Warmer Than the Weather

While Naples isn't typically thought of as an LGBTQ+ destination like Miami or Key West, the **Paradise Coast is evolving**. Thanks to community organizations like Naples Pride and supportive businesses, Naples offers a warm, welcoming experience for LGBTQ+ travelers.

---

## Naples Pride Scene

### Annual Events

| Event | When | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Naples Pride Festival** | June (Pride Month) | Main celebration at Cambier Park, thousands attend |
| **Pride Alliance Events** | Year-round | Community gatherings, networking |
| **SWFL Pride** | June | Regional celebrations |

### Naples Pride Center

The **Naples Pride Center** is a hub for the LGBTQ+ community:
- Support groups and counseling
- Community events
- Resources for visitors
- Alliance business directory

**Location:** Downtown Naples
**Website:** naplespride.org
