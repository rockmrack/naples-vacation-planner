## Be Your Own Captain

Want to explore Naples waterways at your own pace? Rent a boat and discover hidden islands, dolphin pods, and secluded beaches that tour boats don't reach.

## Safety Tips

1. **Weather check** before departure
2. **Phone in waterproof bag** — essential
3. **Stay in marked channels** when unfamiliar
4. **Anchor away from grass** (seagrass protected)
5. **Watch for manatee zones** — slow speeds required
6. **Return before storms** — afternoon storms common
