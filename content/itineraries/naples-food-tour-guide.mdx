## Naples: A Foodie's Paradise

Beyond the famous stone crabs, Naples boasts a **sophisticated culinary scene** that rivals cities twice its size. From guided food tours to hands-on cooking classes, this guide helps you eat like a local.

---

## Guided Food Tours

### Paradise Food Tours

The premier food tour company in Southwest Florida:

| Tour | Duration | Price | Highlights |
|------|----------|-------|------------|
| **Feast on 5th** | 3 hours | ~$85 | 5th Ave restaurants, upscale |
| **Ciao Down Naples** | 3 hours | ~$85 | Italian focus |
| **Nacho Average Taco** | 3 hours | ~$75 | Mexican & Latin |
| **A Slice of Naples** | 3 hours | ~$75 | Pizza tour |
| **Brunch Bunch** | 2.5 hours | ~$75 | Weekend brunch |
| **Dolce Vita Desserts** | 2 hours | ~$65 | Sweet tour |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Book "Feast on 5th" for the most comprehensive Naples food experience. Tours include tastings at 5–6 restaurants, cultural insights, and walking through the beautiful downtown.
</ExpertTip>
