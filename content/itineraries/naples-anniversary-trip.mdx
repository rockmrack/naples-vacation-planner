## Celebrate Your Love Story

Anniversary trips deserve special touches. Naples offers the perfect backdrop for romance—sunset beach dinners, couples' spa treatments, private tours, and moments you'll treasure forever.

---

## Romantic Accommodations

### Splurge-Worthy Options

| Hotel | Why It's Special | Starting |
|-------|------------------|----------|
| **The Ritz-Carlton** | Ultimate luxury, beach | $$$$ |
| **LaPlaya Beach** | Intimate beachfront | $$$$ |
| **Inn on Fifth** | Downtown, rooftop | $$$ |

### Anniversary Package Tips
- Ask about anniversary packages when booking
- Mention your celebration—many hotels add special touches
- Request room upgrades (especially mid-week)
