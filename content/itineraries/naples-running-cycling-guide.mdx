## Stay Active in Paradise

Naples offers **excellent running and cycling** for active travelers. From scenic waterfront paths to shaded nature trails, you can maintain your fitness routine while enjoying Florida's natural beauty.

---

## Top Running Routes

### Urban Routes

| Route | Distance | Highlights |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Gulf Shore Blvd** | 2–6 miles | Waterfront, flat, scenic |
| **5th Ave to Naples Pier** | 2 miles RT | Downtown, ocean views |
| **Lowdermilk to Pier** | 3 miles RT | Beach parallel |

### Trail Running

| Route | Distance | Highlights |
|-------|----------|------------|
| **Gordon River Greenway** | 2.5 miles | Shaded, paved, beautiful |
| **Baker Park** | 1+ mile loops | New park, varied terrain |
| **Naples Preserve** | 1.5 miles | Nature trails |

### Beach Running

| Beach | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Lowdermilk to Pier** | Hard-packed near water |
| **Vanderbilt Beach** | Wide, firm sand |
| **Barefoot Beach** | Natural, scenic |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Run early morning (6–8 AM) to avoid heat. The sand near the waterline is firmest 1–2 hours before low tide. Bring water—Florida is deceptively humid.
</ExpertTip>

## Featured Route: Gordon River Greenway

The **best trail in Naples** for runners and cyclists:

### Details
- **Length:** 2.5 miles (5 miles round trip)
- **Surface:** Paved, smooth
- **Shade:** Mostly shaded
- **Connects to:** Naples Botanical Garden

### What You'll See
- Gordon River views
- Mangrove forests
- Wildlife (herons, turtles)
- Connecting bridges
- Exercise stations

### Access Points
- Naples Botanical Garden (south end)
- Fleischmann Park (north end)
- Parking at both locations

## Practical Tips

### When to Run/Ride

| Time | Pros | Cons |
|------|------|------|
| **5–8 AM** | Cool, quiet, sunrise | Early wake-up |
| **4–6 PM** | Cooling down | Summer storms possible |
| **Avoid midday** | — | Dangerous heat in summer |

### What to Bring

- [ ] Water (hydration pack or bottle)
- [ ] Sunscreen
- [ ] Sunglasses
- [ ] Hat or visor
- [ ] Phone for navigation
- [ ] Cash (some rentals are cash only)

### Safety Tips

1. **Hydrate aggressively** (Florida humidity is intense)
2. **Wear bright colors** (visibility on roads)
3. **Start early** in summer (heat is serious)
4. **Know your limits** (adjust for humidity)
5. **Lightning awareness** (afternoon storms)
