## Celebrate Before "I Do"

Naples offers a sophisticated alternative to the typical party destinations. Think private boat charters, world-class golf, luxury spas, and beach time—without the Vegas chaos.

---

## Bachelorette Itinerary

### The Agenda

| Day | Theme | Activities |
|-----|-------|------------|
| **Day 1** | Arrival & Vibe | Check in, pool party, 5th Ave dinner |
| **Day 2** | Pamper & Party | Spa morning, beach, rooftop drinks |
| **Day 3** | Boat & Brunch | Boat charter, farewell brunch |

### Top Experiences

| Activity | Duration | Group Price |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Group spa package** | 3-4 hours | $150-300/person |
| **Private sunset cruise** | 3 hours | $800-1500 total |
| **Poolside cabana** | Half-day | $200-500 |
| **Private yoga** | 1.5 hours | $200-300 total |

## Group Accommodations

### Best Options

| Type | Best For | Price Range |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Vacation rental** | Full group together | $300-800/night |
| **Resort (adjoining rooms)** | Luxury amenities | $400-700/night |
| **Inn on Fifth** | Downtown walking | $350-500/night |

### Neighborhood Tips

- **Downtown:** Walking to restaurants, bars
- **Vanderbilt Beach:** Pool, beach access
- **Marco Island:** Secluded, larger rentals

## Group Dining

### For Large Groups

| Restaurant | Capacity | Vibe |
|------------|----------|------|
| **Tommy Bahama** | Private room | Island |
| **Brio** | Large groups | Italian |
| **The Continental** | Semi-private | Classic |
| **Barbatella** | Communal tables | Lively Italian |

### Make Reservations

- Book 2-3 weeks ahead for groups of 8+
- Mention it's a bachelor/bachelorette party
- Ask about prix-fixe or group menus
