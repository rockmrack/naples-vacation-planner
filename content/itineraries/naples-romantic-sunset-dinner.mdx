## Romance at Golden Hour

There's something magical about dining as the sun dips into the Gulf of Mexico. Naples offers some of Florida's most romantic sunset dining experiences—the key is knowing where to book.

---

## Restaurant Details

### The Turtle Club (Top Pick)

The ultimate Naples romantic beach dining:
- **Location:** Vanderbilt Beach
- **Vibe:** Toes-in-the-sand elegance
- **Must-order:** Fresh catch, key lime pie
- **Reserve:** 2+ weeks ahead
- **Dress:** Resort casual (no shoes required!)

### Baleen at LaPlaya

Upscale beachfront sophistication:
- **Location:** LaPlaya Beach Resort
- **Vibe:** Modern, elegant
- **Must-order:** Stone crab, craft cocktails
- **Reserve:** 1-2 weeks ahead
- **Dress:** Smart casual

### The Bay House

Waterfront American with stunning views:
- **Location:** Naples Bay
- **Vibe:** Classic, refined
- **Must-order:** Seasonal seafood, wine flight
- **Reserve:** 1 week ahead
- **Dress:** Business casual
