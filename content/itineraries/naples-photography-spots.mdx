## Capture Paradise

Naples is photographers' paradise—pristine beaches, dramatic sunsets, Spanish-style architecture, and abundant wildlife. Here's where to point your lens.

---

## Best Sunrise Spots

| Location | Best For | Arrive By |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Naples Pier** | Classic pier shot | 30 min before sunrise |
| **Lowdermilk Beach** | Wide beach view | 20 min before sunrise |
| **Delnor-Wiggins** | Natural beach | 30 min before sunrise |

### Sunrise Tips
- Summer sunrise: ~6:30 AM
- Winter sunrise: ~7:00 AM
- Arrive early for calm water reflections
- Bring coffee—it's worth the early alarm

---

## Best Sunset Spots

| Location | Best For | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| **Naples Pier** | Iconic | Crowded but worth it |
| **Vanderbilt Beach** | Wide views | Less crowded |
| **Clam Pass** | Secluded | Natural setting |
| **Bayfront** | Boat reflections | Downtown access |

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Naples Pier sunsets are magical but crowded. For a more intimate experience, try Clam Pass or Barefoot Beach. The 10 minutes after sunset (blue hour) often yields the best colors.
</ExpertTip>

## Wildlife Photography

| Location | Wildlife | Best Season |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Corkscrew Swamp** | Herons, owls, gators | Year-round |
| **Ding Darling** | Spoonbills, egrets | Winter |
| **Naples Pier** | Dolphins, pelicans | Year-round |
| **Clam Pass** | Shorebirds | Year-round |

### Equipment Tips
- 200-400mm for wildlife
- Polarizer for water shots
- Tripod for low light
- Waterproof cover for boat trips
