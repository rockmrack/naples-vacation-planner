## Restaurant Details

### Skillets (Local Favorite)

The Naples brunch institution:
- **Location:** Multiple locations
- **Hours:** 7 AM – 2 PM
- **Signature:** Pancake flights, eggs
- **Wait:** Common on weekends, arrive early
- **Tip:** The Pine Ridge location is usually less crowded

### The Bay House

Waterfront elegance:
- **Location:** Naples Bay
- **Hours:** Brunch Sat-Sun 10 AM – 2 PM
- **Signature:** Seafood brunch, views
- **Reserve:** Recommended
- **Vibe:** Upscale casual
