## How to pick the right beach for your day

Instead of hunting for “the best beach,” pick based on constraints:

- Do you need easy parking?
- Are you traveling with kids (gear + shade)?
- Are you going for sunset (arrive earlier in peak season)?

---

## Timing tips

- In peak season (Jan–Mar), arrive earlier than you think for stress-free parking.
- In summer, go early to avoid heat and afternoon storms.

## Quick checklist

- Water
- Sunscreen
- Shade plan (hat/umbrella)
- Snacks
- Towel + change of clothes
