## Old-World Italian Elegance

Capriccio transports diners to an elegant Italian past. With live piano music, impeccable service, and traditional Italian preparations, it's a dining experience from another era.

## The Cuisine

Authentic Italian with no shortcuts:
- **Osso Buco:** The Milanese classic, perfection
- **Fresh Pasta:** Made daily by hand
- **Veal Piccata:** Tender, lemony, classic
- **Seafood:** Fresh preparations nightly

## The Atmosphere

The lounge atmosphere includes live piano music most evenings, creating a sophisticated background for dinner. It's intimate, romantic, and genuinely elegant.

## Special Occasions

Capriccio excels at:
- **Anniversaries:** The piano sets the mood
- **Proposals:** Romantic and private
- **Birthdays:** Tableside celebrations done right

## Tips

- **Reservations recommended:** Popular with regulars
- **Dress code:** Smart casual to dressy
- **Closed Sundays:** Plan accordingly
- **Live music:** Adds to the experience
