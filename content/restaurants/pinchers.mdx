## Florida Seafood, Family Style

Pinchers brings the Florida fish shack experience to multiple Naples locations. It's casual, fun, and genuinely family-friendly—kids can get messy with crab legs while parents enjoy cold beer and fresh catches.

## Stone Crab Central

Pinchers operates its own stone crab boats, ensuring top-quality claws at fair prices. During season (October-May), this is one of the best values for stone crab in Naples.

## The Menu

Classic Florida seafood in a casual setting:

- **Stone Crab Claws:** The star attraction
- **King Crab Legs:** For bigger appetites
- **Grouper Sandwich:** Fresh, fried or grilled
- **Fish Tacos:** A crowd-pleaser
- **Coconut Shrimp:** Sweet, crunchy, delicious

## Family-Friendly

Kids love Pinchers—the atmosphere is relaxed, the menu has familiar options, and nobody cares if your toddler makes a mess. Crayons and activity sheets available.

## Locations

- **5th Avenue South:** Downtown convenient
- **Tin City:** Waterfront location
- **Coconut Point:** Mall-adjacent

## Tips

- **No reservations:** First come, first served
- **Early bird specials:** Check for deals
- **Takeout works well:** Great for vacation rentals
- **Stone crab pricing:** Market-rate, varies daily
