## Naples' Best Wings

When locals want wings, they go to Pelican Larry's. Multiple locations, consistent quality, and a true sports bar atmosphere make it Naples' go-to for game day.

- **Classic Buffalo:** Hot, medium, or mild
- **Garlic Parmesan:** Crowd favorite
- **Caribbean Jerk:** Spicy and sweet
- **Boneless options:** For neater eating
