## Authentic Italian on 5th Avenue

Alberto's brings authentic Italian flavors to downtown Naples with a focus on quality ingredients and traditional preparations. The warm, welcoming atmosphere makes it a favorite for locals and visitors alike.

## The Menu

Classic Italian with consistent execution:
- **Veal Saltimbocca:** Tender veal with sage and prosciutto
- **Lobster Tail:** Butter-poached decadence
- **Fresh Pasta:** Made in-house daily
- **Seafood Risotto:** Creamy and generous

## The Setting

The dining room balances elegance with warmth. The outdoor patio on 5th Avenue is prime real estate for people-watching during the season.

## Perfect For

- **Date nights:** Romantic without being stuffy
- **Family celebrations:** Welcoming to all ages
- **Pre-theater:** Convenient downtown location
