## Dinner and a Show

South Street combines casual American dining with live entertainment—specifically, dueling pianos that turn dinner into a singalong party. It's Naples-area nightlife at its most fun.

## The Entertainment

The dueling piano shows are the draw—two pianists taking requests, encouraging audience participation, and creating an energetic, interactive experience.

## The Food

Casual American with crowd-pleasers:
- **Pizzas:** Various signature options
- **Burgers:** Solid and satisfying
- **Shareables:** Great for groups
- **Full bar:** Cocktails and beer

## Great For

- **Groups:** The more the merrier
- **Celebrations:** Birthdays, bachelorettes
- **Fun seekers:** Active, participatory evening
- **Singing along:** No judgment zone

## Tips

- **Showtime varies:** Call ahead for schedule
- **Get there early:** Good seats go fast
- **Tip the pianists:** It's expected and appreciated
- **Be ready to participate:** It's interactive!
