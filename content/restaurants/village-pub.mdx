## Casual Waterfront Charm

The Village Pub offers the rare combination of bay views and family-friendly casual dining. Located in the upscale Village at Venetian Bay, it's a refreshingly accessible option among the boutiques and galleries.

## The Menu

Comforting American classics:
- **Burgers:** Several creative options plus the classic
- **Fish & Chips:** Crispy, generous portions
- **Salads:** Fresh and satisfying
- **Appetizers:** Great for sharing

## Bay Views Without Bay Prices

While The Village at Venetian Bay is upscale, The Village Pub is reasonably priced. You can enjoy the view without the fine-dining price tag.

## Great for Families

Kids are welcome, the menu has familiar options, and the casual atmosphere means you don't need to worry about noise levels.

## Location

Perfect for combining shopping at The Village with a waterfront meal. The outdoor seating puts you right on the bay.
