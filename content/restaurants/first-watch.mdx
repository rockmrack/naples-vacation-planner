## Fresh Start to Your Day

First Watch has built a devoted following for its commitment to fresh, quality ingredients and creative breakfast dishes. While it's a chain, the food quality and healthy options make it a solid choice for morning meals.

## What Sets It Apart

- **Fresh-made juices:** Pressed on-site daily
- **Quality ingredients:** No heat lamps, made-to-order
- **Healthy options:** Plenty of wholesome choices
- **No dinner:** Dedicated breakfast/brunch focus

## Menu Highlights

### Healthy Options
- **Avocado Toast:** With poached eggs, everything spice
- **Power Bowls:** Quinoa, greens, ancient grains
- **Superfood Salads:** Substantial and satisfying

### Indulgent Choices
- **Million Dollar Bacon:** Candied, thick-cut, addictive
- **Chickichanga:** Breakfast burrito, good size
- **Pancakes & Waffles:** Classic comfort
