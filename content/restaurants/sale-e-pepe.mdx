## Marco Island's Italian Gem

Sale e Pepe (Salt and Pepper) has been Marco Island's go-to Italian restaurant for years. The waterfront location, elegant atmosphere, and consistent quality make it the island's premier dining destination.

## Waterfront Dining

Request outdoor seating for stunning views of the Marco waterway. Sunset here is particularly beautiful, with the sky reflecting off the water.

## The Menu

Fresh Italian with Gulf influences:
- **Fresh Seafood Pasta:** Daily specials with local catch
- **Branzino:** Mediterranean sea bass, simply grilled
- **Veal Piccata:** Tender with lemon-caper sauce
- **Risotto:** Rotating seasonal preparations

## Perfect For

- **Marco Island visitors:** The island's best upscale option
- **Romantic dinners:** Waterfront ambiance
- **Special occasions:** Celebrations with a view

## Tips

- **Reservations essential:** Especially for outdoor waterfront tables
- **Sunset timing:** Book for 30 minutes before sunset
- **Dress:** Resort casual to dressy
