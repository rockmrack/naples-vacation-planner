## Bay Views, No Pretense

Tavern on the Bay delivers what everyone wants—beautiful water views without the fine-dining formality or price tag. It's casual, welcoming, and the views are free.

## The Setting

Located on Bayfront, the outdoor seating puts you right on the water with views of Naples Bay and the downtown skyline. Sunset here is a treat.

## The Menu

Casual American with seafood slant:
- **Fresh Fish:** Grilled, blackened, or fried
- **Burgers:** Classic and creative
- **Tacos:** Fish and shrimp options
- **Salads:** Fresh and generous

## Drinks

The bar excels at tropical cocktails—mojitos, margaritas, and rum drinks that match the waterfront vibe.

## Great For

- **Families:** Kids welcome, casual dress
- **Happy hour:** Great sunset specials
- **Tourists:** Easy to find, reliable quality
- **Boat watching:** Pass the time watching vessels come and go

## Location

Right on Bayfront with easy parking and a walkable location from downtown hotels.
