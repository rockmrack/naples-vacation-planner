## The Concept

The front of the house features a gleaming fish case filled with the day's arrivals: grouper, snapper, mahi-mahi, stone crab, shrimp, and more. Point to what looks good, and the kitchen will prepare it grilled, blackened, fried, or any way you like.

## Restaurant Highlights

- **Catch of the Day:** Whatever's freshest, cooked to order
- **Stone Crab Claws:** In season, competitive prices
- **Fish Tacos:** Fresh, generous, delicious
- **New England Clam Chowder:** Award-winning recipe

## The Market

Beyond the restaurant, the market sells fresh fish, prepared salads, sauces, and everything you need for a seafood dinner at your vacation rental.

## Why It Works

- **Transparency:** You see exactly what you're getting
- **Freshness:** Daily deliveries, never frozen
- **Value:** Restaurant-quality at market prices
- **Flexibility:** Eat in or take out

- **Lunch is quieter:** Dinner can get busy
- **Seasonal specials:** Ask about what just came in
- **Takeout popular:** Great for beach picnics
