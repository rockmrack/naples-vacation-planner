## Island Vibes on 5th Avenue

Yabba Island Grill brings tropical flavors and Caribbean energy to downtown Naples. It's a welcome departure from Italian and steakhouse standards, offering bold flavors and a festive atmosphere.

## The Cuisine

Caribbean-inspired with fresh Gulf seafood:
- **Jamaican Jerk Chicken:** Authentic spice, perfect heat
- **Coconut Shrimp:** Crispy, sweet, addictive
- **Island Fish Tacos:** Fresh catch with tropical slaw
- **Caribbean Lobster:** When available, spectacular
