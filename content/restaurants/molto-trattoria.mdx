## Family Recipes, Warm Welcome

Molto Trattoria is family-owned and operated, bringing the warmth of an Italian nonna's kitchen to Naples. The recipes are traditional, the portions generous, and the welcome genuine.

## What to Order

The **Gnocchi alla Sorrentina** is the standoutâ€”pillowy potato gnocchi with tomato, mozzarella, and fresh basil. It's the dish that keeps regulars returning.

Other favorites:
- **Chicken Parmigiana:** Golden, cheesy, comforting
- **Linguine alle Vongole:** Fresh clams, white wine, garlic
- **Veal Marsala:** Tender with mushrooms

## The Experience

Unpretentious and genuinely welcoming, Molto feels like dining at a friend's Italian home. The prices are reasonable, especially for the quality and portion sizes.

## Tips

- **No reservations:** First come, first served
- **Popular:** Can get busy on weekends
- **Closed Mondays:** Plan accordingly
- **BYOB:** Check their policy on wine
