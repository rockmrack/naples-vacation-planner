## Why Choose Naples Park?

**Naples Park** is the budget-friendly secret—an older, unpretentious neighborhood just minutes from Vanderbilt Beach and Mercato, offering significant savings on vacation rentals.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Beach proximity** – 10 min to Vanderbilt Beach
- **Affordable** – Much less than beachfront areas
- **Local character** – Established neighborhood
- **Near Mercato** – Modern shopping/dining close by

---

## The Vibe

Think **old Florida neighborhood near the beach**.

- Older, established area
- Mix of retirees and families
- Unpretentious, authentic
- Local restaurants and shops

**Who stays here:** Budget families, vacation rental seekers, those who prioritize beach over glitz.

---

## Staying in Naples Park

### Vacation Rentals ($-$$)
- Single-family homes
- Many with pools
- Often half the price of beachfront

### Limited Hotels
Few hotels; vacation rentals are the norm.
