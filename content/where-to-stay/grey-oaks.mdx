## Why Choose Grey Oaks?

**Grey Oaks** is Naples' most prestigious private golf community—featuring 54 holes of championship golf, luxury estates, and an exclusive country club atmosphere.

Here's what makes it special:
- **54 holes of golf** – Three championship courses
- **Private club atmosphere** – Exclusive membership
- **Luxury estates** – Multi-million dollar homes
- **Central location** – 15 min to downtown

---

## The Vibe

Think **country club living at its finest**.

- Ultra-exclusive atmosphere
- Golf-centric lifestyle
- Gated, secure community
- Impeccably maintained

---

## Staying in Grey Oaks

Vacation rentals only:

### Vacation Rentals ($$$$+)
- Luxury estate homes
- Some with golf course views
- Pool homes standard
- Must arrange club access separately

---

## Golf at Grey Oaks

### Three Courses
- Pine, Palm, and Cypress nines (member play)
- Guests may access with member sponsorship
- World-class facilities
