## Why Choose Royal Harbor?

**Royal Harbor** is Naples' premier boating community—offering deep-water canal access to the Gulf of Mexico and a short drive to Old Naples dining.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Deep-water access** – Large boats, easy Gulf access
- **Gordon Pass** – Direct route to Gulf
- **Established community** – Well-maintained, prestigious
- **Close to downtown** – 10 min to Old Naples

---

## The Vibe

Think **serious boating lifestyle with Naples luxury**.

- Boating-centric community
- Large waterfront homes
- Established, quiet neighborhood
- Mix of full-time and seasonal residents
