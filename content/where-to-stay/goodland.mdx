## Why Choose Goodland?

**Goodland** is Marco Island's hidden gem—an authentic fishing village that feels like stepping back in time, famous for Stan's Idle Hour and fresh-off-the-boat seafood.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Stan's Idle Hour** – Legendary waterfront bar with Sunday mullet toss
- **Authentic atmosphere** – Real fishing village, no pretense
- **Fresh seafood** – Right off local boats
- **Local character** – Quirky, welcoming community

---

## The Vibe

Think **Old Florida time capsule**.

- Funky, authentic
- Fishing village culture
- Live music on weekends
- Local characters everywhere

**Who loves it here:** Fishers, live music lovers, those seeking genuinely authentic experiences.
