## Why Choose The Moorings?

**The Moorings** is an upscale residential community sandwiched between Old Naples and Park Shore—offering waterfront living, beach access, and a quieter alternative to busier areas.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Private beach access** – Moorings Beach, less crowded
- **Waterfront canals** – Many homes with boat docks
- **Central location** – Between downtown and Pelican Bay
- **Upscale residential** – Quiet, established neighborhood

---

## The Vibe

Think **upscale residential neighborhood with beach perks**.

- Quiet, established community
- Mix of waterfront homes and condos
- Less touristy feel
- True Naples living experience

**Who loves it here:** Vacation rental seekers, boaters, those wanting residential peace.

## Staying in The Moorings

No hotels in The Moorings—it's residential. Options include:
