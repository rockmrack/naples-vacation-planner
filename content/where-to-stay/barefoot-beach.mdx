## Why Choose Barefoot Beach?

**Barefoot Beach** is one of Florida's last undeveloped barrier islands—a pristine natural preserve with stunning beaches and limited visitor access.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Pristine, natural beach** – No development on the beach
- **Gopher tortoises** – Endangered species habitat
- **Excellent shelling** – Some of the best in the area
- **Limited access** – Never crowded

---

## The Vibe

Think **natural Florida paradise**.

- Preserved, pristine environment
- Limited commercial development
- Wildlife-focused experience
- Quiet, peaceful atmosphere

**Who loves it here:** Nature lovers, eco-tourists, shelling enthusiasts.

---

## Barefoot Beach Preserve

The main attraction—a county preserve featuring:
- **1.5 miles of pristine beach**
- **Gopher tortoise habitat**
- **Mangrove nature trails**
- **Kayak launch**
- Limited daily parking (arrive early!)
