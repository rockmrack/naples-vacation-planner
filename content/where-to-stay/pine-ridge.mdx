## Why Choose Pine Ridge?

**Pine Ridge** is Naples' premier inland residential area—featuring large estate homes, golf course communities, and peaceful living away from the tourist crowds.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Estate-style homes** – Large properties with space
- **Golf communities** – Multiple courses nearby
- **Privacy** – Away from tourist areas
- **Good value** – More space for the money than beachfront

---

## The Vibe

Think **golf community estates with Naples access**.

- Quiet, residential atmosphere
- Large homes, big lots
- Golf-oriented lifestyle
- Family-friendly

**Who lives here:** Golfers, families, those wanting space.
