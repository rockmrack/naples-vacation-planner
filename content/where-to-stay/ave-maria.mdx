## Why Choose Ave Maria?

**Ave Maria** is one of America's newest towns—a unique planned community built around Ave Maria University, featuring affordable housing, a public golf course, and distinctive architecture.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Unique architecture** – The Oratory is a striking landmark
- **Ave Maria University** – Catholic higher education campus
- **Very affordable** – Lowest housing costs in Collier County
- **Public golf course** – Well-regarded, affordable play

---

## The Vibe

Think **small-town America, new construction**.

- Clean, modern development
- Strong faith-based community
- Family-oriented
- Very affordable

**Who lives here:** Families, retirees, university community.

---

## Getting There

Ave Maria is in eastern Collier County:
- Naples downtown: 35-40 min
- Naples beaches: 45-50 min
- I-75: 20 min
