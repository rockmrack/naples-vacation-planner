## Why Choose Naples Bay/Bayfront?

**Naples Bay** offers the unique combination of waterfront living with walking distance to downtown—featuring marinas, waterfront dining, and the popular Naples Bay Resort.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Waterfront atmosphere** – Bay views, marinas, sunsets
- **Walk to downtown** – Steps to 5th Avenue
- **Naples Bay Resort** – Only major resort in area
- **Boating access** – Marina facilities

---

## The Vibe

Think **waterfront meets walkable downtown**.

- Marina and boating culture
- Waterfront restaurants
- Active, social atmosphere
- Combination of tourists and locals

**Who loves it here:** Boaters, waterfront lovers, downtown enthusiasts.
