## Why Choose Park Shore?

**Park Shore** offers the best of both worlds—positioned between the beach and the bay, with **Venetian Village** as your backyard shopping and dining destination.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Venetian Village access** – waterfront shopping and dining
- **Clam Pass Beach** – scenic boardwalk beach access
- **Central location** – between Old Naples and North Naples
- **Upscale but relaxed** – luxury without the downtown crowds

If you want proximity to great shopping AND the beach without choosing one over the other, Park Shore delivers.

---

## The Vibe

Think **affluent Naples living meets waterfront elegance**.

- High-rise condos with Gulf views
- Sailboats moored in the bay
- Upscale shoppers strolling Venetian Village
- Less touristy than downtown—more residential feel
- Sunsets from both beach AND bay

**Who loves it here:** Couples who appreciate shopping, travelers wanting peace without isolation, return visitors who know the area.

---

## Location & Getting Around

Park Shore is positioned between Old Naples (10 min south) and Vanderbilt Beach (10 min north).

### Walkability: ⭐⭐⭐

- Walk to Venetian Village ✅
- Walk to Clam Pass Beach (via boardwalk) ✅
- Walk to 5th Avenue ❌ (10-15 min drive)

### Key Landmarks

**Venetian Village** – Your waterfront hub:
- 40+ shops and boutiques
- 10+ restaurants on the bay
- Art galleries
- Watch paddle boarders and dolphins

**Clam Pass Beach** – Via scenic boardwalk:
- 3/4 mile boardwalk through mangroves
- Less crowded, more natural
- Free tram available
- Concessions on the beach

## What's Nearby

### Venetian Village

The heart of Park Shore:

**Dining:**
- **M Waterfront Grille** – upscale American, stunning views
- **Bayside Seafood Grill & Bar** – waterfront casual
- **The Village Pub** – casual gastropub

**Shopping:**
- Upscale boutiques
- Art galleries
- Home décor stores

### Beaches

**Clam Pass Beach:**
- Scenic boardwalk access
- Less crowded than Naples Pier area
- Natural mangrove setting

**Lowdermilk Park** (5 min drive):
- More traditional beach experience
- Playground, facilities

### Other Nearby

**Waterside Shops** (5 min):
- Nordstrom, Saks Fifth Avenue
- Upscale dining options

## Pros & Cons

### ✅ Pros
- Venetian Village at your doorstep
- Clam Pass Beach walkable
- More peaceful than downtown
- Upscale shopping and dining
- Beautiful bay views

### ⚠️ Cons
- Still need car for Old Naples/5th Avenue
- Fewer hotel options than other areas
- More residential—less "vacation" atmosphere
- Beach requires boardwalk walk or tram
