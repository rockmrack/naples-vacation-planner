## Why Choose Coquina Sands?

**Coquina Sands** is Naples' hidden gem—a beachfront residential neighborhood with direct access to beloved Lowdermilk Park Beach and proximity to downtown.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Lowdermilk Park** – Perfect beach with facilities
- **Beachfront location** – Walk to the sand
- **Close to downtown** – 10 min to 5th Avenue
- **Residential quiet** – Not touristy

---

## The Vibe

Think **family beach neighborhood meets Old Naples access**.

- Beachfront but residential
- Family-oriented atmosphere
- Less crowded beach area
- Good balance of access and peace

**Who loves it here:** Families, beach lovers, vacation rental seekers.

---

## Lowdermilk Park Beach

One of Naples' best public beaches:
- **Facilities** – Restrooms, showers, pavilions
- **Playground** – Great for kids
- **Volleyball courts**
- **Concession stand**
- **Less crowded** than Naples Pier beach

---

## Staying in Coquina Sands

Mostly vacation rentals:

### Vacation Rentals ($$$-$$$$)
- Beachfront condos
- Single-family homes
- Weekly rentals common

### Limited Hotels
- Few hotel options; mostly rental properties
