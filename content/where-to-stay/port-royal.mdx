## Why Choose Port Royal?

**Port Royal** is Naples' most exclusive address—where multi-million dollar waterfront estates line deep-water canals with direct Gulf access. This isn't a typical tourist destination, but for those with the means, it offers unparalleled luxury.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Ultra-exclusive** – Most expensive zip code in Florida
- **Waterfront living** – Deep-water yacht access
- **Privacy** – Gated, secure, quiet
- **Location** – Minutes from Old Naples

---

## The Vibe

Think **billionaire's retreat on the Gulf**.

- Extremely private and exclusive
- No commercial development
- Estate-style living only
- Yachts in every backyard

**Who lives here:** CEOs, celebrities, ultra-high-net-worth individuals.

---

## Staying in Port Royal

There are no hotels in Port Royal—it's entirely residential. However, some estate owners offer vacation rentals:

### Vacation Rentals ($$$$$++)
- Expect $10,000-50,000+ per week
- Deep-water dock access
- Private pool and grounds
- Full staffing available
