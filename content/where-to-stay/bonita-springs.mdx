## Why Choose Bonita Springs?

**Bonita Springs** offers the sweet spot between Naples luxury and Fort Myers accessibility—with some of the area's most beautiful natural beaches and family-friendly resorts.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Barefoot Beach Preserve** – One of Florida's last undeveloped barrier islands
- **Lovers Key State Park** – Natural beach paradise
- **Hyatt Coconut Point** – Major family resort with lazy river
- **More affordable** – Better value than Naples proper

---

## The Vibe

Think **natural Florida beauty meets family resort**.

- More relaxed than Naples
- Strong outdoor/nature focus
- Family-oriented atmosphere
- Great golf communities

**Who loves it here:** Families, nature lovers, golfers, budget-conscious beach seekers.

## Location & Getting Around

### Drive Times
- Naples downtown: 20-25 min
- RSW Airport: 25-30 min
- Marco Island: 35-40 min
- Fort Myers: 20 min

### Walkability: ⭐⭐

Car required for most activities.
