## Why Choose East Naples?

**East Naples** is the budget-friendly choice—offering affordable hotels near I-75, easy access to the Everglades, and a practical base for exploring the Naples area.

Here's what makes it practical:
- **Best hotel values** – Significant savings vs. beach areas
- **Everglades access** – Perfect for nature day trips
- **I-75 convenience** – Easy highway access
- **Lely Resort golf** – Championship courses nearby

---

## The Vibe

Think **practical, affordable, convenient**.

- More suburban/commercial
- Budget hotels along major roads
- Less touristy, more local
- Gateway to Everglades

**Who stays here:** Budget travelers, road trippers, Everglades visitors.
