## Why Choose Fiddler's Creek?

**Fiddler's Creek** is a master-planned resort community between Naples and Marco Island—offering golf, pools, and vacation rental condos with resort-style amenities.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Resort amenities** – Pools, fitness, spa
- **Golf course** – Public/semi-private play available
- **Good value** – More affordable than beach areas
- **Community feel** – Well-maintained, active HOA
