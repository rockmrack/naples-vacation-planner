## Why Choose Aqualane Shores?

**Aqualane Shores** is one of Naples' most prestigious waterfront neighborhoods—offering deep-water canal access, luxury estates, and walkable proximity to 3rd Street South.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Deep-water access** – Direct Gulf access via Gordon Pass
- **Walk to 3rd Street** – Close to Old Naples dining
- **Prestigious address** – Among Naples' finest neighborhoods
- **Yacht-friendly** – Large boats welcome

---

## The Vibe

Think **quiet luxury with boating lifestyle**.

- Established, prestigious community
- Waterfront living on canals
- Walking distance to Old Naples
- Very exclusive atmosphere

---

## Staying in Aqualane Shores

No hotels—vacation rentals only:

### Vacation Rentals ($$$$+)
- Waterfront estates with docks
- Luxury condos
- Often $5,000-20,000+ per week

## What's Nearby

- **3rd Street South** – Walking distance
- **Naples Pier** – 15 min walk
- **Gordon Pass** – Boat access to Gulf
- **Port Royal** – Adjacent neighborhood
