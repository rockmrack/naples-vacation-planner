## Why Choose Marco Island?

**Marco Island** is a barrier island paradise just 20 minutes south of Naples—offering wider beaches, less crowds, and a true island escape atmosphere.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Pristine beaches** – Wider, less crowded than Naples beaches
- **World-class shelling** – Some of the best in Florida
- **Resort atmosphere** – Major hotels on the beach
- **Wildlife access** – Close to Everglades and 10,000 Islands

---

## The Vibe

Think **relaxed island living meets Gulf Coast luxury**.

- Slower pace than Naples
- Resort-centric experience
- More family-friendly beach atmosphere
- Excellent for outdoor water activities

**Who loves it here:** Families, beach lovers, kayakers, nature enthusiasts.

---

## Location & Getting Around

### Drive Times
- Naples downtown: 20-25 min
- Naples Airport (APF): 25 min
- RSW Airport: 50 min
- Everglades National Park: 45 min

### Walkability: ⭐⭐

Marco Island requires a car for most activities. Resorts are self-contained, but exploring the island and mainland requires driving.

## Best Hotels on Marco Island

### Luxury ($$$$)
- **JW Marriott Marco Island** – Premier beachfront resort
- **Hilton Marco Island** – Full-service with spa

### Mid-Range ($$$)
- **Marco Beach Ocean Suites** – Condo-style luxury
- **Marriott's Crystal Shores** – Vacation club quality

### Value ($$)
- **Marco Island Lakeside Inn** – Best budget option
- **Olde Marco Island Inn** – Charming, affordable

## What's Nearby

### Beaches
- **South Marco Beach** – Wide, pristine, resort access
- **Tigertail Beach** – Natural lagoon, shelling
- **Residents' Beach** – Less touristy

### Activities
- **Dolphin and manatee tours**
- **Kayaking 10,000 Islands**
- **Deep sea fishing**
- **Everglades day trips**
