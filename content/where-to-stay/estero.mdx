## Why Choose Estero?

**Estero** is the practical choice for value-focused travelers—home to Miromar Outlets, Hertz Arena, and numerous mid-range hotels near I-75.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Miromar Outlets** – 140+ stores, great deals
- **Hertz Arena** – Florida Everblades hockey, concerts
- **Value pricing** – Best hotel deals in the area
- **Central location** – Between Naples and Fort Myers

---

## The Vibe

Think **convenient, affordable, shopping-focused**.

- More suburban than resort
- Shopping and entertainment oriented
- Easy highway access
- Budget-friendly options

**Who loves it here:** Shoppers, budget travelers, event attendees.
