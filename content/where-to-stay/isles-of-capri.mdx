## Why Choose Isles of Capri?

**Isles of Capri** is a small, authentic island community between Naples and Marco Island—offering fishing village charm and access to the pristine Ten Thousand Islands.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Authentic island feel** – Small, close-knit community
- **Fishing paradise** – Excellent backwater fishing
- **Ten Thousand Islands access** – Kayaking and eco-tours
- **Local restaurants** – Capri Fish House, waterfront dining

---

## The Vibe

Think **old Florida fishing village**.

- Small island atmosphere
- Fishing and boating focused
- Local, unpretentious
- Quiet, peaceful

**Who loves it here:** Fishers, kayakers, those seeking authentic local experience.
