## Why Choose Pelican Bay?

**Pelican Bay** is Naples' premier master-planned community—a **gated oasis** offering resort-style living with private beach access and manicured grounds.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Private beach access** – tram service to uncrowded Gulf beaches
- **Gated and peaceful** – residential feel, away from tourists
- **Nature preserve** – 88-acre preserve with walking trails
- **Artis—Naples access** – world-class performing arts center

If you value privacy, quiet, and a "living like a local" experience over downtown energy, Pelican Bay delivers.

---

## The Vibe

Think **country club meets nature preserve**.

- Manicured landscaping and quiet streets
- Tram to private beach areas
- Residents walking dogs and biking
- Art lovers heading to performances
- A distinctly non-touristy atmosphere

**Who loves it here:** Return visitors, retirees, extended-stay travelers, anyone seeking peace over activity.

---

## Location & Getting Around

Pelican Bay is in North Naples, positioned between Park Shore and Vanderbilt Beach.

### Walkability: ⭐⭐

The community is spread out; you'll want a car for most needs.

**Within Pelican Bay:**
- Beach tram (residents/guests)
- Walking and biking paths
- Nature preserve trails

**Nearby by car:**
- Waterside Shops (5 min)
- Artis—Naples (adjacent)
- Mercato (10 min)
- Old Naples (15-20 min)

## Pelican Bay Amenities

### The Beach

Two private beach facilities:
- **North Beach** – casual, tiki bar
- **Sandpiper** – restaurant, bar, more formal

Both accessible via:
- Tram from community parking
- Boardwalk walk through preserve

The beaches are significantly less crowded than public beaches.

### Nature Preserve

88 acres of protected habitat:
- Walking and biking trails
- Bird watching opportunities
- Mangrove boardwalks
- Connects to Clam Pass

### Artis—Naples

World-class performing arts center:
- Naples Philharmonic
- Broadway shows
- Jazz and classical performances
- Baker Museum (visual arts)

## Pros & Cons

### ✅ Pros
- Private, uncrowded beach access
- Peaceful, gated environment
- Beautiful nature preserve
- Less tourist traffic
- Excellent for extended stays

### ⚠️ Cons
- Need car for almost everything
- Limited on-site dining
- Less "vacation energy"
- Can feel isolated
- Not ideal for first-time visitors
