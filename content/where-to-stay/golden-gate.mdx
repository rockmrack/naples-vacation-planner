## Why Choose Golden Gate?

**Golden Gate** is Naples' suburban community—offering the most affordable accommodation options and a genuine local Florida experience away from tourist crowds.

Here's what makes it practical:
- **Most affordable** – Lowest rates in Naples area
- **Local experience** – Non-touristy, authentic
- **Space** – Larger vacation rentals available
- **Quiet** – Residential, peaceful

---

## The Vibe

Think **genuine Florida suburban living**.

- Residential community
- Mix of families and retirees
- Local restaurants and shops
- Far from tourist crowds

---

## Staying in Golden Gate

### Vacation Rentals ($-$$)
- Single-family homes
- Often with pools
- Best value in Naples area

### Limited Hotels
- Few options; mostly rental properties
