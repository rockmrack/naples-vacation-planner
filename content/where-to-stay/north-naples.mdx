## Why Choose North Naples?

**North Naples** is where modern Naples meets upscale resort living. Home to Mercato, Waterside Shops, and major beachfront resorts, this area appeals to those who want contemporary amenities.

Here's what makes it special:
- **Mercato** – Open-air shopping, dining, movies, concerts
- **Major resorts** – Ritz-Carlton, Naples Grande, LaPlaya
- **Newer developments** – Modern architecture and amenities
- **Easy beach access** – Clam Pass, Vanderbilt Beach

---

## The Vibe

Think **modern luxury meets Gulf Coast relaxation**.

- Contemporary rather than historic
- Resort-centric experience
- Great shopping and dining
- Family-friendly with activities

**Who loves it here:** Shoppers, resort seekers, modern dining enthusiasts.
