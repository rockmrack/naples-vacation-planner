**The Capri Inn** (formerly Trianon Old Naples) has been beautifully updated with Mediterranean-style architecture and breezy coastal-chic interiors that capture the timelessness of Old Naples.

## Key Amenities

- **Spacious one-bedroom suites** with living areas
- **Two-bedroom cottage** for families/groups
- Coastal-chic, breezy decor
- Intimate courtyards
- **Free electric vehicle shuttle** to Naples beach
- Short drive to 5th Avenue

---

## Best For

- **Couples** seeking romantic, intimate setting
- **Groups** (cottage option)
- **Those who appreciate boutique** over chain hotels
- **Repeat visitors** wanting something special

## Skip If

- You need resort amenities
- Prefer modern, contemporary style
- Want direct beach access
