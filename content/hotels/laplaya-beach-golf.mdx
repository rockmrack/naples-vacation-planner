## Intimate Beachfront Elegance

**LaPlaya Beach & Golf Resort** offers a more intimate alternative to the larger beachfront resorts. Reopened in 2024 after thoughtful renovations, LaPlaya delivers boutique luxury directly on the Gulf.

## Key Amenities

### Beach Experience
- Private Gulf beach
- **Beach butler service** (chairs, umbrellas, beverages)
- Watersports available
- Sunset viewing deck

### Pool & Spa
- Heated beachside pool
- Full-service spa
- Fitness center

### Golf
Access to **LaPlaya Golf Club** with stay-and-play packages available.

### Renovations
Both the Gulf Tower and Beach House were renovated and reopened in early 2024 with fresh, coastal-elegant design.
