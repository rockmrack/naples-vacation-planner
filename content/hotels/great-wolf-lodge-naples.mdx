## Florida's First Indoor Water Park Resort

**Great Wolf Lodge Naples** opened in September 2024 as Florida's first indoor water park—a game-changer for families visiting the Naples area, especially during rainy days or hot summer afternoons.

## Water Park Features

### Indoor Water Park
- **12 water slides** of varying thrill levels
- **3 pools** including wave pool (Slap Tail Pond)
- **Multilevel splash zone** with interactive elements
- **Toddler splash pad** with small slides
- **Large tipping bucket** – a crowd favorite

### Outdoor
- Zero-entry outdoor pool
- Lily pad course

### All-Weather Fun
The indoor water park means no weather cancellations—perfect for summer afternoon storms or cooler winter days.

## Beyond the Water Park

- **MagiQuest** – interactive wand adventure game
- Arcade
- Build-A-Bear Workshop
- Multiple dining options
- Themed suites (wolf den, cabin, etc.)
