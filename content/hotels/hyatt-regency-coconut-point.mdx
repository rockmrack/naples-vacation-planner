**Hyatt Regency Coconut Point Resort & Spa** is a sprawling family paradise between Fort Myers Beach and Naples, offering everything from a massive lazy river to private island beach access.

## Key Amenities

### Water Complex
- **1,000-foot lazy river** – the star attraction
- 5 swimming pools total
- Water slides
- Steam room and sauna

### Private Island
Take the resort boat to a private island beach—unique and memorable experience for families.

### Golf
18-hole championship golf course on property.

### Kids' Club
Supervised children's activities give parents time to enjoy the spa.

### Dining
Three on-site restaurants plus pool bar and in-room dining.

---

## Best For

- **Families with kids** of all ages
- **Multi-generational groups**
- **Golfers** who want family amenities
- **Those wanting variety** without leaving resort

## Skip If

- You want beachfront (island requires boat)
- Prefer intimate boutique experience
- Naples downtown is priority
