## The Heart of Downtown Naples

## Key Amenities

### Location
- **Directly on 5th Avenue South** – Naples' premier street
- Walk to 50+ restaurants
- Walk to Naples Pier (10-15 min)
- Walk to 3rd Street galleries

### Rooftop Experience
- Heated rooftop pool
- Rooftop bar with sunset views
- Perfect for evening cocktails

### Club Level
Upgraded guests enjoy:
- Private concierge
- Enhanced amenities
- Oversized suites

### Contemporary Art
The hotel features a curated collection of contemporary art throughout public spaces and rooms.

---

## Pet Policy

Inn on Fifth welcomes well-mannered dogs and cats:
- No weight limits or breed restrictions
- $150 non-refundable pet fee
- Pets allowed in guest rooms and main lobby
- Not permitted in restaurants or pool area

---

## Best For

- **Couples** who want walkable dining and nightlife
- **First-time visitors** wanting the quintessential Naples experience
- **Foodies** with endless restaurant options nearby
- **Pet owners** traveling with dogs or cats

## Skip If

- You want beachfront accommodation
- Prefer resort-style pools and activities
- Traveling with children who need kids' programs
