## Southwest Florida's Premier Boat Show

The Naples Boat Show brings together boat dealers, marine products, and boating enthusiasts for four days of maritime excellence. Whether you're in the market for a new boat or just dreaming, this is the event for you.

## Event Details

- **When:** January 22-25, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am - 5pm daily
- **Where:** Naples City Dock and surrounding areas
- **Admission:** FREE

## What You'll See

### Boats on Display
- **Fishing boats:** From flats boats to offshore sportfishers
- **Cruising yachts:** Power and sail
- **Pontoons and deck boats:** Family-friendly options
- **Personal watercraft:** Jet skis and more
- **Kayaks and paddleboards:** Human-powered craft

### Marine Products & Services
- Marine electronics and accessories
- Fishing tackle and gear
- Boat accessories and parts
- Marine services and dealers

## Special Features

- **Sea trials:** Test ride select boats
- **Seminars:** Boating education sessions
- **Demonstrations:** Product demos and how-tos

## Tips

- **Comfortable shoes:** Lots of walking on docks
- **Sunscreen:** Southwest Florida sun is strong
- **Bring questions:** Dealers are ready to talk
- **Consider a trade-in:** Many dealers offer evaluations
