## Music & Dancing on Sarasota Bay

Ringling by the Bay transforms the Bolger Campiello at The Ringling Museum into an outdoor dance floor with live music and spectacular bay views. It's one of Sarasota's most unique evening experiences.

## The Setting

The Bolger Campiello, a beautiful outdoor courtyard overlooking Sarasota Bay, provides the perfect setting for live music. With the bay as backdrop and the museum grounds surrounding you, it's magical.

## What to Expect

- Live music (jazz, swing, Latin, pop)
- Dancing encouraged
- Cash bar available
- Beautiful sunset timing
- Museum grounds open for exploration

## Plan Your Visit

- **When:** Select evenings January-March
- **Hours:** Typically 5-8pm
- **Tickets:** $10-20 (members may have discounts)
- **Check schedule:** Dates vary

## Tips

- **Arrive early:** Explore the museum grounds
- **Dress smart casual:** Sarasota style
- **Sunset timing:** Many events start at sunset
- **Combine with dinner:** Downtown Sarasota restaurants nearby
