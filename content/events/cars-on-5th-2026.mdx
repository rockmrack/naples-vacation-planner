## Naples' Premier Automotive Event

Cars on 5th transforms downtown's 5th Avenue South into a world-class automobile museum. Over 200 exotic, classic, and vintage vehicles line the street, creating one of Southwest Florida's most anticipated annual events.

## Event Details

- **When:** Saturday, February 7, 2026, 10am - 4pm
- **Where:** 5th Avenue South, downtown Naples
- **Admission:** FREE to spectators
- **Judging:** Concours d'Elegance style competition

## What You'll See

### Vehicle Categories
- **Exotic cars:** Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, Porsche
- **Classic American:** Corvettes, muscle cars, hot rods
- **Vintage:** Pre-war classics, antique automobiles
- **Motorcycles:** Classic and custom bikes
- **Special displays:** Rotating themed collections

### More Than Cars
- Live entertainment throughout the day
- Restaurant patios perfect for viewing
- People-watching at its finest
- Photography opportunities galore
