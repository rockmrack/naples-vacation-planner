## Incredible Art That Washes Away

The Sarasota Chalk Festival brings together world-class street artists who create stunning, temporary masterpieces on pavement. Watch art come to life, then disappear with the next rain.

## What to See

### 3D Chalk Art
International artists create mind-bending 3D illusions and hyper-realistic paintings using only chalk. The skill is remarkable.

### Artists at Work
Watch artists create their pieces over the festival weekend. Many are happy to discuss their techniques.

## The Experience

Burns Square in downtown Sarasota provides the canvas for this unique festival. Stroll among the art, watch artists work, and appreciate the temporary nature of this beautiful medium.

## Tips

- **Free admission:** One of Sarasota's best free events
- **Photography:** Bring your camera
- **Multiple visits:** Art evolves over the weekend
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours for this unique experience
