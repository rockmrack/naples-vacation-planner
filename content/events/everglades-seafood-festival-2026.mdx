## Florida's Largest Free Festival

The Everglades Seafood Festival has been celebrating the bounty of the 10,000 Islands for over 50 years. This beloved tradition draws tens of thousands of visitors to tiny Everglades City each February for fresh seafood straight from the Gulf.

## A Day Trip from Naples

Located just 35 miles from downtown Naples, Everglades City is the gateway to the 10,000 Islands and the western Everglades. The festival is a perfect day trip for visitors staying in Naples.

## What to Eat

### Fresh Seafood
- **Stone crab claws:** The star of the show
- **Gulf shrimp:** Multiple preparations
- **Fresh fish:** Fried, grilled, blackened
- **Gator bites:** A Florida specialty
- **Lobster:** Florida lobster in season

### Festival Favorites
- **Seafood gumbo:** Hearty and flavorful
- **Crab cakes:** Fresh and generous
- **Fried specialties:** Calamari, oysters, scallops

## Beyond Food

- **Live music:** Country, rock, and community bands
- **Arts and crafts:** Local vendors
- **Fishing village culture:** Experience Old Florida
- **Airboat rides:** Adventure nearby

## Tips

- **Arrive early:** Parking fills quickly
- **Bring cash:** Faster for food vendors
- **Combine with Everglades:** Add a swamp tour
- **Sunscreen:** February sun is still strong
