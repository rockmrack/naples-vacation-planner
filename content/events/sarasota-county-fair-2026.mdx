## 10 Days of County Fair Fun

The Sarasota County Fair is a beloved annual tradition bringing together rides, livestock shows, fair food, live entertainment, and family activities for 10 days of classic American fair fun.

## What to Expect

### Rides & Midway
- Carnival rides for all ages
- Games and prizes
- Midway attractions

### Agricultural Exhibits
- Livestock shows
- 4-H competitions
- Agricultural displays
- Farm animals

### Fair Food
- Funnel cakes and corn dogs
- Deep-fried everything
- Local food vendors
- Classic fair treats

### Entertainment
- Live concerts
- Shows and performances
- Special attractions
- Contests and competitions

## Ticket Information

- **General Admission:** $12-15
- **Children/Seniors:** Discounts available
- **Ride wristbands:** Additional cost
- **Parking:** Free

## Tips

- **Weekday visits:** Less crowded
- **Wear comfortable shoes:** Lots of walking
- **Bring cash:** Faster at food vendors
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours north
