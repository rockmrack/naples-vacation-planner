## Holiday Magic on 5th Avenue

Christmas Walk transforms Naples' most famous street into a winter wonderland—well, a Florida version. Strolling carolers, twinkling lights, Santa photos, and festive cheer make this beloved annual tradition a highlight of the holiday season.

## Event Highlights

### Entertainment
- **Strolling carolers:** Holiday songs throughout
- **Santa Claus:** Photo opportunities
- **Holiday characters:** Meet seasonal favorites
- **Live music:** Performances at key locations

### The Avenue
- **Festive decorations:** Lights and displays
- **Extended shopping hours:** Holiday deals
- **Restaurant specials:** Festive menus
- **Hot cocoa and treats:** Get in the spirit

## Perfect for Families

Children love Christmas Walk—the atmosphere is magical, Santa is present, and the whole evening feels special. Add dinner at a 5th Avenue restaurant for a complete holiday experience.

## Tips

- **Dress festively:** Get into the spirit
- **Dinner reservations:** Book ahead
- **Arrive at dusk:** Lights are magical
- **Camera ready:** Photo opportunities abound
