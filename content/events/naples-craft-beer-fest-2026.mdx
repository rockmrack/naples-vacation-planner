## Southwest Florida's Premier Craft Beer Event

The Naples Craft Beer Fest brings together the best of Florida's craft brewing scene—and beyond—for an afternoon of suds, music, and bay views. Over 100 craft beers, ciders, and seltzers await at this popular annual event.

## Event Details

- **When:** Saturday, March 7, 2026, 2pm - 6pm
- **Where:** Bayfront, downtown Naples
- **Tickets:** $50-75 (includes tastings and commemorative glass)
- **21+ Event:** Valid ID required

## What's Included

### Beer Selections
- 100+ craft beers from Florida and national breweries
- Ciders, hard seltzers, and specialty brews
- Limited release and festival-exclusive tastings

### Entertainment
- Live music on two stages
- Food trucks with festival fare
- Lawn games and activities
- VIP area with exclusive tastings

## Ticket Tiers

- **General Admission ($50):** Entry at 3pm, tastings, commemorative glass
- **VIP ($75):** Early entry at 2pm, exclusive VIP area, premium tastings

## Tips

- **Designate a driver:** Rideshare recommended
- **Stay hydrated:** Water stations available
- **Eat something:** Food trucks have great options
- **Get tickets early:** This event sells out
