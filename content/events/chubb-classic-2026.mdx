## Golf Legends in Naples

The Chubb Classic brings PGA Tour Champions action to Naples' premier golf venue. Watch legends of the game compete on Tiburón's challenging Gold Course, designed by Greg Norman.

## The Tournament

### What to Expect
- 78 PGA Tour Champions competitors
- Three-day stroke play format
- Pro-Am events
- Practice rounds open to spectators

### Past Champions
The Chubb Classic has attracted golf's biggest names in the 50+ category, with past champions reading like a Who's Who of golf history.

## The Course

Tiburón Golf Club's Gold Course provides a challenging test for competitors and a beautiful viewing experience for spectators. Norman's design incorporates Florida's natural landscape.

## Ticket Options

- **Daily grounds tickets:** $25-40
- **Weekly badges:** Better value for multiple days
- **VIP packages:** Premium viewing and hospitality

## Tips

- **Arrive early:** Parking fills
- **Comfortable shoes:** Walking the course
- **Sunscreen:** February sun is strong
- **Autographs:** Champions often sign

## Perfect Location

Tiburón is in North Naples, easily accessible and close to Mercato for post-golf dining.
