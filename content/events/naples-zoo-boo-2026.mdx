## Not-Too-Scary Halloween at the Zoo

Boo at the Zoo is Naples' premiere family Halloween event, with trick-or-treating throughout the zoo, costumes, entertainment, and animal encounters.

## The Fun

### Trick-or-Treating
Candy stations throughout the zoo provide treats for costumed kids. It's trick-or-treating without the neighborhood walking!

### Costumes
Kids (and adults!) are encouraged to wear costumes. Look for costume contests and themed fun.

### Entertainment
- DJ and dancing
- Games and activities
- Animal enrichment (watch animals enjoy pumpkins!)
- Photo opportunities
