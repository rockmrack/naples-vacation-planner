## Three Weeks of Musical Excellence

The Sarasota Music Festival is one of America's premier chamber music festivals, bringing together exceptional musicians from around the world for three weeks of performances, education, and musical celebration.

## The Festival

### Performances
- Chamber music concerts
- Orchestra performances
- Solo recitals
- New music premieres

### Artists
The festival attracts internationally acclaimed musicians alongside talented emerging artists, creating a unique blend of established excellence and fresh perspectives.

## Venues

Performances take place at various Sarasota venues, including the historic Sarasota Opera House, with its excellent acoustics and intimate atmosphere.

## Festival Schedule

- **When:** May 31 - June 14, 2026
- **Frequency:** Multiple performances weekly
- **Tickets:** $25-75 depending on performance

## Tips

- **Book early:** Popular concerts sell out
- **Series packages:** Better value for multiple concerts
- **Dress code:** Smart casual to dressy
- **Pre-concert talks:** Often available
