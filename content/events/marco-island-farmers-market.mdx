## Island Fresh Every Wednesday

The Marco Island Farmers Market brings the island community together every Wednesday morning during season. Fresh produce, prepared foods, crafts, and more await at beautiful Veterans Community Park.

## What You'll Find

### Fresh Produce
- Seasonal fruits and vegetables
- Florida citrus
- Fresh herbs

### Prepared Foods
- Breakfast items and pastries
- Ready-to-eat lunches
- Baked goods

### Local Goods
- Handmade crafts
- Jewelry and art
- Plants and flowers

## Season

- **When:** November 12 - April 15
- **Day:** Every Wednesday
- **Hours:** 7:30am - 1pm

- **Arrive early:** Best selection
- **Bring cash:** Some vendors prefer it
- **Breakfast on the island:** Start your Marco day here
