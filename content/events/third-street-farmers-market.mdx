## Naples' Premier Saturday Market

For over 20 years, the Third Street South Farmers Market has been the Saturday morning gathering place for Naples residents and visitors. Rain or shine, this beloved market brings the community together with fresh produce, local specialties, and a festive atmosphere.

## What You'll Find

### Fresh Produce
- **Seasonal fruits:** Florida citrus, strawberries, tropical fruits
- **Vegetables:** Farm-fresh greens, tomatoes, peppers
- **Herbs:** Fresh basil, cilantro, and more

### Artisan Foods
- **Fresh bread:** Artisan loaves baked that morning
- **Cheeses:** Local and imported selections
- **Prepared foods:** Empanadas, crepes, and ready-to-eat treats
- **Honey and jams:** Local Florida honey, preserves

### Handcrafted Goods
- **Flowers:** Beautiful bouquets and plants
- **Arts & crafts:** Local artists' creations
- **Jewelry:** Handmade pieces

## Pro Tips

- **Bring cash:** Some vendors prefer it
- **Bring reusable bags:** Be eco-friendly
- **Breakfast:** Grab a pastry and coffee to enjoy while browsing
- **Parking:** Street parking on 3rd Street or nearby lots
- **Location note:** From May-November, the market moves to the Neapolitan lot behind Tommy Bahama
