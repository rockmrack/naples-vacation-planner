## Celebrating Italian Cinema

The Naples Italian Film Festival brings the best of Italian cinema to Southwest Florida with feature films, documentaries, and short films, many with English subtitles.

## The Festival

### Film Screenings
- Contemporary Italian cinema
- Classic Italian films
- Documentaries
- Short film programs

### Special Events
- Filmmaker Q&As
- Italian cultural events
- Wine and film pairings
- Opening and closing galas

## The Venue

Silverspot Cinema at Mercato provides a luxurious viewing experience with premium seating, excellent food and beverage options, and state-of-the-art projection.

## Tickets

- **Individual screenings:** $15
- **Multi-film passes:** $40-50
- **Gala events:** Additional pricing

## Perfect Pairing

Combine the film festival with dinner at one of Mercato's Italian restaurants for a complete Italian evening in Naples.
