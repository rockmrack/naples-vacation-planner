## Explore Naples' Creative Hub

The Naples Design District Block Bash opens the doors to Naples' thriving art community. Studios and galleries welcome visitors for an evening of creativity, refreshments, and discovery.

## The District

The Naples Art District is home to dozens of artists, designers, and creative businesses. This annual event showcases the neighborhood's vibrant creative energy.

## What to Expect

### Open Studios
- Meet working artists
- See art in progress
- Purchase directly from creators

### Galleries
- Current exhibitions
- Opening receptions
- Artist talks

### Entertainment
- Live music
- Food trucks
- Refreshments

## Getting There

The Naples Art District is located off Airport-Pulling Road, easily accessible from downtown Naples.

## Tips

- **Evening event:** Typically 5-9pm
- **Wear comfortable shoes:** Walking between studios
- **Bring buying capacity:** Unique finds available
- **Meet artists:** They're happy to chat
