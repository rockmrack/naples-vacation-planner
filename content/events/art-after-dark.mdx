## Culture for Free

Art After Dark is one of Naples' best-kept cultural secrets. On the last Wednesday of each month during season, The Baker Museum opens its doors for free, combining world-class art with live music and light refreshments.

## What to Expect

### The Museum
- **Free admission:** Explore all galleries
- **Current exhibitions:** Rotating shows and permanent collection
- **Docent tours:** Guided exhibition walks
- **Interactive elements:** Engage with art

### The Party
- **Live music:** Jazz, classical, contemporary
- **Light bites:** Complimentary refreshments
- **Cash bar:** Wine and cocktails available
- **Mingling:** Meet fellow art lovers

## Why Go

The Baker Museum houses an impressive collection of modern and contemporary art in a stunning architectural setting. Art After Dark lets you experience it in a relaxed, social atmosphere—perfect for date night or cultural exploration.

## Tips

- **Arrive at 5pm:** Beat the crowd, see more art
- **Dress smart casual:** Naples cultural attire
- **Check exhibitions:** Some shows have special events
- **Combine with dinner:** Many Pelican Bay restaurants nearby
