## Four Days of Film Excellence

The Naples International Film Festival (NIFF) is Southwest Florida's premier film event, showcasing the best in independent cinema from around the world. Held annually at Artis—Naples, the festival combines excellent films with engaging conversations with filmmakers.

## Event Schedule

- **Opening Night (Oct 22):** Red carpet premiere, opening night party
- **Festival Days (Oct 23-24):** Film screenings, filmmaker Q&As, panels
- **Closing Night (Oct 25):** Final screenings, awards ceremony, closing party

## Festival Highlights

### Film Categories
- **Narrative Features:** Full-length independent films
- **Documentary Features:** Compelling true stories
- **Short Films:** Curated short programs
- **Student Films:** Emerging filmmaker showcase
- **Florida Focus:** Films with Florida connections

### Beyond Screenings
- Filmmaker Q&A sessions after screenings
- Industry panels and discussions
- Meet-and-greets with directors and actors
- Opening and closing night parties

## Ticket Options

- **Individual Screenings:** $15
- **Day Pass:** $75
- **Full Festival Pass:** $150
- **VIP Pass:** $250 (includes parties and premium seating)

## Tips

- **Book early:** Popular screenings sell out
- **Read descriptions:** Find films that match your interests
- **Stay for Q&As:** Hearing from filmmakers enhances the experience
- **Dress up:** Opening/closing nights are festive
