## World-Class Opera in Paradise

The Sarasota Opera is renowned for exceptional productions in the intimate, historic Sarasota Opera House. The winter/spring season brings beloved classics and fresh discoveries.

## The Season

### Productions
- Classic operas (Verdi, Puccini, Mozart)
- Rarely performed gems
- Contemporary works
- Opera in Concert series

### The Venue
The historic Sarasota Opera House, celebrating its centennial in 2026, provides exceptional acoustics and an intimate viewing experience.

## Ticket Information

- **Single tickets:** $40-150
- **Season packages:** Available for multi-opera savings
- **Students/Youth:** Discounted pricing

## Tips

- **Book early:** Popular productions sell out
- **Dress code:** Dressy/smart casual
- **Pre-opera dining:** Downtown restaurants nearby
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours, a worthy cultural excursion
