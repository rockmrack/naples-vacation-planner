## Naples' Ultimate Food Event

Taste of Collier brings together 40+ of Naples' best restaurants, each serving signature samplings, at beautiful Bayfront. It's the ultimate way to discover Naples dining.

## The Experience

### Restaurant Samplings
Each participating restaurant serves small plates of their signature dishes. It's like dining at dozens of restaurants in one evening.

### Variety
- Fine dining
- Casual favorites
- International cuisine
- Sweet treats

### Entertainment
- Live music
- Bay views
- Celebrity chef appearances
- VIP experiences

## For a Good Cause

Proceeds benefit local charities, making your delicious evening also a charitable contribution.

## Tickets

- **General admission:** $50-65
- **VIP:** $75+ (early entry, exclusive tastings)
- **Note:** Tickets often sell out

## Tips

- **Pace yourself:** Lots to taste
- **Come hungry:** But don't fill up fast
- **Arrive early:** Most popular items run out
- **Rideshare:** Drinks are available
