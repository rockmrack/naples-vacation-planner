## Southwest Florida's Premier Community Theater

The Naples Players has been entertaining audiences for over 60 years with professional-quality productions at the beautiful Sugden Community Theatre on 5th Avenue South.

## The Season

### What to Expect
- Broadway musicals
- Classic and contemporary plays
- Comedies and dramas
- Special holiday productions

### Quality Productions
The Naples Players is consistently ranked among America's best community theaters, with production values rivaling professional companies.

## The Venue

Sugden Community Theatre's 5th Avenue location makes for easy combination with downtown dining before or after the show.

## Tickets

- **Shows:** $35-55
- **Season subscriptions:** Available for savings
- **Student/Youth:** Discounted pricing

## Tips

- **Book early:** Popular shows sell out
- **Dinner before:** 5th Avenue restaurants nearby
- **Parking:** Available in downtown Naples
