## 30 Years of Art Excellence

The Naples New Year's Art Fair has been kicking off the year with world-class art for three decades. This juried art show brings together over 200 artists from across the country to showcase paintings, sculpture, jewelry, photography, and more.

## Event Details

- **When:** January 3-4, 2026, 10am - 5pm daily
- **Where:** Cambier Park, downtown Naples
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Parking:** Street parking and nearby lots

## What to Expect

### Art Categories
- Painting (oil, acrylic, watercolor)
- Sculpture and 3D art
- Photography
- Jewelry and wearable art
- Ceramics and pottery
- Glass art
- Mixed media

### Beyond Art
- Live music throughout the day
- Food vendors and refreshments
- Artist demonstrations
- Meet the artists and learn about their work

## Why Visit

This is one of the highest-rated art fairs in the country, drawing serious collectors and casual browsers alike. The setting in beautiful Cambier Park, with its mature trees and downtown Naples backdrop, makes for a perfect day out.

## Tips for Visitors

- **Come early:** Beat the crowds and see art at its freshest
- **Comfortable shoes:** You'll be walking on grass and paths
- **Prepare to buy:** Bring cash or cards for purchases
- **Take breaks:** Benches and refreshment areas available
