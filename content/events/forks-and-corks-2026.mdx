## Sarasota's Ultimate Food & Wine Experience

Now in its 19th year, Forks & Corks brings together world-class chefs, prestigious wineries, and culinary enthusiasts for five days of epicurean events culminating in the Grand Tasting at The Ringling Museum.

## Event Schedule

### Thursday-Friday
- Winemaker dinners at top Sarasota restaurants
- Intimate multi-course experiences
- Meet winemakers and chefs

### Saturday: Grand Tasting
- **1-4pm at Ringling Museum Courtyard**
- 100+ wines and champagnes
- Gourmet food stations from top restaurants
- Live entertainment
- This is the main event!

### Additional Events
- Celebrity chef demonstrations
- Educational seminars
- Special dinners

## Ticket Tiers

- **Grand Tasting:** $75-100
- **Winemaker Dinners:** $150-200
- **VIP Packages:** $250+

## Tips

- **Book early:** Winemaker dinners sell out
- **Grand Tasting:** The marquee event
- **Designate a driver:** Lots of wine!
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours, worth the trip
