## Honoring Dr. King's Legacy

The Naples Martin Luther King Jr. Day Parade brings the community together to celebrate the life and legacy of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. with a parade, music, and reflection.

## The Parade

### Participants
- Community organizations
- Schools and youth groups
- Local dignitaries
- Marching bands
- Faith communities

## Community Celebration

Beyond the parade, the day typically includes community gatherings, speakers, and events honoring Dr. King's message of equality, justice, and service.

## Event Details

- **When:** Martin Luther King Jr. Day (January 19, 2026)
- **Time:** Late morning start
- **Route:** Downtown Naples streets
- **Admission:** FREE

## A Day of Service

Many community members use the day for volunteer service, honoring Dr. King's commitment to serving others.
