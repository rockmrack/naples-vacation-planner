## Classic American Band Music

The Naples Concert Band continues the great American tradition of community band concerts. These free Sunday afternoon performances feature everything from marches and classical selections to Broadway favorites and patriotic anthems.

## The Repertoire

### What You'll Hear
- John Philip Sousa marches
- Classical overtures and excerpts
- Broadway medleys
- Popular music arrangements
- Patriotic favorites

## The Setting

The Cambier Park Bandshell provides perfect acoustics and a charming setting. Audience members spread across the lawn with blankets and chairs for a quintessentially American afternoon.

## A Naples Tradition

The band draws talented musicians from across Southwest Florida, many with professional and teaching backgrounds. The quality is exceptional for a community ensemble.

## Tips

- **Lawn chairs/blankets:** Limited fixed seating
- **2pm start:** Perfect afternoon activity
- **Check schedule:** Concert dates vary
- **Combine with downtown:** Walk to 5th Avenue after
