## A Celebration of Horticultural Excellence

The Naples Flower Show transforms Naples Botanical Garden into a showcase of floral artistry, garden design, and horticultural achievement. It's a must for garden lovers.

## What to See

### Design Competitions
- Large-scale garden designs
- Creative container gardens
- Floral arrangements
- Photography exhibition

### Educational Programs
- Expert lectures
- Hands-on workshops
- Master gardener advice
- Plant sales

## The Setting

Naples Botanical Garden provides a stunning 170-acre backdrop for the show, with themed gardens and cultivated collections complementing the special exhibits.

## Event Details

- **When:** March 20-22, 2026
- **Hours:** 9am-5pm
- **Tickets:** $15-25 (includes garden admission)
- **Parking:** Free at the garden

## Tips

- **Early morning:** Best for photography
- **Comfortable shoes:** Lots of walking
- **Bring a hat:** Sun exposure
- **Shop the plant sale:** Unique finds
