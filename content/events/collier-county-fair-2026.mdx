## Classic American Fair Fun

The Collier County Fair brings traditional fair excitement to Naples with carnival rides, agricultural exhibits, live entertainment, fair food, and family memories.

## What to Expect

### Rides & Midway
- Carnival rides for all ages
- Thrill rides for adventurers
- Kiddie rides for the little ones
- Games and prizes

### Agricultural Heritage
- Livestock shows
- 4-H competitions
- Agricultural exhibits
- Farm demonstrations

### Fair Food
- Funnel cakes
- Corn dogs
- Deep-fried everything
- Sweet treats

### Entertainment
- Live concerts
- Pig races
- Magic shows
- Hypnotist performances

## Ticket Information

- **Gate admission:** $10-15
- **Ride wristbands:** Additional
- **Discounts:** Kids, seniors, special days

## Tips

- **Weekday visits:** Less crowded
- **Comfortable shoes:** Lots of walking
- **Bring cash:** Faster at food vendors
