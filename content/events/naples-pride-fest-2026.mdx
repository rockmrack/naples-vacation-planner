## Celebrating Diversity in Paradise

Naples PRIDE Fest is Southwest Florida's premier LGBTQ+ celebration, bringing together the community and allies for a day of entertainment, education, and unity in beautiful Cambier Park.

## Event Highlights

### Entertainment
- **Main stage performances:** Local and regional entertainers
- **DJs and music:** Dance and celebrate
- **Drag performances:** Spectacular shows
- **Live bands:** Diverse musical acts

### Community
- **Speakers:** Community leaders and advocates
- **Vendor marketplace:** LGBTQ+-owned businesses and allies
- **Resource booths:** Health, advocacy, and community organizations
- **Family activities:** Kid-friendly zone

## Why Attend

Naples Pride has grown significantly in recent years, reflecting Southwest Florida's welcoming and diverse community. It's a celebration of love, acceptance, and equality in one of Florida's most beautiful cities.

## Tips

- **Sunscreen:** June sun is intense
- **Water:** Stay hydrated
- **Comfortable shoes:** Grass and walking
- **Colorful attire:** Show your pride!
