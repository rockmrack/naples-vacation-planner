## Art on the Island

Marco Island Fine Art in the Park transforms Veterans Community Park into an outdoor gallery featuring over 100 artists from across the country. This juried show brings exceptional quality to the island setting.

## What to See

### Art Categories
- Painting (oil, acrylic, watercolor)
- Sculpture and 3D works
- Photography
- Fine jewelry
- Ceramics
- Glass art
- Mixed media

## The Setting

The shaded Veterans Community Park provides a pleasant setting for browsing art. The island atmosphere is relaxed, and artists are available to discuss their work.

## Event Details

- **When:** March 14-15, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am-5pm Saturday, 10am-4pm Sunday
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Location:** Veterans Community Park

## Perfect Day Combination

Combine the art show with a Marco Island beach visit, lunch at a waterfront restaurant, and exploring the island's shops and galleries.

## Tips

- **Parking:** Free at the park
- **Comfortable shoes:** Walking on grass
- **Bring buying capacity:** Original art for sale
