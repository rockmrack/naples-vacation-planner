## Authentic Jewish Cuisine

The Sarasota Jewish Food Festival celebrates traditional Jewish food and culture with homemade specialties, entertainment, and community gathering.

## The Food

### Traditional Favorites
- Pastrami and corned beef sandwiches
- Matzo ball soup
- Knishes
- Potato latkes
- Blintzes
- Babka and rugelach
- And more deli classics

## Beyond Food

- Live klezmer music
- Cultural displays
- Judaica vendors
- Community celebration

## Event Details

- **When:** February 1, 2026
- **Time:** 10am-3pm (typically)
- **Admission:** FREE (food for purchase)
- **Location:** Temple Sinai, Sarasota

## Tips

- **Come hungry:** Lots to eat
- **Arrive early:** Popular items sell out
- **Bring cash:** Faster at food stations
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours north
