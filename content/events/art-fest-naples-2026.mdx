## 200+ Artists in Old Naples

Art Fest Naples at Fleischmann Park is one of the area's most anticipated art events, drawing over 200 artists from across the country to this beautiful Old Naples park setting.

## What to See

### Art Categories
- Painting (all media)
- Sculpture and 3D
- Photography
- Fine jewelry
- Ceramics
- Glass art
- Fiber art
- Mixed media

## The Setting

Fleischmann Park provides a spacious, shaded setting in the heart of Old Naples. The park's mature trees and convenient location make this an ideal art show venue.

## Event Details

- **When:** January 17-18, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am-5pm both days
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Parking:** Available in park and nearby

## Combine With Downtown

Fleischmann Park is close to downtown Naples. After browsing art, walk to 5th Avenue or 3rd Street for lunch and additional exploration.
