## Naples' Monthly Block Party

Evening on Fifth transforms 5th Avenue South into a street festival every first Saturday from October through May. Live music fills the air, artists display their work, and the community comes together for an evening of celebration.

## What to Expect

### Live Entertainment
- **Multiple stages:** Bands throughout the avenue
- **Variety of genres:** Rock, jazz, acoustic, dance
- **Strolling performers:** Street entertainers

### Art & Culture
- **Local artists:** Displaying work along the avenue
- **Pop-up galleries:** Special exhibitions
- **Interactive experiences:** Get creative

### Dining & Drinks
- **Restaurant patios:** Extended outdoor seating
- **Happy hour specials:** Many restaurants participate
- **Food and beverage vendors:** Options throughout
