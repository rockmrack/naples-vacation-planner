## A Celebration of Gulf Coast Flavors

The Marco Island Seafood & Music Festival brings together the best of island livingâ€”fresh Gulf seafood, live music, and the beautiful Marco Island setting. This annual celebration is a highlight of the island's event calendar.

## What to Eat

### Fresh Seafood
- Stone crab claws (in season through mid-May)
- Gulf shrimp in multiple preparations
- Fresh fish tacos
- Lobster rolls
- Local oysters

### More Food Options
- Key lime pie and tropical desserts
- Food truck favorites
- Beverages and tropical drinks

## Live Entertainment

Multiple stages feature live music throughout the festival, from Gulf Coast country to rock and beach reggae. The setting creates that perfect island vibe.

## Island Setting

Marco Island's beautiful beaches and parks provide the backdrop. The combination of seafood, music, and Gulf views creates an unforgettable experience.

## Tips

- **Weekend event:** Saturday and Sunday
- **Free admission:** Pay for food and drinks
- **From Naples:** About 30 minutes south
- **Make it a day trip:** Explore Marco Island
