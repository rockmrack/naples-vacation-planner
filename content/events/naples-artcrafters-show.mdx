## Monthly Art in the Park

The Naples Artcrafters have been bringing high-quality fine art and crafts to Cambier Park since 1959. These monthly shows (running November through April) feature juried artists from across Florida and beyond.

## Show Dates 2026

- **January 10, 2026**
- **February (check website for date)**
- **March 7, 2026**
- **April 11, 2026**

## What You'll Find

### Art Categories
- **Paintings:** Oil, acrylic, watercolor
- **Photography:** Fine art prints and originals
- **Jewelry:** Handcrafted pieces
- **Pottery and ceramics**
- **Sculpture:** Various media
- **Fiber arts:** Wearable and decorative

### The Setting
Beautiful Cambier Park provides a lush, shaded setting for browsing art. The mature trees and downtown Naples backdrop make this a pleasant morning or afternoon activity.

## Tips

- **Moderate crowds:** Less intense than major art fairs
- **Meet the artists:** They're happy to chat
- **Affordable pieces:** Find art at various price points
- **Combine with downtown:** Walk to 5th or 3rd Street after
