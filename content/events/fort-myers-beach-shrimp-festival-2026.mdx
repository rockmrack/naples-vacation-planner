## Celebrating the Gulf's Pink Gold

The Fort Myers Beach Shrimp Festival honors the region's shrimping heritage with parades, pageants, live entertainment, and of course, plenty of fresh Gulf shrimp.

## Festival Highlights

### Shrimp Shrimp Shrimp
- Fresh Gulf shrimp prepared every way
- Shrimp boils
- Fried shrimp
- Shrimp scampi
- And more shrimp!

### Parade
The Shrimp Festival Parade rolls through Times Square with floats, bands, and community fun.

### Entertainment
- Live music all weekend
- Arts and crafts vendors
- Family activities
- Blessing of the Fleet

## Event Details

- **When:** March 7-8, 2026
- **Location:** Times Square, Fort Myers Beach
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Parking:** Beach parking (fees apply)

## Tips

- **From Naples:** About 40 minutes north on 41
- **Go for the shrimp:** It's the star
- **Watch the parade:** Saturday morning
