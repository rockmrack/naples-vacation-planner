## Popular Music Meets World-Class Orchestra

The Naples Philharmonic Pops concerts bring popular music, Broadway favorites, and special guest artists to Hayes Hall in a celebration of beloved tunes performed by a world-class orchestra.

## The Concerts

### Pops Programming
- Broadway hits and show tunes
- Movie music and film scores
- Rock and pop classics
- Holiday favorites
- Tribute concerts

### Special Guest Artists
Guest vocalists and instrumentalists join the orchestra for special performances throughout the season.

## The Venue

Hayes Hall at Artis—Naples is a state-of-the-art concert hall with exceptional acoustics and comfortable seating.

## Tickets

- **Individual concerts:** $40-100
- **Subscription packages:** Available for savings
- **Location matters:** Premium seats cost more

## Tips

- **Dress:** Smart casual to dressy
- **Pre-concert dining:** Several nearby options
- **Arrive early:** Explore the campus
