## Fireworks Over Naples Bay

Naples celebrates Independence Day with spectacular fireworks over Naples Bay, live entertainment, and festive activities throughout downtown.

## The Celebration

### Fireworks
The main event: a stunning fireworks display over Naples Bay, visible from Bayfront, Crayton Cove, and throughout downtown Naples.

### Entertainment
Live music and entertainment throughout the day lead up to the evening fireworks display.

### Community Fun
- Patriotic decorations throughout downtown
- Restaurant specials
- Beach day before fireworks

## Best Viewing Spots

- Naples Bay Resort area
- Bayfront
- Crayton Cove
- Naples Pier (if open)
- Boat on Naples Bay

## Tips

- **Arrive early:** Viewing spots fill quickly
- **Bring chairs/blankets:** For lawn viewing
- **Parking challenge:** Plan ahead
- **Dinner reservations:** Popular evening
- **Hot:** Stay hydrated, seek shade
