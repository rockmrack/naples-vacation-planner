## The Gulf Coast's Premier Boat Show

The Suncoast Boat Show brings together hundreds of boats—from fishing boats to luxury yachts—at beautiful Marina Jack in downtown Sarasota. It's the region's largest marine event.

## What to See

### Boats on Display
- Fishing boats (center console, bay boats, offshore)
- Cruising yachts (power and sail)
- Pontoons and deck boats
- Personal watercraft
- Kayaks and paddleboards

### Marine Products
- Electronics and technology
- Fishing gear and tackle
- Boat accessories
- Services and insurance

## In the Water

Many boats are displayed in the water at Marina Jack, allowing close-up viewing and comparison. Sea trials may be available for select vessels.

## Event Details

- **When:** April 17-19, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am-6pm (varies by day)
- **Tickets:** $15-20
- **Location:** Marina Jack, downtown Sarasota

## Tips

- **Comfortable shoes:** Lots of dock walking
- **Sunscreen:** Florida sun is strong
- **Questions ready:** Dealers want to talk
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours, worth the trip for boaters
