## 28 Years of Bikes, Music & More

Thunder By The Bay is Southwest Florida's premier motorcycle event, drawing thousands of riders and enthusiasts for three days of two-wheeled fun, live music, and charitable giving.

## Event Highlights

### Motorcycles
- Bike shows and competitions
- Vendor marketplace
- Demo rides
- Custom builds on display

### Live Music
- National touring acts
- Regional bands
- Multiple stages
- Concerts all three days

### More Fun
- Food vendors
- Beer garden
- Contests and games
- Charity poker run

## For a Good Cause

Thunder By The Bay raises funds for Suncoast Charities for Children, which serves children in the Sarasota and Manatee area facing special challenges.

## Plan Your Visit

- **When:** February 13-15, 2026
- **Where:** Sarasota Fairgrounds
- **Tickets:** $20-50 depending on day/package
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours north
