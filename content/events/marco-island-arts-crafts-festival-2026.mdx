## Island Artistry

The Marco Island Arts and Crafts Festival brings together local and regional artisans at St. Mark's Episcopal Church for a weekend of handmade arts, crafts, and creativity.

## What to Find

### Art Categories
- Paintings and prints
- Handmade jewelry
- Pottery and ceramics
- Photography
- Fiber arts and textiles
- Woodwork
- Unique crafts

## Community Event

This church-hosted event has a friendly, community atmosphere. Artists are happy to discuss their work, and the setting is relaxed and welcoming.

## Event Details

- **When:** February 14-15, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am-4pm
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Location:** St. Mark's Episcopal Church

## Make a Day of It

Combine the festival with a Marco Island beach visit, lunch at a waterfront restaurant, and exploring the island's shops.
