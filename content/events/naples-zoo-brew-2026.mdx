## Craft Beer Meets Wildlife

Naples Zoo Brew is an adults-only evening event combining 100+ craft beers with a stroll through Naples Zoo at Caribbean Gardens. It's one of Naples' most unique beer events.

## The Experience

### Craft Beer
- 100+ craft beers, ciders, and seltzers
- Local Florida breweries
- National craft favorites
- Unlimited tastings

### The Zoo After Dark
- Zoo animals active at dusk
- Unique atmosphere
- Photo opportunities
- Animal keeper talks

### Entertainment
- Live music
- Food trucks
- Games and activities

## Ticket Options

- **General admission:** $50 (entry at 6pm)
- **VIP:** $75 (early entry, exclusive tastings)

## Tips

- **21+ only:** Valid ID required
- **Designate a driver:** Rideshare encouraged
- **Comfortable shoes:** Walking the zoo
- **Last chance animals:** Many animals active at dusk
