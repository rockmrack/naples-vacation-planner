## Art in Downtown Bonita

The Bonita Springs Art Walk transforms downtown's Old 41 Road into an outdoor gallery with artists, galleries, live music, and entertainment every second Saturday.

## What to Expect

### Street Fair Atmosphere
- Local artists displaying and selling work
- Open galleries with new exhibitions
- Live music and entertainment
- Food and beverage options

### Art Categories
- Painting and prints
- Jewelry and crafts
- Photography
- Sculpture

## The Setting

Downtown Bonita Springs has revitalized Old 41 into a walkable arts and entertainment district. The Art Walk showcases this neighborhood's creative energy.

## Schedule

- **When:** 2nd Saturday of each month
- **Time:** 4-8pm
- **Season:** Year-round (best Nov-Apr)

## Tips

- **Between Naples \u0026 Fort Myers:** Convenient location
- **Evening event:** Cool and comfortable
- **Dinner after:** Nearby restaurants
