## Art at Sarasota's Iconic Circle

The St. Armands Fine Art Festival transforms Sarasota's renowned shopping and dining destination into an outdoor gallery featuring over 200 artists from across the nation.

## The Setting

St. Armands Circle is already a destination for upscale shopping and dining. During the art festival, the circle becomes even more vibrant with artists displaying original works amid the boutiques and restaurants.

## What to See

### Art Categories
- Painting (all media)
- Sculpture
- Photography
- Jewelry
- Ceramics
- Glass
- Mixed media

## The Perfect Day

- Browse art in the morning
- Lunch at a Circle restaurant
- More art appreciation
- Sunset on the beach (nearby)
- Dinner on the Circle

## Event Details

- **When:** May 23-24, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am-5pm
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Parking:** Street and nearby lots

## Tips

- **Combine with beach:** Lido Key is minutes away
- **Make reservations:** Circle restaurants fill up
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours, excellent day trip
