## Art Meets Upscale Shopping

The Mercato Art Celebration combines world-class art with Naples' premier shopping and dining destination. Stroll among 100+ artist booths, then enjoy lunch at one of Mercato's excellent restaurants.

## Event Details

- **When:** January 24-25, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am - 5pm daily
- **Where:** Mercato, North Naples
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Parking:** Free in Mercato garage

## The Setting

Mercato's modern, open-air design provides an elegant backdrop for fine art. Artists set up along the main street and plaza areas, creating a gallery-like experience among the boutiques and restaurants.

## What to See

### Art Categories
- Original paintings and prints
- Fine jewelry design
- Sculpture and 3D works
- Photography
- Mixed media and crafts

### Beyond Art
- Mercato shopping: Upscale boutiques
- Dining: 20+ restaurants
- Entertainment: Live music throughout

## Tips

- **Combine activities:** Shop, art, dine
- **Easy parking:** Garage is convenient
- **Stay for dinner:** Make it an evening
- **North Naples base:** Great for nearby hotel guests
