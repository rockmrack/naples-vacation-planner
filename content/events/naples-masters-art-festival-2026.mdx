## Masters of Their Craft on 5th Avenue

The Naples Masters Art Festival transforms 5th Avenue South into an outdoor gallery featuring over 200 nationally recognized artists in a prestigious juried show.

## What to See

### Art Categories
- Painting (various media)
- Sculpture
- Photography
- Mixed media
- Fine jewelry
- Ceramics
- Glass

### The Artists
This show attracts established artists with national reputations. Quality is consistently high across all categories.

## The Setting

5th Avenue South provides an elegant backdrop for fine art. The combination of art, dining, and boutique shopping makes for a perfect day.

## Event Details

- **When:** February 28 - March 1, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am-5pm both days
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Location:** 5th Avenue South

## Tips

- **Quality over quantity:** Take your time
- **Meet artists:** They're happy to discuss work
- **Combine with dining:** 5th Ave restaurants
- **Early for best viewing:** Mornings are less crowded
