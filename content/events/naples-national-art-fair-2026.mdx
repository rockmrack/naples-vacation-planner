## One of the Nation's Top Art Fairs

The Naples National Art Fair is consistently ranked among the top art festivals in America. This juried show brings together exceptional artists from across the country for three days of art excellence in beautiful Cambier Park.

## Event Details

- **When:** February 20-22, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am - 5pm daily
- **Where:** Cambier Park, downtown Naples
- **Admission:** FREE

## What to See

### Art Categories
- **Painting:** All media, from realism to abstract
- **Sculpture:** Bronze, stone, mixed media
- **Ceramics:** Functional and decorative
- **Photography:** Fine art prints
- **Jewelry:** Original designed pieces
- **Glass art:** Blown, fused, and more
- **Fiber arts:** Wearable and decorative

### Experience
- Meet artists and discuss their work
- Watch demonstrations
- Find one-of-a-kind pieces
- Enjoy live entertainment

## Tips

- **Come early:** Morning crowds are lighter
- **Wear comfortable shoes:** Lots of walking
- **Bring buying capacity:** You'll want something
- **Multiple visits:** Three days to explore
