## Opa! Greek Celebration in Naples

The Naples Greek Festival is a beloved annual celebration of Greek food, music, and culture. The authentic cuisine and festive atmosphere draw crowds from throughout Southwest Florida.

## The Food

### Greek Favorites
- Gyros (lamb and chicken)
- Moussaka
- Spanakopita
- Souvlaki
- Baklava and Greek pastries
- Greek wine and ouzo

## Entertainment

- Live Greek music
- Traditional dancing
- Dance lessons available
- Cultural demonstrations

## Church Tours

The beautiful St. Katherine Greek Orthodox Church offers tours showcasing Byzantine architecture and iconography.

## Event Details

- **When:** February 20-22, 2026
- **Admission:** FREE (food for purchase)
- **Location:** St. Katherine Greek Orthodox Church
- **Parking:** Available on site

## Tips

- **Come hungry:** Amazing food
- **Try the pastries:** Don't miss baklava
- **Watch the dancing:** Join in if you dare!
