## 200+ Artists on Sarasota Bay

The Sarasota Fine Art Festival is one of the region's premier juried art shows, featuring original handmade works from local, regional, and national artisans in a stunning bayfront setting.

## What to See

### Art Categories
- Original paintings and prints
- Sculpture and 3D works
- Fine jewelry
- Ceramics and pottery
- Photography
- Glass art
- Fiber arts

## The Setting

J.D. Hamel Park provides a beautiful backdrop with Sarasota Bay views. The combination of art and waterfront makes this a visually stunning event.

## Plan Your Visit

- **When:** January 3-4, 2026, 10am-5pm
- **Where:** J.D. Hamel Park, downtown Sarasota
- **Admission:** FREE
- **From Naples:** About 2 hours north on I-75

## Tips

- **Combines with downtown:** Walk to Main Street after
- **Bring buying capacity:** Original art available
- **Comfortable shoes:** Grass and walkways
