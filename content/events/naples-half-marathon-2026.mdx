## A Race in Paradise

The Naples Half Marathon offers runners the rare opportunity to race through one of America's most beautiful cities. The flat, fast course winds through downtown Naples and along the waterfront, offering stunning scenery and perfect winter running weather.

## Race Details

- **Date:** January 18, 2026
- **Distance:** Half Marathon (13.1 miles)
- **Start Time:** 7:00 AM
- **Course:** Downtown Naples, flat and scenic

## The Course

The route takes runners through:
- Naples' historic downtown
- Waterfront areas with bay views
- Tree-lined residential neighborhoods
- Past iconic Naples landmarks
