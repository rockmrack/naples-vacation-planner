## World-Class Sand Art on America's #1 Beach

The Siesta Key Crystal Classic brings together the world's best sand sculptors to create incredible works of art on the famous white quartz sand of Siesta Key Beach.

## The Competition

### Professional Division
World-class sculptors compete to create massive, intricate works from nothing but sand and water. The skill is extraordinary.

### Master Sculptors
Watch masters shape seemingly impossible details—faces, feathers, architectural elements—from Siesta Key's unique crystal sand.

## Why Siesta Key

Siesta Key's unique quartz crystal sand provides ideal sculpting conditions. The sand is cool to the touch and holds detail beautifully.

## Event Details

- **When:** November 14-23, 2026 (10 days)
- **Admission:** FREE
- **Location:** Siesta Key Beach
- **Best viewing:** Later in the event when sculptures are complete

- **From Naples:** About 2.5 hours for this unique event
