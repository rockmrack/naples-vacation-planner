## Naples' Largest Farmers Market

The Vanderbilt Farmers Market is the granddaddy of Naples-area markets. With over 70 vendors during season, it's a one-stop shop for produce, prepared foods, artisan goods, and community connection.

## What Makes It Special

### Pet-Friendly
Unlike some markets, Vanderbilt welcomes your four-legged friends. Dogs on leashes are a common sightâ€”many vendors keep treats on hand.

### Vendor Variety
- **Fresh produce:** Local and organic options
- **Prepared foods:** Ready-to-eat breakfast and lunch
- **Baked goods:** Bread, pastries, desserts
- **Artisan products:** Soaps, jewelry, crafts
- **Plants and flowers:** Tropical plants and bouquets

### Live Entertainment
Local musicians perform throughout the morning, adding to the festive atmosphere.

## Seasonal Schedule

- **Peak Season (November-May):** Full market with 70+ vendors
- **Off-season:** Reduced vendor count but still operational

## Tips

- **Arrive at 8am:** For best selection and easier parking
- **Bring the dog:** It's pet-friendly!
- **Eat breakfast:** Many prepared food options available
- **Parking:** Free in the shopping center lot
