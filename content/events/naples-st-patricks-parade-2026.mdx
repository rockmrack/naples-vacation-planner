## Go Green in Naples

The Naples St. Patrick's Day Parade turns 3rd Street South into an Irish celebration with floats, marching bands, dancers, and plenty of green. It's one of Naples' most festive community events.

## The Parade

### What to Expect
- Colorful floats
- Irish dancers
- Marching bands
- Community groups
- Classic cars
- Lots of green!

## Family Fun

This is a family-friendly event that draws crowds of all ages. Children love the parade atmosphere, and the downtown setting provides a festive backdrop.

## The Celebration

After the parade, 3rd Street restaurants and bars continue the celebration with Irish-themed specials, live music, and general merriment.

## Event Details

- **When:** Saturday before St. Patrick's Day (typically)
- **Time:** Usually 11am start
- **Route:** 3rd Street South
- **Admission:** FREE

## Tips

- **Arrive early:** Claim your viewing spot
- **Wear green:** It's required!
- **Lunch reservations:** Make them in advance
- **Family friendly:** Great for kids
