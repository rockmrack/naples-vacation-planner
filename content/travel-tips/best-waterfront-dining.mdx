## Dining with a View

Naples is all about the water. Here are the top spots to enjoy a meal with a view, categorized by vibe.

---

## Ultra-Fine Dining (Sunset Views)

### 1. Baleen Restaurant (LaPlaya Resort)
**Vibe:** Sophisticated luxury. Tables directly on the sand.
**Best For:** Romantic anniversaries.
**Dish to Try:** Blackened Grouper.
*Tip: Reserve a "beach table" weeks in advance.*

### 2. The Turtle Club
**Vibe:** Old-school Naples elegance.
**Best For:** Sunset dinners with your toes in the sand.
**Note:** Closing for renovations occasionally; check status.

### 3. Nosh on Naples Bay
**Vibe:** Modern, chic, global cuisine.
**Best For:** Watching yachts pass by.
**Location:** Naples Bay Resort.

---

## Upscale Casual (Naples Bay)

### 4. The Dock at Crayton Cove
**Vibe:** Nautical, open-air, bustling.
**Best For:** Post-boating lunch or lively dinner.
**Dish to Try:** The Dock's Famous Nachos.

### 5. Riverwalk at Tin City
**Vibe:** Classic Florida, fun energy.
**Best For:** Families and casual groups.
**View:** Gordon River and working fishing boats.

### 6. Boathouse on Naples Bay
**Vibe:** Historic landmark, panoramic views.
**Best For:** Golden hour cocktails.

---

## Casual & Fun (Feet in Sand)

### 7. Gumbo Limbo (Ritz-Carlton)
**Vibe:** Tiki luxury.
**Best For:** Sunset cocktails and light bites.
**Note:** Open to public (check seasonal policy).

### 8. Sunset Beach Bar (The Naples Beach Hotel)
*(Note: Hotel undergoing redevelopment - check status)*
**Alternative:** **Rhode's End** at Naples Grande for beachfront Greek food.

### 9. Buzz's Lighthouse (North Naples)
**Vibe:** Dive bar charm on the backwater.
**Best For:** The best Reuben sandwich and cold beer.

---

## Hidden Jewel

### 10. Celebration Park
**Vibe:** Waterfront food truck park with a full bar.
**Best For:** Variety and younger crowds.
**Location:** Bayshore Arts District.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Timing:** During winter, sun sets around 5:30-6:00 PM. Book your table for 5:00 PM to catch the full show. In summer (8:00 PM sunset), aim for 7:30 PM.
</ExpertTip>
