## The Classics

### 1. Jane's Cafe on 3rd
**Location:** 3rd Street South (Old Naples)
**Vibe:** Courtyard dining, charming, see-and-be-seen.
**Order:** Fabulous French Toast or Lobster Benedict.

### 2. EJ's Bayfront Cafe
**Location:** Bayfront
**Vibe:** Local favorite, fast service, reliable.
**Order:** Skillets and Banana Stuffed French Toast.
*Note: Consistently voted Best Breakfast.*

### 3. The Cove Inn Coffee Shop
**Location:** Naples Bay
**Vibe:** Old-school diner, cash only (sometimes), no frills.
**Order:** Pancakes. The best in town.

---

## Trendy Brunch

### 4. The French
**Location:** 5th Avenue South
**Vibe:** Parisian chic, jazz brunch (Sundays).
**Order:** Croque Madame and Champagne.

### 5. Lake Park Diner
**Location:** 7th Ave N
**Vibe:** Modern, healthy-ish, outdoor garden.
**Order:** Breakfast sandwich and a smoothie.

### 6. First Watch (Banyan Blvd)
**Location:** Multiple, but Banyan is the original flagship.
**Vibe:** Consistently fresh and healthy.

---

## Hidden Gems

### 7. Goldies Restaurant
**Location:** Industrial park area
**Vibe:** Pure local diner. Zero tourists.
**Order:** Biscuits and gravy.

### 8. Blueberry's
**Location:** Tamiami Trail N
**Vibe:** Huge menu, family friendly.
**Order:** Anything with blueberries.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Weekend Wait Times:** In season (Jan-Apr), the wait at Jane's or EJ's can be 45+ minutes by 10 AM. Go before 9 AM or after 1 PM.
</ExpertTip>
