## Naples Loves Dogs (Mostly)

Naples is pet-friendly, but dogs are NOT allowed on most beaches. Here's how to navigate it.

---

## Dog-Friendly Beaches

### 1. Bonita Beach Dog Park
**Location:** Bonita Springs (20 min north).
**Hours:** Sunrise to sunset.
**Rules:** Dogs can be off-leash in designated area.

### 2. Dog Beach at Lover's Key (Seasonal)
**Location:** Lover's Key State Park.
**Status:** Check seasonal restrictions.

**Note:** Naples and Marco Island beaches prohibit dogs on the sand.

---

## Dog-Friendly Parks

- **Naples Preserve** – Leashed dogs welcome on trails.
- **Freedom Park** – 50-acre preserve, dogs allowed.
- **Poinciana Dog Park** – Off-leash area in East Naples.
- **Canine Cove (Sugden Park)** – Off-leash dog park.

---

## Pet-Friendly Hotels

- **The Inn on Fifth** – Downtown, dogs under 25 lbs.
- **LaPlaya Beach Resort** – Pet fee applies.
- **Many Vacation Rentals** – Check Vrbo/Airbnb filters.

---

## Pet Services

- **Camp Bow Wow Naples** – Doggy daycare.
- **VCA Naples Animal Hospital** – 24-hour vet.
- **PetSmart / Petco** – Multiple locations.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Restaurant Patios:** Many restaurants with outdoor seating welcome dogs (Barbatella, The Dock). Ask when you arrive.
</ExpertTip>
