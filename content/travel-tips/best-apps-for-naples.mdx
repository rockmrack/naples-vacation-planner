## Travel Smarter with These Apps

Download these before you arrive to make your Naples trip smoother.

---

## Transportation

### Uber / Lyft
**Why:** Essential for airport transfers and nights out.
**Tip:** Check both apps—pricing varies.

### ParkMobile
**Why:** Pay for beach and street parking from your phone.
**Coverage:** Most Naples meters.

### Google Maps / Apple Maps
**Why:** Navigation. Works well in Naples.
**Tip:** Download offline maps in case of spotty cell service in the Everglades.

---

## Dining

### OpenTable
**Why:** Reserve at top Naples restaurants.
**Tip:** Book 2-3 weeks ahead for peak season.

### Yelp
**Why:** Quick reviews and photos.
**Alternative:** Google Reviews.

---

## Weather & Safety

### Weather Channel / MyRadar
**Why:** Track afternoon thunderstorms (summer) and hurricane updates.

### MyFWC (Florida Fish & Wildlife)
**Why:** Red tide status, fishing licenses, wildlife alerts.

---

## Beach & Nature

### AllTrails
**Why:** Find hiking and walking trails (Fakahatchee, Corkscrew, etc.).

### Tides Near Me
**Why:** Check tide charts for shelling.

---

## Local Insights

### NextDoor (after arrival)
**Why:** See local recommendations and events.

### Naples Daily News App
**Why:** Local news, events, restaurant openings.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Offline Essentials:** Download Google Maps offline for Everglades trips—cell service is limited.
</ExpertTip>
