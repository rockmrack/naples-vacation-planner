## La Dolce Vita in Naples

Naples, FL has a strong Italian-American community, and the dining scene reflects it.

---

## Upscale Italian

### 1. Barbatella
**Location:** 3rd Street South
**Vibe:** Chic, modern Italian small plates.
**Order:** Burrata and the Tagliatelle.

### 2. Osteria Tulia
**Location:** 5th Avenue South
**Vibe:** James Beard-nominated. Rustic-elegant.
**Order:** House-made pasta (any), Lamb Ragu.

### 3. The Local
**Vibe:** Farm-to-table with Italian influence.
**Order:** Seasonal menu changes frequently.

---

## Classic & Cozy

### 4. Ciao Ristorante
**Location:** Crayton Cove
**Vibe:** Old-school romantic, candlelit.
**Order:** Veal Marsala.

### 5. Caffé Milano
**Location:** 5th Ave
**Vibe:** Lively, great people-watching.
**Order:** Penne Arrabbiata.

### 6. Vergina
**Location:** 5th Ave
**Vibe:** Mediterranean-Italian with outdoor seating.
**Order:** Seafood Linguine.

---

## Casual Favorites

### 7. Blue Agave
**Wait:** It's Tex-Mex, but hear us out—their Italian specials are fire. (Kidding, skip this one for pure Italian.)

### 8. DaRuMa
**Wait:** This is Japanese. (I'm testing you.) See "Best Sushi" for this one.

### 9. Ristorante Ciao
**The Real Deal:** North Naples gem. Family-owned.
**Order:** Gnocchi Sorrentina.

  **Sunday Dinner Tradition:** Many Italian spots offer family-style Sunday dinners. Call ahead for specials.
