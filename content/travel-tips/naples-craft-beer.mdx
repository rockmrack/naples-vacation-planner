## Craft Beer in Paradise

The Naples craft beer scene is small but growing. Here are the best spots.

---

## Local Breweries

### 1. Ankrolab Brewing Company
**Location:** Bonita Springs
**Vibe:** Science-themed taproom, indoor/outdoor.
**Specialties:** IPAs, lagers, rotating experimentals.
**Food:** Food trucks on weekends.

### 2. Bone Hook Brewing Company
**Location:** North Naples
**Vibe:** Fishing and outdoor-themed. Large patio.
**Specialties:** Blonde Ale, Amber Ale.
**Food:** Full kitchen.

### 3. Bury Me Brewing
**Location:** Fort Myers (30 min)
**Vibe:** Rock and roll, skull decor, eclectic.
**Why Go:** Largest tap list in SWFL.

---

## Beer Bars

### 4. World of Beer
**Location:** Mercato
**Selection:** 50+ taps from around the world.
**Vibe:** Chain but solid selection.

### 5. Pelican Larry's
**Location:** Multiple
**Selection:** Good domestic and craft taps.
**Vibe:** Sports bar with wings.
