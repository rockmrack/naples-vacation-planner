## Steak Night in Naples

Naples has world-class steakhouses with Prime beef, extensive wine lists, and impeccable service.

---

## The Classics

### 1. The Capital Grille
**Location:** Mercato
**Vibe:** Clubby, dark wood, professional service.
**Order:** Bone-in Ribeye, Lobster Mac & Cheese side.
**Tip:** They dry-age on premises.

### 2. Ruth's Chris
**Location:** Tamiami Trail
**Vibe:** Consistent chain excellence.
**Order:** The signature sizzling butter finish.

### 3. Fleming's Prime Steakhouse
**Location:** Mercato
**Vibe:** Modern, lively, great bar.
**Order:** Prime Filet, any of the 100+ wines by the glass.

---

## Local Favorites

### 4. Shula's Steak House
**Location:** Naples Hilton
**Vibe:** Sports-themed (Don Shula legacy).
**Order:** The 48oz Porterhouse for two.

### 5. Chops City Grill
**Location:** 5th Avenue & Bonita Springs
**Vibe:** Upscale chop house with Asian fusion touches.
**Order:** Filet Mignon with truffle butter.
