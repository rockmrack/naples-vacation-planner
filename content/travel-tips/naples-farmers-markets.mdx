## Saturday Morning Tradition

Farmers markets are a beloved Naples tradition, especially during the winter season.

---

## Top Markets

### 1. Third Street South Farmers Market
**When:** Saturday, 7:30 AM – 12:00 PM (Nov – Apr)
**Where:** 3rd Street South, behind Tommy Bahama
**What:** Fresh produce, flowers, baked goods, live music.
**Vibe:** Upscale, social, people-watching.
**Tip:** Get there early for parking.

### 2. Naples Farmers Market at Cambier Park
**When:** Saturday, 6:30 AM – 11:00 AM (year-round, varies)
**Where:** Cambier Park
**What:** Local farmers, food trucks, crafts.
**Vibe:** Family-friendly.

### 3. Ave Maria Town Center Market
**When:** Saturday mornings (check schedule)
**Where:** Ave Maria, 40 min east
**What:** Small-town charm, local produce.

### 4. Bonita Springs Farmers Market
**When:** Saturday, 8 AM – 12 PM
**Where:** Riverside Park
**What:** Produce, plants, food vendors.

---

## What to Buy

- **Florida Citrus:** Oranges, grapefruit (winter).
- **Local Honey:** Great gift.
- **Fresh Bread & Pastries.**
- **Plants & Orchids.**

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Bring Cash:** Many vendors are cash-only. ATMs are available but lines can be long.
</ExpertTip>
