## The Golf Capital of the World

Naples has more golf holes per capita than almost anywhere on Earth. Here are the best places to play.

---

## Public Courses

### 1. Tiburón Golf Club
**Designer:** Greg Norman
**Location:** Ritz-Carlton Golf Resort
**Holes:** 36 (Black & Gold courses)
**Note:** Home of the PGA TOUR's QBE Shootout.

### 2. Naples Beach Hotel & Golf Club
**Status:** Undergoing redevelopment—check availability.
**History:** One of the oldest courses in Naples.

### 3. Lely Resort (Flamingo Island & Mustang)
**Location:** East Naples
**Value:** Great rates, especially in summer.
**Vibe:** Robert Trent Jones Sr. design.

---

## Semi-Private & Resort

### 4. The Club at Mediterra
**Access:** Limited public play via Golf Now.
**Quality:** Consistently ranked top in Florida.

### 5. Hammock Bay Golf Course (Marco Island)
**Designer:** Peter Jacobsen & Jim Hardy
**Access:** Semi-private, public welcome.

---

## Budget-Friendly

### 6. Valencia Golf & Country Club
**Why:** Solid course, fair pricing.

### 7. Arrowhead Golf Club
**Why:** Easy access, walkable layout.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Summer Deals:** May through October, greens fees drop 50-70%. It's hot, but a steal for serious golfers. Tee off early (7 AM) to beat the heat.
</ExpertTip>
