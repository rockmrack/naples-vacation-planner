## Wine Culture in Naples

Naples attracts sophisticated palates, and the wine scene reflects it.

---

## Wine Bars

### 1. The Wine Loft
**Location:** Mercato
**Vibe:** Modern tasting room, 40+ wines by the glass.
**Order:** Wine flight + cheese board.

### 2. Naples Flatbread & Wine Bar
**Location:** 5th Ave & Mercato
**Vibe:** Casual, great pairings with flatbreads.
**Feature:** Extensive by-the-glass selection.

### 3. Decanted Wines
**Location:** Naples Wine District (N Naples)
**Vibe:** Tasting room + retail shop.
**Feature:** Local wine education events.

---

## Restaurant Cellars

- **Barbatella** – Italian-focused, excellent sommeliers.
- **The French** – Champagne and French selections.
- **Capital Grille** – 350+ bottle list.
- **Fleming's** – 100 wines by the glass.

---

## Wine Events

- **Naples Winter Wine Festival** (January) – Ultra-exclusive charity event.
- **Wine Walks** – Check 5th Ave and Mercato calendars.
- **Local Winery Tours** – Day trips to Florida wineries available.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Happy Hour Hack:** Many upscale restaurants offer wine by the glass at 50% off during happy hour. Fleming's and Barbatella are standouts.
</ExpertTip>
