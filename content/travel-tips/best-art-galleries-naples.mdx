## Naples: An Art Lover's Paradise

With significant collector wealth and a vibrant arts community, Naples punches above its weight culturally.

---

## Major Institutions

### 1. Baker Museum at Artis-Naples
**Location:** Pelican Bay
**The Collection:** Contemporary and modern works. Chihuly glass.
**Tip:** Combine with a Naples Philharmonic performance.

### 2. Naples Art District (NAD)
**Location:** Bayshore Arts District
**The Collection:** Cluster of 40+ galleries in industrial spaces.
**Best Time:** First Saturday Art Walks (6-9 PM, season).

---

## Downtown Galleries

### 3. Sheldon Fine Art
**Location:** 5th Avenue
**Focus:** Impressionism, realist landscapes.

### 4. DeBruyne Fine Art
**Location:** 5th Avenue
**Focus:** Contemporary sculpture and paintings.

### 5. Galerie Biba
**Location:** 3rd Street South
**Focus:** European antiques and fine art.

---

## Emerging & Contemporary

### 6. Naples Botanical Garden (Art Installations)
**What:** Rotating outdoor sculpture exhibitions.
**Best For:** Art meets nature.

### 7. Marco Island Center for the Arts
**What:** Local and regional artists.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Art Season:** January through March is peak art season with major festivals: Naples National Art Festival, Naples Art Fest, and gallery openings galore.
</ExpertTip>
