## Naples: Made for Romance

With stunning sunsets, candlelit dining, and serene beaches, Naples is a top destination for couples.

---

## Romantic Experiences

### Beach & Sunset
- **Sunset at the Pier** – The classic Naples moment.
- **Private Beach Cabana** – Book at The Ritz or LaPlaya.
- **Sunset Sailing** – Sweet Liberty catamaran.

### Dining
- **Baleen** – Tables on the sand.
- **The French** – Jazz brunch.
- **Barbatella Courtyard** – Intimate Italian.

### Spa & Wellness
- **Couples Massage** – The Ritz-Carlton Spa or Naples Grande.
- **Sunrise Yoga** – On the beach (seasonal).

### Activities
- **Wine Tasting** – Naples winery tours.
- **Kayaking at Dawn** – Rookery Bay for just you two.
- **Art Gallery Walk** – 3rd Street South.

---

## Romantic Hotels

- **LaPlaya Beach & Golf Resort** – Beachfront boutique.
- **The Inn on Fifth** – Downtown luxury, walkable.
- **The Escalante** – Secret garden hideaway.

---

## Anniversary/Proposal Tips

- **Request Beach Table** at Baleen or Turtle Club.
- **Private Sunset Cruise** – Can arrange champagne moments.
- **Photography:** Book a surprise photoshoot on the beach.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Best Season for Romance:** November and December. Perfect weather, holiday lights, uncrowded (pre-Valentine's rush).
</ExpertTip>
