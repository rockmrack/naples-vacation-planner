## Meet the Locals (The Furry and Finned Ones)

Naples Bay and the surrounding waters are home to bottlenose dolphins, manatees, sea turtles, and incredible birds.

---

## Dolphin Tours

### 1. Naples Princess Sunset Cruise
**Type:** Large catamaran with bar/snacks.
**Duration:** 2-3 hours.
**Why:** Relaxed, great for all ages.

### 2. Pure Naples (Port O Call Marina)
**Type:** Smaller boats, more intimate.
**Best Tour:** Sunset dolphin cruise.

### 3. Marco Island Dolphin Watching
**Type:** Speedboats and catamarans.
**Why:** High dolphin encounter rate.

---

## Manatee Viewing

### Best Season: November-March (manatees seek warm water).

### 4. Manatee Park (Fort Myers)
**Cost:** Free (small parking fee).
**Why:** Guaranteed manatee sightings in winter.

### 5. Rookery Bay Reserve
**Type:** Kayak or boat tour.
**Why:** Natural habitat, no crowds.

---

## Eco & Wildlife Tours

### 6. Everglades National Park Boat Tours
**Launch:** Everglades City (45 min from Naples).
**See:** Gators, manatees, birds, dolphins.

### 7. Conservancy of Southwest Florida
**Experience:** Electric boat eco-tour.
**Best For:** Families with kids.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Best Time of Day:** Early morning and late afternoon. Dolphins are most active when feeding.
</ExpertTip>
