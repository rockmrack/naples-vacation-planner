## Solo in Paradise

Naples is one of the safest cities in Florida, making it ideal for solo travelers.

---

## Why Naples is Great Solo

1. **Extremely Safe:** Low crime, well-lit streets, friendly locals.
2. **Walkable Downtown:** 5th Ave and 3rd St are perfect for solo strolling.
3. **Bar Seating Culture:** Fine dining restaurants welcome solo diners at the bar.
4. **Nature Access:** Beaches, parks, and trails are solo-friendly.

---

## Best Solo Activities

### Beach & Nature
- Sunrise walk at Naples Pier.
- Solo kayak at Clam Pass.
- Naples Botanical Garden (peaceful self-guided tour).

### Dining
- Bar seating at Osteria Tulia or The French.
- Happy hour at Truluck's (easy to chat with bartenders).
- Coffee and reading at Kunjani.

### Wellness
- Spa day at Woodhouse or The Ritz.
- Yoga classes at Naples Yoga Center.

### Culture
- Baker Museum at Artis-Naples.
- Naples Art District First Saturday gallery walk.

---

## Meeting People

- **Cambier Park Concerts:** Easy mingling, casual vibe.
- **Group Tours:** Kayak tours, dolphin cruises.
- **Hotel Bars:** Ritz-Carlton, Naples Grande lounges.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Safety Tip:** Naples is safe, but use standard precautions—lock your car, don't leave valuables on the beach.
