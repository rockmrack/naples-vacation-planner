## Accessible Naples

Naples is a popular retirement destination, meaning accessibility is excellent throughout the city.

---

## Beach Accessibility

Naples offers amenities to help everyone enjoy the sand.

### Beach Wheelchairs
Available for **free** loan at:
- **Lowdermilk Park** (at concession stand)
- **Naples Pier** (at concession stand)
- **Vanderbilt Beach** (ask rangers)
- **Delnor-Wiggins Pass State Park**

*Tip: Call ahead to reserve if possible.*

### Matting (Mobi-Mats)
Mobi-mats (blue pathways) extend onto the sand at:
- Lowdermilk Park
- Naples Pier
- Clam Pass Park (tram is also wheelchair accessible!)

---

## Top Accessible Attractions

1.  **Naples Pier:** Fully accessible wooden styling.
2.  **Naples Botanical Garden:** 90% of paths are paved; wheelchairs available.
3.  **Naples Zoo:** Paved paths throughout; boat ride is accessible.
4.  **Corkscrew Swamp Sanctuary:** 2.25-mile boardwalk is fully wheelchair friendly.
5.  **5th Avenue South:** Wide, flat sidewalks with curb cuts.
