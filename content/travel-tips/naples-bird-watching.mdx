## A Birder's Paradise

Naples sits on the Atlantic Flyway and borders the Everglades, making it one of the best birding destinations in North America.

---

## Top Birding Spots

### 1. Corkscrew Swamp Sanctuary
**Why:** Old-growth cypress swamp, incredible biodiversity.
**Species:** Wood Storks (nesting colony), Barred Owls, Painted Buntings.
**Best Time:** December-March.

### 2. Rookery Bay Reserve
**Why:** Pristine estuary, minimal crowds.
**Species:** Roseate Spoonbills, Ospreys, Shorebirds.
**Access:** Kayak or visitor center trails.

### 3. Delnor-Wiggins Pass State Park
**Why:** Beach and mangrove mix.
**Species:** Pelicans, Terns, Plovers.

### 4. Tigertail Beach (Marco Island)
**Why:** Lagoon attracts wading birds.
**Species:** Herons, Egrets, Shorebirds.

### 5. Clam Pass
**Why:** Easy access, mangrove boardwalk.
**Species:** Anhingas, Herons, Hawks.

---

## Species Checklist (Common)

- Brown Pelican
- Great Blue Heron
- Snowy Egret
- Roseate Spoonbill
- White Ibis
- Anhinga
- Osprey
- Bald Eagle
- Reddish Egret
- Black Skimmer
