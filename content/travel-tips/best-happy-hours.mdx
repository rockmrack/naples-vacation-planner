## Naples Happy Hour Culture

Happy hour is *sacred* in Naples. Most restaurants offer specials from 3-6 PM, and the bar menus are often half the price of dinner.

---

## Best Value

### 1. Truluck's
**Time:** 4:30-6:30 PM
**Deal:** $9 specialty cocktails, $2 off wine/beer, discounted appetizers.
**Why:** High-end seafood at mid-range prices.

### 2. Ocean Prime
**Time:** 5-7 PM (bar/lounge only)
**Deal:** Half-price sushi, $8 cocktails.
**Why:** Upscale without the upscale bill.

### 3. The French Brasserie
**Time:** 3-6 PM
**Deal:** $7 wines, $8 cocktails, discounted bites.
**Why:** Parisian vibes, Naples prices.

---

## Waterfront Happy Hours

### 4. The Dock at Crayton Cove
**Time:** 4-6 PM
**Deal:** $5 well drinks, $4 domestic beers.
**Why:** Best view-to-price ratio.

### 5. Pelican Larry's (multiple locations)
**Time:** 3-6 PM
**Deal:** $2.50 drafts, $.60 wings.
**Why:** Sports bar energy, local favorite.

---

## 5th Avenue Deals

### 6. Barbatella
**Time:** 3-6 PM
**Deal:** $8 spritzes, $10 Negronis, discounted small plates.

### 7. Naples Flatbread
**Time:** 3-5:30 PM
**Deal:** Half-off select drinks, $5 appetizers.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Locals' Secret:** Many happy hours extend to 7 PM in the off-season (May-October). Ask!
