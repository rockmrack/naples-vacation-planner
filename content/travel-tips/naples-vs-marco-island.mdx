## Naples vs Marco Island

Both are beautiful, but they offer different vibes. Here's how to choose.

---

## Quick Comparison

| Factor | Naples | Marco Island |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| **Vibe** | Upscale town, walkable downtown | Quiet island, condo/resort focus |
| **Beaches** | Longer, busier, more access points | Crescent Beach—one stunning beach |
| **Dining** | 100+ restaurants, fine dining hub | Fewer options, resort-focused |
| **Nightlife** | Moderate (5th Ave bars) | Very quiet |
| **Activities** | Museums, shopping, golf | Shelling, boating, 10,000 Islands |
| **Drive to Everglades** | 45 min | 30 min |

---

## Choose Naples If:
- You want walkable dining and shopping.
- You love variety in restaurants.
- You want easy access to golf and cultural activities.
- You're visiting for the first time.

## Choose Marco Island If:
- You want a quieter, more secluded getaway.
- You prioritize beach quality over quantity.
- You're focused on boating, fishing, and 10,000 Islands.
- You prefer resort-style all-in-one stays.

---

## Why Not Both?

Marco Island is only 30 min from Naples. Many visitors split their time or do a day trip.
