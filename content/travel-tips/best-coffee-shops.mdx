## Caffeinating in Paradise

Naples has moved way beyond chain coffee. The local scene is thriving.

---

## Top Picks for Connoisseurs

### 1. Kunjani Craft Coffee & Gallery
**Location:** North Naples (near Waterside)
**Vibe:** Woman-owned, organic, fair-trade, African decor.
**The Cup:** Cold Brew is legendary.
**Bonus:** Itâ€™s also an art gallery/boutique.

### 2. Narrative Coffee Roasters
**Location:** Mercato & Central Ave
**Vibe:** Minimalist, hipster, serious about beans.
**The Cup:** Pour-over (seasonal single origins).
**Best For:** Working on your laptop.

### 3. Bad Ass Coffee of Hawaii
**Location:** Old Naples (3rd St S)
**Vibe:** Hawaiian relaxed.
**The Cup:** 100% Kona Coffee.
**Best For:** Iced coffee on a hot day.

---

## Cozy & Comfortable

### 4. The Bean
**Location:** Tamiami Trail N
**Vibe:** Old-school coffee house feels. Comfy chairs.
**The Cup:** Flavored lattes.

### 5. Divine Naples Coffee & Wine
**Location:** Downtown
**Vibe:** European style.
**The Cup:** Cappuccino.

  **Working Remotely?** Narrative (Mercato) and Kunjani have the best WiFi and "work from anywhere" atmosphere.
