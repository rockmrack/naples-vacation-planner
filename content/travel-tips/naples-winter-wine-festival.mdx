## America's Most Successful Charity Wine Auction

The **Naples Winter Wine Festival** isn't your typical wine festival—it's a **three-day extravaganza** featuring celebrity chefs, world-renowned vintners, and an auction that has raised over **$275 million** for children's charities in Collier County.

---

## What Is the Naples Winter Wine Festival?

### The Basics

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **When** | Late January/Early February |
| **Where** | The Ritz-Carlton Naples + private venues |
| **Duration** | 3 days (Friday–Sunday) |
| **Ticket Price** | $12,500–$20,000 per couple |
| **Charity Impact** | $275+ million raised since 2001 |
| **Beneficiary** | Naples Children & Education Foundation |

### Why It Matters

The festival supports children in Collier County through:
- Early childhood education
- Out-of-school programs
- Mental health services
- Pediatric healthcare

---

## The Festival Experience

### The Three Days

**Friday: Vintner Dinners**
- Intimate dinners at private homes
- World-class chefs paired with famous winemakers
- 20+ simultaneous dinners across Naples

**Saturday: Grand Tasting**
- Hundreds of wines to sample
- Celebrity chef cooking stations
- Art auction preview

**Sunday: Live Auction**
- Premium lots (trips, wine collections, experiences)
- Record-breaking bids
- Emotional charity moments

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Tickets sell out almost immediately when they go on sale (typically October). Join the mailing list at napleswinefestival.com to be notified. Many attendees return year after year.
</ExpertTip>

---

## How to Attend

### Getting Tickets

**The Challenge:** Tickets are extremely limited and sell out in hours.

**The Process:**
1. Join the NCEF mailing list
2. Be ready when tickets open (usually October)
3. Have $12,500–$20,000 per couple ready
4. Click fast!

**Alternatives:**
- Patron packages (higher price, guaranteed access)
- Vintner connections (if you know a participating winery)
- Volunteer opportunities (limited)

## Practical Information

### If You're Attending

**What to Pack:**
- Formal attire (black tie optional)
- Comfortable shoes (you'll be standing)
- Light jacket (outdoor venues)
- Business cards (networking!)

**Where to Stay:**
- The Ritz-Carlton (festival HQ)
- The Inn on Fifth
- LaPlaya Beach

**Transportation:**
- Festival provides shuttles between venues
- Taxis/Uber for evening events (don't drink and drive!)

### Festival Week Atmosphere

Even if you're not attending the festival:
- Restaurants are busy—make reservations
- Wine bars are buzzing
- Celebrity chef sightings possible
- General energy is elevated
