## The Best Way to End the Day

There's nothing like watching the sun melt into the Gulf from the deck of a boat.

---

## Large Group Cruises

### 1. Naples Princess
**Capacity:** 149 passengers
**Style:** Large catamaran with bar, snacks, live narration.
**Duration:** 2 hours.
**Price:** ~$45-65/person.
**Best For:** First-time visitors, groups, families.

### 2. Pure Florida
**Capacity:** Smaller boats (50-80).
**Style:** Catamarans and sightseeing vessels.
**Unique:** Dolphin sighting guarantee.

---

## Private & Romantic

### 3. Sweet Liberty Catamaran
**Style:** Sailing catamaran.
**Vibe:** More intimate, wind-powered.
**Bonus:** Champagne options.

### 4. Private Charter (Captain's Choice)
**What:** Hire your own captain and boat.
**Price:** $300-600 for 2-6 people.
**Best For:** Anniversaries, proposals, celebrations.

---

## Unique Experiences

### 5. Tiki Bar Cruises
**What:** Floating tiki bar.
**Vibe:** Party atmosphere, BYOB in some cases.

### 6. Paddleboard Sunset Tour
**What:** SUP into the sunset from Clam Pass.
**Best For:** Active couples.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Timing:** Winter sunsets are around 5:30-6:00 PM. Summer sunsets are 8:00+ PM. Check cruise times accordingly.
</ExpertTip>
