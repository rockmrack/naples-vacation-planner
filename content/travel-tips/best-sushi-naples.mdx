## Sushi in Southwest Florida

Yes, you can get excellent sushi in Naples. The proximity to the Gulf means fresh fish, and the upscale dining scene attracts talented chefs.

---

## Top Picks

### 1. Sushi Thai
**Location:** Multiple (5th Ave, Mercato, etc.)
**Vibe:** Upscale, modern, lively.
**Order:** Specialty rolls, Pad Thai if you want variety.
**Tip:** The 5th Ave location is best for ambiance.

### 2. DaRuMa
**Location:** Tamiami Trail
**Vibe:** Interactive hibachi + sushi bar.
**Order:** Hibachi for show, sushi for taste.
**Why:** Fun for groups and families.

### 3. Sushi Deli 2
**Location:** Vanderbilt Beach Road
**Vibe:** Strip mall gem. No-frills.
**Order:** Chef's choice sashimi.
**Why:** Best fish-to-price ratio.

---

## Upscale Omakase

### 4. Masa (at The Zushi)
**Location:** Naples area (check for current location)
**Vibe:** Intimate omakase experience.
**Order:** The omakase (let the chef guide you).

### 5. Ocean Prime (Sushi Bar)
**Vibe:** Steakhouse with stellar sushi.
**Order:** Happy hour sushi (half-price, 5-7 PM).

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Best Value:** Sushi Deli 2 for quality. Sushi Thai for ambiance. Ocean Prime happy hour for luxury on a budget.
