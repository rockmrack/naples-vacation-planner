## Naples After Dark

The nightlife in Naples is intimate, not wild. Think jazz trios, acoustic sets, and island vibes rather than nightclubs.

---

## Jazz & Sophisticated

### 1. The Bay House
**Vibe:** Jazz on weekends, waterfront elegance.
**When:** Friday & Saturday evenings.

### 2. The French Brasserie
**Vibe:** Live jazz during Sunday brunch.
**Order:** Champagne and croissants.

### 3. Blue Martini (Mercato)
**Vibe:** Upscale lounge, DJs and live bands.
**Best For:** Later night energy.

---

## Casual & Fun

### 4. South Street City Oven
**Location:** 5th Avenue
**Vibe:** Dueling pianos, sing-along energy.
**Best For:** Groups looking for fun.

### 5. Pelican Larry's
**Vibe:** Sports bar with acoustic acts.
**When:** Select weeknights.

### 6. Celebration Park
**Vibe:** Outdoor waterfront, local bands.
**When:** Weekends.

---

## Beach & Resort Vibes

### 7. Gumbo Limbo (Ritz-Carlton)
**Vibe:** Tiki bar with acoustic island sets.

### 8. Sunset Beach Bar
**Vibe:** Laid-back beach tunes.

---

## Free Concerts

- **Cambier Park** – Sunday concerts in season (big band, jazz).
- **Mercato** – Thursday night live music (seasonal).
- **5th Avenue Events** – Check Naples calendar for street festivals.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **In Season:** January-April has the most live music events. Summer is quieter.
</ExpertTip>
