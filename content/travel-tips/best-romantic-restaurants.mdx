## Date Night Done Right

Naples is made for romance. These restaurants deliver on ambiance, food, and that special feeling.

---

## Beachfront Romance

### 1. Baleen at LaPlaya
**Why:** Tables on the sand, literally feet from the Gulf.
**Order:** Fresh catch with a glass of champagne.
**Tip:** Request a beach table at sunset.

### 2. The Turtle Club
**Why:** Toes-in-the-sand dining, elegant but relaxed.
**Order:** Stone crabs (in season).

---

## Downtown Elegance

### 3. The French
**Location:** 5th Avenue South
**Why:** Parisian-style brasserie. Jazz brunch on Sundays.
**Order:** Steak Frites, Crème Brûlée to share.

### 4. Barbatella
**Location:** 3rd Street South
**Why:** Intimate courtyard, Italian small plates to share.
**Order:** Wine pairings + burrata.

### 5. Osteria Tulia
**Why:** Rustic Italian, James Beard-nominated.
**Order:** Trust the pasta.

---

## Waterfront Views

### 6. Nosh on Naples Bay
**Why:** Modern elegance overlooking yachts.
**Order:** Tasting menu for the adventurous.

### 7. The Bay House
**Why:** Sunsets over Naples Bay.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Proposal Tip:** Baleen and Turtle Club can arrange private moments. Call ahead and ask for their special occasion packages.
