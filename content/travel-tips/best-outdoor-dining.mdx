## Dine Under the Florida Sky

With 270+ days of sunshine, outdoor dining is the Naples way. Here are the best patios and terraces.

---

## Waterfront Patios

### 1. The Dock at Crayton Cove
**View:** Marina, fishing boats, pelicans.
**Vibe:** Lively, casual, nautical.

### 2. Riverwalk at Tin City
**View:** Gordon River.
**Vibe:** Touristy but fun.

### 3. Celebration Park
**View:** Bayshore waterfront.
**Vibe:** Food truck park with outdoor seating.

---

## Courtyard Dining

### 4. Barbatella's Courtyard
**Location:** 3rd Street South
**Vibe:** European courtyard, string lights, romantic.

### 5. Jane's Cafe
**Location:** 3rd Street South
**Vibe:** Garden patio, perfect for brunch.

---

## 5th Avenue Al Fresco

### 6. Vergina
**Vibe:** Mediterranean, great people-watching.

### 7. Caffé Milano
**Vibe:** Italian sidewalk café energy.

### 8. Barbatella (5th Ave)
**Vibe:** Chic Italian.

---

## Beach & Resort Dining

### 9. Gumbo Limbo (Ritz-Carlton)
**Vibe:** Tiki bar on the beach. Casual luxury.

### 10. Rhode's End (Naples Grande)
**Vibe:** Greek food, sand underfoot.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Season Tip:** October through April is perfect for outdoor dining. May-September can be hot and buggy—opt for shaded patios with fans.
</ExpertTip>
