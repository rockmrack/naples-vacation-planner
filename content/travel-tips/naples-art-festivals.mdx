## Naples: An Art Capital

Naples hosts world-class art festivals, especially from January through March. Plan your trip around these events.

---

## Major Annual Festivals

### Naples National Art Festival
**When:** February (President's Day weekend)
**Where:** Cambier Park & 8th Street
**What:** 250+ juried artists from across the country.
**Vibe:** High-quality, serious art buyers.

### Naples Invitational Art Fest
**When:** January
**Where:** Downtown Naples
**What:** Curated national artists.

### Coconut Point Art Festival
**When:** January
**Where:** Coconut Point Mall, Estero
**What:** Outdoor festival with 200+ artists.

### Art in the Park (Naples Art Association)
**When:** November – April (first Saturday)
**Where:** Cambier Park
**What:** Local and regional artists. Free admission.

---

## Gallery Events

### First Saturday Art Walks
**When:** November – April
**Where:** Naples Art District (Bayshore)
**What:** 40+ galleries open late. Complimentary wine.

### Third Thursdays on 3rd Street
**When:** Monthly, November – April
**Where:** 3rd Street South
**What:** Art, music, pedestrian street party.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Buying Art:** Bring cash for smaller artists. Many offer shipping for larger pieces.
