## Naples: A Seafood Paradise

The Gulf of Mexico delivers the freshest catch to Naples tables daily. Here are the must-visit spots.

---

## Fine Dining

### 1. USS Nemo
**The Vibe:** Intimate, creative, globally-inspired seafood.
**Order:** Miso-Glazed Sea Bass (legendary).
**Tip:** Reservations essential. BYOB on Mondays.

### 2. The Bay House
**The Vibe:** Waterfront elegance on Naples Bay.
**Order:** Fresh catch with Caribbean flair.

### 3. Truluck's
**The Vibe:** Upscale chain, but excellent stone crabs.
**Order:** Stone Crab Claws (in season: Oct-May).

---

## Casual & Iconic

### 4. The Dock at Crayton Cove
**The Vibe:** Bustling marina views.
**Order:** Dock Nachos with seafood toppings.

### 5. Pinchers
**The Vibe:** Family-friendly, casual, fun.
**Order:** Blue Crab Cakes.
**Bonus:** Kids eat free with adult entrée.

### 6. Kelly's Fish House
**The Vibe:** Old Florida charm since 1953.
**Order:** Fried Grouper Sandwich.

---

## Hidden Gems

### 7. Grouper & Chips
**Location:** East Naples (strip mall—trust us).
**Order:** Fish and Chips (British-style).

### 8. Randy's Fishmarket
**The Vibe:** Part market, part restaurant.
**Order:** Whatever's fresh that day.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Stone Crab Season:** October 15 – May 15. Outside these dates, you'll only find frozen claws.
</ExpertTip>
