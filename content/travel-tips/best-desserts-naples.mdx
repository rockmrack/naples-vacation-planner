## Sweet Naples

After a hot day at the beach, nothing beats a cold treat. Here are the best dessert spots.

---

## Ice Cream & Gelato

### 1. Kilwins
**Location:** 5th Avenue South & 3rd Street
**The Pick:** Hand-dipped ice cream, fresh fudge.
**Vibe:** Classic American sweet shop.

### 2. Royal Scoop
**Location:** Bonita Springs
**The Pick:** Homemade ice cream since 1985.
**Try:** Butter Pecan or Key Lime.

### 3. Gelato Go
**Location:** Mercato
**The Pick:** Authentic Italian gelato.
**Vibe:** Light, creamy, European-style.

---

## Bakeries & Pastries

### 4. Norman Love Confections
**Location:** Fort Myers & Naples
**The Pick:** Artisan chocolates (great gifts).

### 5. Floridian Café
**The Pick:** Pies (Key Lime, Chocolate Cream).

---

## Unique Sweets

- **Rita's Italian Ice** – Frozen custard blends.
- **Häagen-Dazs** (Waterside Shops) – Simple classic.
- **The French** – Crème Brûlée and pastry cart.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Key Lime Pie:** Florida's signature dessert. Best versions: Kermit's Key West (visitor center) or The Turtle Club (if dining there).
</ExpertTip>
