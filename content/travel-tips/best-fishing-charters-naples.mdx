## Fishing Paradise

The Naples waters are teeming with tarpon, snook, redfish, grouper, and more.

---

## Types of Fishing

### Inshore (Backwater)
**What:** Calm waters, shallow flats, mangroves.
**Target Fish:** Snook, Redfish, Spotted Sea Trout.
**Best For:** Beginners, families, light tackle enthusiasts.
**Duration:** Half-day (4 hrs).

### Nearshore
**What:** Just off the coast (1-10 miles).
**Target Fish:** Grouper, Snapper, Cobia.
**Best For:** Moderate experience.
**Duration:** Half to full day.

### Offshore (Deep Sea)
**What:** 20+ miles out in the Gulf.
**Target Fish:** Mahi-Mahi, Kingfish, Tuna.
**Best For:** Experienced anglers seeking big game.
**Duration:** Full day (8+ hrs).
