## Spa Paradise

Naples attracts visitors seeking relaxation, and the spa scene delivers world-class treatments.

---

## Resort Spas

### 1. The Spa at The Ritz-Carlton, Naples
**Vibe:** Ultimate luxury.
**Signature:** Coastal-inspired treatments (seaweed wraps, salt scrubs).
**Access:** Guests and day-spa visitors.

### 2. The Spa at Naples Grande
**Vibe:** Tropical elegance.
**Signature:** Mangrove mud body wrap.
**Bonus:** Use of pools and fitness center with treatment.

### 3. LaPlaya Beach Resort Spa
**Vibe:** Boutique beachfront.
**Signature:** Aromatherapy massage with Gulf views.

---

## Day Spas

### 4. Woodhouse Day Spa (Mercato)
**Vibe:** Nationally acclaimed, consistent quality.
**Signature:** The Renewal (full-body experience).

### 5. Bellissimo Day Spa
**Location:** 5th Avenue South
**Vibe:** Intimate, personalized service.

### 6. Shangri-La Springs (Bonita)
**Vibe:** Historic wellness retreat.
**Unique:** Thermal pools, yoga, organic restaurant.

---

## Wellness & Yoga

- **Naples Yoga Center** – Classes for all levels.
- **Pelican Bay Fitness** – Day passes available.
- **Outdoor Yoga at Naples Pier** – Sunrise classes (seasonal).

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Book Ahead:** During peak season (Jan-Apr), resort spas book 2-3 weeks in advance. Plan early.
</ExpertTip>
