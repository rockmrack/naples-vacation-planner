## Ultimate Relaxation in Paradise

Naples is home to some of the world's most luxurious spas, offering everything from organic seaweed wraps to diamond facials. Whether you're staying at a resort or looking for a day escape, here is your guide to bliss.

---

## Top Resort Spas

### 1. The Spa at The Ritz-Carlton, Naples
**Vibe:** Sophisticated luxury with Gulf views
**Signature:** The "Naples Organic Massage"
**Amenities:** Steam rooms, saunas, mineral pool, outdoor relaxation terrace

### 2. SpaTerre at LaPlaya Beach & Golf Resort
**Vibe:** Exotic, Balinese-inspired beachfront sanctuary
**Signature:** Javanese Royal Pedicure
**Amenities:** Private soaking tubs, outdoor showers, meditation courtyard

### 3. The Spa at Naples Grande
**Vibe:** Modern zen with Japanese-style architecture
**Signature:** Salt Float and Massage
**Amenities:** Meditation labyrinth, sundeck, whirlpools

---

## Best Day Spas

If you aren't staying at a resort, these local day spas offer incredible service:

- **The Woodhouse Day Spa:** Known for its volcanic stone massage
- **Purely You Spa:** 100% organic and eco-friendly options
- **Spa at The Inn on Fifth:** Convenient luxury in the heart of downtown

---

<ExpertTip>
**Pro Tip:** Most resort spas allow non-hotel guests to book treatments, but access to resort pools may be restricted. Always ask about "day pass" amenities when booking your massage.
</ExpertTip>
