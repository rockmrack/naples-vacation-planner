## Casual Eats with Character

Not every meal needs a reservation. Naples has a growing scene of food trucks, taquerias, and casual spots.

---

## Celebration Park

**The Spot:** Naples' waterfront food truck park on Bayshore Drive.
**Vibe:** Picnic tables, string lights, live music, full bar.
**Trucks to Try:**
- **Sizzle SWFL** – Korean BBQ
- **Falafel & Shawarma** – Mediterranean
- **Pelican Larry's Express** – Wings
- **Kona Ice** – Shaved ice for kids

*Tip: Go at sunset for the best atmosphere.*

---

## Hidden Gems

### Taco Stands
- **El Tarasco** (Pine Ridge Rd) – Authentic Mexican tacos.
- **Tacos Lupita** – Local favorite, cash only.

### BBQ
- **Smokin' R's BBQ** – Texas-style brisket.

### Burgers
- **Brooks Burgers** – Cult favorite. Try the Build-Your-Own.

---

## Quick Bites Downtown

- **5th Avenue Coffee Co.** – Grab-and-go pastries.
- **Kilwins** – Ice cream & fudge on 5th Ave.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Celebration Park** is the best option if everyone in your group wants something different. Multiple cuisines, one location.
</ExpertTip>
