## Pizza Perfection

From wood-fired Neapolitan to cheesy New York slices, Naples has surprisingly great pizza.

---

## Neapolitan Style

### 1. Pizzeria Verità (coming to area)
**Style:** True Neapolitan (soft, charred, minimalist).
**Note:** Check for latest openings in SWFL.

### 2. Barbatella
**Location:** 3rd Street South
**Style:** Italian-inspired thin crust.
**Order:** Margherita or seasonal specials.

---

## New York Style

### 3. Naples Flatbread & Wine Bar
**Location:** 5th Ave & Mercato
**Style:** Thin, crispy, creative toppings.
**Order:** The "Fig & Pig" or "Bianca."

### 4. Joey D's Italian Grille
**Location:** Coconut Point
**Style:** Classic NY slices.
**Order:** Pepperoni by the slice.

---

## Casual & Family

### 5. Aurelio's Pizza
**Location:** North Naples
**Style:** Chicago-thin (not deep dish).
**Order:** Sausage & Mushroom.

### 6. Crust
**Location:** Mercato
**Style:** Upscale casual, creative.
**Order:** Prosciutto & Arugula.

---

<ExpertTip>
  **Late Night:** Most pizza places close by 9-10 PM. Plan accordingly if you're a midnight pizza craver.
</ExpertTip>
