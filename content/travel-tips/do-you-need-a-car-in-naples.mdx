## The Short Answer

**It depends on your trip style:**

| Trip Type | Need a Car? |
|-----------|-------------|
| Downtown-focused (Old Naples) | Maybe not |
| Beach hopping | Yes |
| Day trips (Everglades, Marco Island) | Yes |
| Staying at a resort | Maybe not |
| Families with kids | Probably yes |

---

## When You DON'T Need a Car

### âœ… Staying in Old Naples + Walking Everywhere

If you:
- Stay in **Old Naples** (near 5th Ave or 3rd Street)
- Plan to walk to beaches, shops, and restaurants
- Skip day trips to Everglades or Marco Island
- Are comfortable with occasional rideshare

Then you can likely skip a car.

**What's walkable from Old Naples:**
- Naples Pier and beach (10-15 min walk)
- 5th Avenue South dining
- 3rd Street South shopping
- Lowdermilk Park beach (15-20 min walk)

### âœ… Staying at a Full-Service Resort

Resorts like **The Ritz-Carlton** or **Naples Grande** offer:
- Beach access on-site
- Multiple restaurants
- Shuttle service to nearby attractions
- Concierge-arranged tours

You can enjoy a full vacation without ever renting a carâ€”if you're okay staying within the resort ecosystem.

## When You NEED a Car

### âŒ Day Trips (Everglades, Marco Island, Sanibel)

These destinations have **no public transit** from Naples:
- Everglades City: 45 min drive
- Marco Island: 20-30 min drive
- Fort Myers / Sanibel: 45-60 min drive

If your trip includes nature adventures or island hopping, you need a car.

### âŒ Beach Variety

Want to visit **Vanderbilt Beach, Delnor-Wiggins Pass, or Tigertail Beach**? You'll need transportation. These beaches are 15-30 minutes from downtown Naples.

### âŒ Families with Kids

Hauling strollers, beach gear, coolers, and tired kids = car is essential.

### âŒ Staying in North Naples (Vanderbilt, Pelican Bay)

These areas are less walkable. You'll need a car to reach downtown dining and attractions.

## Cost Comparison

| Option | Daily Cost (Estimate) |
|--------|----------------------|
| Rental car | $50-100/day |
| Rideshare (3-4 trips) | $40-80/day |
| Walking only | Free |

## Our Recommendation by Trip Style

### Weekend Getaway (Old Naples focus)
**Skip the car.** Walk everywhere, use rideshare for longer distances.

### Beach Vacation (3-5 days)
**Rent a car.** You'll want flexibility to visit different beaches and explore.

### Family Trip
**Definitely rent a car.** Kids + gear + flexibility = car is essential.

### Day Trip Heavy (Everglades, Marco, etc.)
**Rent a car.** No practical alternatives for reaching these destinations.

### Resort Stay
**Maybe skip it.** Check what your resort offers and plan accordingly.
