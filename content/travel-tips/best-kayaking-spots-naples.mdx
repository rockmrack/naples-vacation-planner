## Paddle Through Paradise

Naples' calm bays, mangrove tunnels, and clear waters are perfect for kayaking and stand-up paddleboarding.

---

## Top Spots

### 1. Rookery Bay Reserve
**Why:** Pristine, protected estuary. Manatees, dolphins, birds.
**Rental:** Available at the visitor center.
**Difficulty:** Easy to moderate.

### 2. Clam Pass
**Why:** Scenic paddle through mangrove creek to the Gulf.
**Rental:** Clam Pass Beach Park.
**Difficulty:** Easy.

### 3. Delnor-Wiggins Pass
**Why:** Clear water, sea turtles, calm bay.
**Rental:** Nearby outfitters.
**Difficulty:** Easy.

### 4. 10,000 Islands (Everglades)
**Why:** Wilderness paddling. Multi-day trips possible.
**Access:** Port of the Islands, Chokoloskee.
**Difficulty:** Moderate to advanced.

---

## Rental Companies

- **Naples Kayak Company** – Tours and rentals.
- **Paddle Florida** – Standup paddleboards.
- **Everglades Area Tours** – Guided eco-tours.

---

## Best Times

- **Morning:** Calmest water, best wildlife viewing.
- **Season:** November-April (less rain, fewer bugs).
