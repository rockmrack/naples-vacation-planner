## Tipping in Naples

Naples hospitality standards are high, and tipping is expected for good service. Here are the local norms.

---

## Restaurants & Dining

| Service | Standard Tip | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| **Servers** | 18% - 22% | 20% is standard for good service. |
| **Bartenders** | $1-2 per drink | Or 20% of tab. |
| **Buffet Servers** | 10% |  |
| **Takeout** | 10% |  |

<ExpertTip>
  **Large Parties:** Check your bill! Many restaurants automatically add 18-20% gratuity for parties of 6 or more.
</ExpertTip>

---

## Hotels & Resorts

| Service | Standard Tip | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| **Valet** | $3-5 | Each time you pick up car. |
| **Bellhop** | $1-2 per bag | $5 minimum usually. |
| **Housekeeping** | $3-5 per night | Leave daily (different staff). |
| **Concierge** | $5-20 | Depends on request complexity. |

---

## Tours & Activities

| Service | Standard Tip | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| **Fishing Guides** | 15% - 20% | Standard for charter captains. |
| **Tour Guides** | 15% - 20% | For boat/walking tours. |
| **Golf Caddies** | $20-40+ | Ask pro shop for specific policy. |
| **Spa Therapists** | 20% | Often included (check bill). |

---

## Dress Code Etiquette

"Naples Casual" is the standard:
- **Men:** Collared shirts/polos for dinner. Shorts okay at most places, but pants preferred at fine dining.
- **Women:** Sundresses, nice blouses.
- **Beachwear:** Cover-ups required when leaving the sand. Shirt/shoes required in all shops/restaurants.
