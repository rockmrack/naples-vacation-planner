## The Naples Parking Situation

Parking in Naples is manageable, but beach parking can add up. Here's the breakdown.

---

## Beach Parking

| Beach | Cost | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| **Naples Pier** | $4/hr | Fills early on weekends |
| **Lowdermilk Park** | $4/hr | Larger lot, easier access |
| **Vanderbilt Beach** | $10/day | County lot, better value for long stays |
| **Delnor-Wiggins State Park** | $6/vehicle | Best beach, park fee |
| **Clam Pass** | Free with tram | Park at the lot, take free tram |

---

## Downtown (5th Ave & 3rd St)

### Free Parking
- **Downtown Garages:** 8th Street Garage (free!), 3rd Street Garage (free first 2 hrs).
- **Street Parking:** Many spots are free (2-hr limit). Check signs.

### Paid Parking
- Valet at most restaurants: $8-15.

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Arrive Early:** Beach lots fill by 10 AM on winter weekends.
2. **Use Garage Then Walk:** Park in free 8th St garage and walk to 5th Ave (5 min).
3. **Pay by App:** Many meters accept ParkMobile app.
4. **Resident Sticker:** If you're staying 7+ days, some rentals include a Collier County beach sticker (free parking at county beaches).
