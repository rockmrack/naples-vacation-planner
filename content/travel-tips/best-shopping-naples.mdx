## Shop 'Til You Drop

Naples offers everything from designer boutiques to outlet bargains.

---

## Luxury & Boutique

### 5th Avenue South
**Vibe:** The "Rodeo Drive" of Naples.
**Shops:** Gattle's, Marissa Collections, Panama-to-Africa.
**Best For:** High-end fashion, jewelry, art.

### 3rd Street South
**Vibe:** European village charm.
**Shops:** Tommy Bahama, Chico's, local boutiques.
**Best For:** Resort wear, gifts, browsing.

### Waterside Shops
**Location:** Pelican Bay
**Vibe:** Open-air luxury mall.
**Shops:** Saks Fifth Avenue, Louis Vuitton, Hermès.
**Best For:** Serious luxury shopping.

---

## Upscale Casual

### Mercato
**Location:** North Naples
**Vibe:** Modern outdoor shopping + dining complex.
**Shops:** Nordstrom Rack, Vineyard Vines, Whole Foods.
**Best For:** Mix of shopping and dinner.

### The Village on Venetian Bay
**Location:** Park Shore
**Vibe:** Waterfront shops and restaurants.
**Shops:** Galleries, jewelry, resort wear.
**Best For:** Window shopping with a view.

---

## Outlet & Value

### Miromar Outlets
**Location:** Estero (20 min north)
**Stores:** 140+ outlets (Nike, Coach, Kate Spade).
**Best For:** Serious bargain hunters.
