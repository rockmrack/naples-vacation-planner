## Dining Attire Guide

Naples has a dress code culture. Here's what to pack:

### Casual Beach Restaurants
- Shorts, sundresses, nice sandals
- Cover-up over swimsuit is fine

### 5th Avenue / 3rd Street Dining
- Smart casual minimum
- Collared shirts for men
- Sundresses or nice pants for women
- Closed-toe shoes often required

### Fine Dining (Ritz, LaPlaya, etc.)
- Resort elegant
- No shorts, no flip-flops
- Jackets optional but appreciated
